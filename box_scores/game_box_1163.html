Albies Hits 2 home runs in Braves Win
Ozzie Albies was the difference-maker for the Atlanta Braves in what turned into a shootout with the Miami Marlins. Albies went 3-for-4 in the 10-9 Braves win. Mark Melancon, now 3-1, was credited with the win. He allowed 1 run on 2 hits, while striking out 1 and walking 0. Will Smith earned save number 5 this season.

Albies had a game-changing home run in the bottom of the sixth. On a 1-1 sinker from Dan Jennings, he banged a grand slam home run to left, giving the Braves a 10-8 lead. It was the 5th home run of the year for Albies.

"I like the grit of this ballclub," Albies said.

The Marlins drop to 11-13 and continue their mediocre start to the 2020 season.
