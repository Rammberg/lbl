Reds Score Late, Top Marlins, 11-9
Cincinnati third baseman Mike Moustakas made his presence felt in the 11-9 Reds triumph over the Miami Marlins. Moustakas was 2 for 5 with a home run. He drove in 4 runs and scored once. Raisel Iglesias closed it out for his 5th save. The Marlins fall to 11-17.

Moustakas had a game-changing home run in the bottom of the eighth. On a first pitch changeup from Alex Vesia, he banged a grand slam home run to left, giving the Reds a 11-9 lead. It was the 9th home run of the year for Moustakas.

"I'm proud to wear a Cincinnati jersey," Moustakas told the Cincinnati Bulletin. "We played a good game and came out with the win. What more can you ask for?"
