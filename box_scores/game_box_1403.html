The Toronto Blue Jays and Tampa Bay Rays delighted fans at Tropicana Field.

Nick Anderson recorded the save for Tampa Bay, his 8th of the season.

Tampa Bay took a 3-0 lead on Kevin Kiermaier's 12th double of the season. The 2-run double, which Kiermaier hit in the bottom of the eighth, left Toronto pitcher Tim Mayza shaking his head.

"Can't say enough about our pitching," said Kiermaier.

With the win, Tampa Bay raised its record to 30-23.
