He allowed no runs on 4 hits, with no walks and 8 strikeouts in 5 innings of work. The triumph puts Atlanta at 46-34 for the season.

Ozuna had a key at-bat for Atlanta early in the game. With runners on 1st and 3rd, he hit a run-scoring double. It was his 15th double of the season and gave Atlanta an early 1-0 lead.

"I still think this team has room for improvement," Ozuna told the Atlanta Chronicle.
