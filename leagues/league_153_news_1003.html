Baltimore Orioles Ship Givens to Chicago
Saturday, July 11th, 2020
The Baltimore Orioles have finalized a deal that will send 30-year-old RHP Mychal Givens and $1,940,000 in cash to Chicago for 23-year-old minor league 2B Delvin Zinn, 19-year-old minor league CF Samuel Duarte and 19-year-old minor league RHP Ruben Castillo. Minor details still need to be worked out before the trade becomes official, but the players involved are expected to be with their new clubs soon.


This year Givens has 10 saves. In 29 relief appearances he has tossed 36 innings, fanned 37 and given up 19 walks with a 1-1 record. The Chicago reliever has recorded a 3.00 ERA and allowed the opposition to hit .237 when he is on the mound.
