Erie Third Baseman Nets EL Award
Monday, June 29th, 2020
Isaac Paredes has the tools to stick in this league for many years. The 21-year-old third baseman of the Erie SeaWolves had a hot bat in the Eastern League over the past seven days and won the Player of the Week honors.

Paredes batted .519 with 14 hits in 27 at-bats, 3 home runs and 9 RBIs.

In this current campaign Paredes is hitting at a .320 clip with 7 home runs and 48 RBIs.
