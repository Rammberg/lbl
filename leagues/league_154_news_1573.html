Injury Sidelines Albers for Season
Sunday, August 16th, 2020
The Lehigh Valley IronPigs have made it official: Matt Albers is headed to the injured list. The 37-year-old reliever has been out since 08/15/2020 with a partially torn ulnar collateral ligament. He is expected to miss 10-11 months.

During this season he has made 5 relief appearances, earning a 2-0 record with no saves. In 8.1 innings Albers has struck out 10 with a 1.08 ERA.
