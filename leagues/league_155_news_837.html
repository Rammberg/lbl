American Conference Defeats Pacific Conference 1-0 in Tight All-Star Game
Tuesday, June 30th, 2020
During the star-studded affair, the 2020 American Conference All-Stars defeated the Pacific Conference All-Stars by a score of 1-0.

The fans in attendance at Nelson W. Wolff Municipal Stadium certainly enjoyed the show, seeing the best that the game had to offer.

Chief among the stars was Andrew Knizner, who led the American Conference to victory.

"I will admit I was nervous out there. To share a field with such incredible players is always a humbling experience. But after the first couple pitches, you just have to ignore the talent out there and focus on your game," Knizner commented as he accepted his award after the game.

Knizner is batting .295 lifetime with 143 hits, 22 doubles, no triples, 19 home runs, 80 RBIs to date. He has also scored 67 runs.
