Soto Shows Off, Hits for Cycle
Tuesday, August 25th, 2020
Washington Nationals left fielder Juan Soto had one of the best days of his career today when he hit for the cycle and paved the way for the Nationals 11-7 win over the New York Mets at Nationals Park.

"Everything was just clicking today," he said.

Soto shelled the Mets for 4 hits in 5 at-bats, collected 4 RBI and scored 2 runs.

Juan Soto hit a solo-shot off Jacob deGrom in the 1st, hit an RBI triple in the 3rd, hit an RBI double in the 4th, hit an RBI single in the 6th and struck out in the 8th.

So far this year Soto is batting .284 with 16 home runs, 84 RBIs and 108 runs scored.
