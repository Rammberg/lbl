Seattle Mariners Ship Tuivailala to Milwaukee
Sunday, July 19th, 2020
Both sides appear content with the trade finalized today that will send 27-year-old RHP Sam Tuivailala to the Milwaukee Brewers, while the Seattle Mariners will receive 24-year-old minor league RHP Drew Rasmussen.

"This is a move that filled a few gaps in our roster," said Jerry Dipoto, Mariners general manager.

For his part, the Brewers GM said the trade was made "in the best interest of our organization's future." BNN analysts reported that on paper the trade seemed to be a plus for both ball clubs. The players involved are expected to join their new teams for the next scheduled game.


Tuivailala has taken the mound for 38 relief appearances this season, nailing down 1 save while notching a 2-1 record with a 3.79 ERA. The Milwaukee reliever has limited opposing batters to a .201 batting average.

This season he has made 20 relief appearances, collecting no saves. Rasmussen has worked 19.2 innings, yielding 27 hits and giving out 12 free passes while striking out 24. He has a 1-0 record with a 9.61 ERA.
