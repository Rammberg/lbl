Lehigh Valley Narrowly Wins North Division
Tuesday, September 8th, 2020
The outcome of the North Division was in doubt for everyone around International League. Everyone, that is, except the Lehigh Valley IronPigs.

"We knew we would win it," said Lehigh Valley reliever Francisco Liriano, after his team edged the competition to win the division by one game. "It might have been an extremely close race, but I don't think our players ever lost faith that we would get the job done."

The club got the job done on the strength of a 78-62 record, which earned Lehigh Valley its 1st division title. The team will be seeking the 1st Round 2 in franchise history.

"We're also confident about our chances in the playoffs," Liriano added. "Don't count us out."
