Yankees Pilot Torres Picked as APP's Best
Saturday, September 19th, 2020
A baseball manager's success usually depends on the talent of his club, but in the case of Joe Torres, he seems to have taken the Pulaski Yankees to a higher level and gotten his just due. He was named this season's Manager of the Year in the Appalachian League for leading the team to a very rewarding season.

"Everyone on the team put in a lot of hard work this season," he told reporters. "It's always nice when it's acknowledged in some way."

Torres led Pulaski to a first place finish in the East. The Yankees ended up with a 43-25 record.
