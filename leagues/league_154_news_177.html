Players Suspended in Red Sox-Knights Donnybrook
Sunday, May 3rd, 2020
Pedro Alvarez of the Pawtucket Red Sox and Jimmy Lambert of the Charlotte Knights will be on the sidelines for a few days. They have been suspended for 4 and 8 games respectively by the league office for causing the bench-emptying free-for-all at the recent Red Sox-Knights game at McCoy Stadium.

Neither player had any comment about the suspensions. Both clubs told BNN Alvarez and Lambert will begin serving them soon.
