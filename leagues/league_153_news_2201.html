More Injury Problems For Minnesota's Luis Arraez
Wednesday, September 23rd, 2020
Things have gotten worse for Luis Arraez of the Minnesota Twins. The 23-year-old second baseman will not be back as soon as originally expected. Arraez is undergoing treatment for a bruised thigh, but his doctors have expanded his recovery time to at least 6 days. An unidentified team official told BNN, "Luis tried to rush his rehab and suffered a setback."

Arraez is swinging at a .332 pace on the year with 12 home runs, 74 RBIs and 86 runs scored. He has played in 139 games.
