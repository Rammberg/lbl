Tucker's Hit Streak Soars to 35 Games
Wednesday, July 8th, 2020
Kyle Tucker, left fielder for the Houston Astros, ran his consecutive-game hitting streak to 35 games with 1 hit in his club's 9-6 victory today over the Philadelphia Phillies at Minute Maid Park today.

"Not the outcome we wanted," Philadelphia manager Joe Girardi said. "There's never a good time to lose, and it's never fun to be on the wrong end of somebody else's accomplishment."
Kyle Tucker struck out in the 1st, flied out in the 3rd, flied out in the 5th, walked in the 7th and hit an RBI single in the 8th.

So far this season Tucker is batting .378 with 27 home runs.
