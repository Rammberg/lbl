Kauffmann Takes NWL #1 Pitcher Trophy
Tuesday, September 1st, 2020
When Karl Kauffmann takes the mound, a Boise victory is usually the result. That was the case in August.

That's why he was voted the Northwest League Pitcher of the Month.

Kauffmann throttled league hitters the whole month with an impressive 3-0 mark and 2.67 ERA in 6 starts. The Hawks star hurler also rung up 18 strikeouts in 30.1 innings.

Thus far this season, Kauffmann has worked 55.2 innings with 44 strikeouts en route to a 5-2 record and a 1.94 ERA.
