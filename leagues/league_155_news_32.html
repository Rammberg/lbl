Time to Go: Gosewisch Retires in San Antonio
Monday, April 20th, 2020
Tuffy Gosewisch said years of hard work have taken their toll on him physically, and he's decided it's now time to retire.

"I don't know any way other than full throttle, I've always been that way," he said. "Going so hard for so many years might have cost me some time on my career, but I don't regret a thing."

The San Antonio Missions catcher is leaving the game behind at age 36.

Lifetime Gosewisch is batting .190 and has hit 5 home runs.
