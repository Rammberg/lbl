Gregorius Clobbers 3 HR Against Giants
Sunday, August 9th, 2020
Today was a special one for Didi Gregorius of the Philadelphia Phillies as he slammed three home runs in the 12-5 win over the San Francisco Giants.

"Lately, I've been uppercutting the ball too much," he said to sportswriters after the game. "I would hit it hard, but they just went sky high and stayed in the ballpark. No big long outs for me in this game. I leveled out my swing and connected on the sweet spot. I knew they were gone the minute I hit 'em."

The Philadelphia shortstop collected 3 hits in 3 at-bats, drove in 5, and scored 4 runs in the contest at Citizens Bank Park.

Didi Gregorius hit a solo-shot off Andrew Triggs in the 1st, hit a two-run home run off Andrew Triggs in the 3rd, walked in the 4th, hit a two-run home run off Trevor Oaks in the 5th and walked in the 8th.

In his 2020 season totals, Gregorius has 28 home runs, .322 BA, .362 OBP, 76 RBIs and 70 runs scored.
