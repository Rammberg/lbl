IL Premier Pitcher is Durham's O'Brien
Saturday, August 1st, 2020
Riley O'Brien took a much-deserved turn in the spotlight earlier today as he accepted the International League Pitcher of the Month award for July.

The 25-year-old Bulls starter is a relative newcomer to the league, but nonetheless racked up an impressive 4-0 won-lost record in 5 starts. This month he fanned 43 batters in 35.2 innings and compiled a 1.01 ERA.

So far this year O'Brien has 11 wins and 3 losses and a 2.43 ERA.
