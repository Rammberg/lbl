Rays Hurler Named August's Top Starter
Tuesday, September 1st, 2020
Garrett Hill is the latest Gulf Coast Rookie League Pitcher of the Month trophy winner. The Tampa Bay (GCL) starter sparkled on the diamond time and again last month and finished August with an outstanding 4-0 record.

In 6 starts he tossed 34.2 innings, posted 17 strikeouts, and sported a 1.82 ERA.

This year Hill has made 12 starts, thrown 69 innings, fanned 35 batters and walked 29. His won-lost record is 6-1 with a 2.74 ERA.
