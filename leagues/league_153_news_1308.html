AL's Top Pitcher in July Is Roark
Saturday, August 1st, 2020
Tanner Roark's been around the league long enough to appreciate the honor he received today. After going 4-0 in July, the 33-year-old Toronto hurler has been named American League Pitcher of the Month.

He fanned 19 in 24 innings, logged an ERA of 3.00 and allowed opponents to hit .266 against him.

Up to now Roark has compiled an ERA of 4.42 with a 7-4 mark over 19 starts. He has 83 strikeouts in 95.2 innings and has held opponents to a .277 batting average.
