International League Selects July's Best Batter
Saturday, August 1st, 2020
Seasoned by several years of professional experience, 33-year-old Yonder Alonso put together a solid offensive month for Gwinnett and was crowned July's top batter in the International League.

He batted .340 with 9 home runs, 24 RBIs and 17 runs scored.

Alonso on the season is batting .314 with 28 home runs. His totals include 78 RBIs and 64 runs scored. He has played in 95 games.
