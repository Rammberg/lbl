Stowers, Fishman Receive 3-Game Suspensions
Sunday, May 17th, 2020
The two main figures in yesterday's brawl at George M. Steinbrenner Field have been suspended for 3 games each. That action was taken today by the league commissioner's office and will take effect immediately. The suspended players are Josh Stowers of the Tampa Tarpons and Jake Fishman of the Dunedin Blue Jays.

This season Stowers has delivered 24 hits in 103 at-bats for a .233 average with 1 home run. He is getting on base at a .301 clip and his totals also include 8 RBIs and 9 runs scored. This year Stowers has played in 27 games.

Fishman has taken the mound for 8 relief appearances this season, nailing down 2 saves while notching a 0-1 record with a 9.00 ERA. The Dunedin reliever has limited opposing batters to a .333 batting average.
