Stubbs MID's Best of the Week
Monday, June 29th, 2020
All week long nobody could get C.J. Stubbs out in the Midwest League and that is the prime reason he was voted the Player of the Week.

The Quad Cities catcher took home the trophy by blasting 6 hits in 8 at-bats for a .750 batting average, driving in 2 runs and scoring 3 times.

Stubbs has a .229 average and a .298 on-base percentage for the year. He has picked up 33 hits, 3 home runs and driven in 12 runs.
