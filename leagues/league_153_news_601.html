Injury Brings Season to End for Hatcher
Sunday, June 14th, 2020
Chris Hatcher, the St. Louis reliever, was less than thrilled to learn that his recent injury would keep him out of the rest of the season and any postseason action.

"The playoffs?" he said. "Are you kidding me? The playoffs?"

Hatcher has a torn rotator cuff, an injury that has hampered him since 06/13/2020.

For the year, Hatcher has collected 1 save in 17 relief appearances. He has also rung up 15 strikeouts and 9 walks in 15 innings while registering a 5.40 ERA and a 0-1 won-lost mark.
