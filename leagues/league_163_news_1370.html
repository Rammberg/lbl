Week's Top Performer Honored by SAL
Monday, August 3rd, 2020
Pedro Castellanos of the Greenville Drive was locked in all week as he bullied the pitchers he faced. Castellanos put up some great numbers and hauled in the South Atlantic League Player of the Week trophy.

He hit .423 with no home runs and 6 RBIs. The 22-year-old right fielder also scored 3 runs and for the week went 11-for-26.

So far this year in 93 games Castellanos is hitting .291 with 9 home runs, 58 RBIs and 60 runs scored.
