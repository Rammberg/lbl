Comeaux on a Hot Streak, NWL Player of the Week
Monday, July 20th, 2020
His hard work and hustle paid off this week. The hard-working and hard-playing Braden Comeaux was rewarded with the Northwest League Player of the Week honors.

The scrappy Spokane Indians third baseman hit .435 while collecting 10 hits in 23 at-bats, 1 home run and 3 RBIs.

This year Comeaux has collected a .322 batting average to date with 37 hits in 115 at-bats, 6 RBIs, 1 home run and scored 21 times. He has played in 30 games.
