Week's Best Player Honored by NWL
Monday, June 22nd, 2020
So far in his young career, Daniel Montano has kept a low profile, but after his performance at the plate last week, he attracted the notice of the rest of the Northwest League in a big way as he was named the Player of the Week today for his efforts.

The 21-year-old Boise Hawks center fielder hit at a torrid .421 clip as he collected 8 hits in 19 at-bats, 2 home runs, 8 RBIs and 5 runs scored.
5 Hits for Hawks Perez
Sunday, June 21st, 2020
The Boise Hawks upended the Tri-City Dust Devils at Gesa Stadium today, thanks in large part to first baseman Carlos Perez, who had a smashing day with the bat, racking up 5 hits in 6 at-bats. His big effort helped propel the Hawks to a 13-1 victory. Carlos Perez flied out in the 1st, hit an RBI single in the 2nd, hit a two-run double in the 4th, hit an RBI single in the 6th, singled in the 7th and singled in the 9th.
