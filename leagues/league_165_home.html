NWL Player of the Week Named
Monday, July 13th, 2020
All week long Reese Berberet was the talk of the Northwest League... his name and face were everywhere. So it was no surprise to anyone when he was chosen the Northwest League's Player of the Week for his exceptional performance.

Berberet put up a solid set of stats, compiling a .435 average (10-for-23) and contributing 2 home runs and 9 RBIs to claim the honor.

Thus far this year Berberet is hitting .319 with 3 home runs, 14 RBIs and 11 runs scored.
