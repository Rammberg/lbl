Santos Hears from Medical Staff
Saturday, September 12th, 2020
The Hillsboro Hops have informed Rael Santos that he is likely heading to the injured list. The 19-year-old reliever has been sidelined since 09/07/2020 with a torn labrum. Recent tests have confirmed the extent of the injury. It will be at least 11 months before Santos returns to the field.
