Tucker Boosts Hit Streak to 20 Games
Sunday, June 21st, 2020
The Houston Astros stopped the Oakland Athletics today 11-7 and Kyle Tucker collected a base hit yet again. The Houston left fielder finished with 1 hit to run his consecutive-game hitting streak to 20.

"I want to get a hit every time up," a visibly excited Tucker told reporters after the contest. "I want to get a hit every game I play. Right now, I feel like I could take this streak to 100 games."
Kyle Tucker flied out in the 1st, grounded out in the 3rd, flied out in the 4th, hit a two-run home run off Brian Schlitter in the 6th, grounded out in the 7th and flied out in the 9th.

Tucker is batting .370 with 24 home runs and 65 RBIs this season.
