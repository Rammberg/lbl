Muckdogs Capture Pinckney Division
Saturday, September 5th, 2020
Batavia Muckdogs fans have reason to celebrate today, as their franchise is the class of the Pinckney Division.

With their record standing at 43-30, the club officially wrapped up the division title today, for the 1st time. Manager Jorge Hernandez said everyone on their roster is looking forward to the postseason and trying to capture the club's 1st championship.

"That's what you dream about your whole life," Hernandez said.

Reliever Emerson Hancock added that it's a great feeling to win the division, but said ultimately, a Round 2 victory is what will mark success for the club.
