NWL Star of the Week Announced
Monday, August 17th, 2020
Braden Comeaux is respected by most Spokane Indians fans for his no-frills style of play. The 23-year-old third baseman went about his business with vigor last week to grab the Northwest League Player of the Week honors.

He compiled a .429 average (12-for-28) with no home runs, 1 RBIs and 7 runs scored.

So far this season Comeaux has 1 home run and a .322 batting average.
