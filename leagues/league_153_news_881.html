Tucker Has 31-Game Hitting Streak
Thursday, July 2nd, 2020
Kyle Tucker, left fielder for the Houston Astros, ran his consecutive-game hitting streak to 31 games with 1 hit in his club's 9-6 victory today over the Cleveland Indians at Progressive Field today.

"Not the outcome we wanted," Cleveland manager Terry Francona said. "There's never a good time to lose, and it's never fun to be on the wrong end of somebody else's accomplishment."
Kyle Tucker struck out in the 1st, grounded out in the 3rd, singled in the 5th and grounded out in the 7th.

This year Tucker has collected a .385 batting average to date with 119 hits in 309 at-bats, 80 RBIs, 27 home runs and scored 92 times. He has played in 71 games.
