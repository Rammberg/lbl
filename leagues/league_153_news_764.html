25-Game Hitting Streak for Tucker
Friday, June 26th, 2020
Kyle Tucker has been on a tear for several weeks, and his hitting streak just keeps getting longer. The 23-year-old Houston Astros left fielder extended his streak today to 25 games, going 4-for-5 as Houston got the best of the Texas Rangers at Minute Maid Park, 29-5.

"Kyle has been playing real well lately and we're proud of him," Astros manager Dusty Baker said.

This season Tucker is batting .379 with 25 home runs. His totals include 73 RBIs and 88 runs scored.
