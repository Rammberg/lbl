Barrage of Hits Gets Sano AL Award
Monday, July 13th, 2020
At the age of 27, Miguel Sano might have some potential he hasn't realized yet -- if his success at the plate last week is any indication. The Minnesota first baseman put up some exceptional numbers the past seven days with a .344 batting average. For his efforts Sano collected the American League Player of the Week Award.

He racked up 11 hits in 32 at-bats, 7 home runs and 16 RBIs last week in 8 games.

Sano is presently batting .231 with 21 home runs, 61 runs scored and 70 RBIs. His OBP is .339.
