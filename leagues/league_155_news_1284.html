Mundell on Fire, 6-for-7 Against Las Vegas
Tuesday, July 28th, 2020
No matter what the Las Vegas Aviators threw at him today, Brian Mundell hit it. He easily handled Aviators pitching, going 6 for 7 and helping the Isotopes to a 23-4 triumph at Las Vegas Ballpark.

"The way I was swinging today, I think I could have closed my eyes and hit the ball," he told BNN reporters in the postgame interview.

Told of Mundell's quip, Aviators skipper Fran Riordan said he "would take him up on that next time."

Brian Mundell grounded out in the 1st, hit a three-run home run off Paul Blackburn in the 3rd, singled in the 4th, hit a two-run double in the 5th, hit an RBI triple in the 7th, hit an RBI single in the 8th and singled in the 9th.

In his stats this season, Mundell has posted a batting average of .333 with 75 hits in 225 at-bats, 6 home runs and 27 RBIs. He has also scored 36 runs, walked 20 times and compiled a .389 OBP.
