Jackson Smacks 5 Hits for 66ers
Tuesday, May 26th, 2020
Jeremiah Jackson really sizzled at the plate in today's 15-3 win over Quakes. The Inland Empire second baseman blistered Rancho Cucamonga for 5 hits in 5 at-bats.

"Jackson was too tough to get out today," the Quakes skipper said in the postgame interview. "No matter what we threw him, he hit it. And he hit some good pitches, too."

Jeremiah Jackson hit a solo-shot off Robinson Ortiz in the 2nd, singled in the 3rd, singled in the 4th, hit an RBI single in the 5th, walked in the 7th and singled in the 9th.

So far this season Jackson has 4 home runs and a .253 batting average.
