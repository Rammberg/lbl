Bieber Spectacular in 1-0 Win
Wednesday, July 22nd, 2020
Cleveland Indians hurler Shane Bieber was stingy with runs in his latest outing against the Detroit Tigers, shutting them out 1-0 on 3 hits.

"Shane was very aggressive tonight," Cleveland manager Terry Francona said. "He threw everything he had with a lot of confidence."

Through 9 innings, Bieber tallied 9 strikeouts and allowed no walks.

This season Bieber has compiled a 8-8 mark with a 3.16 ERA.
