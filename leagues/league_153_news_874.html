Tucker Boosts Hit Streak to 30 Games
Wednesday, July 1st, 2020
The Houston Astros stopped the Cleveland Indians today 9-8 and Kyle Tucker collected a base hit yet again. The Houston left fielder finished with 2 hits to run his consecutive-game hitting streak to 30.

"I want to get a hit every time up," a visibly excited Tucker told reporters after the contest. "I want to get a hit every game I play. Right now, I feel like I could take this streak to 100 games."
Kyle Tucker flied out in the 1st, struck out in the 3rd, hit a sac fly in the 5th, flied out in the 7th, singled in the 9th and hit an RBI single in the 10th.

This season Tucker is swinging the bat at a .387 rate with 118 hits in 305 at-bats. To date he has hit 27 home runs, scored 92 runs and driven in 80.
