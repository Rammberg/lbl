Moncada Smashes 3 Homers for Chicago
Friday, August 28th, 2020
Hitting three home runs in a game is usually something to celebrate, but there was no celebration to be found at Guaranteed Rate Field after the hitting barrage from Yoan Moncada. The Chicago White Sox dropped the contest 12-9 to the Houston Astros, leaving Moncada with only a historical footnote as consolation.

Yoan is blessed with a natural home run swing so he gets the ball up in the air real quickly," Chicago manager Rick Renteria said after the loss. "It's just a shame we couldn't win the game, too. I'm sure it spoils the day a little for him."

Along with putting three in the stands, Moncada went 4-for-5 with 5 RBIs and 3 runs scored.

"It's rare that you actually try to hit a home run," he told reporters after the game. "Mostly you'll fail if you really try to hit one, but today I tried on the last one."

Yoan Moncada doubled in the 1st, struck out in the 3rd, hit a solo-shot off Brad Peacock in the 6th, hit a two-run home run off Forrest Whitley in the 7th and hit a two-run home run off Austin Pruitt in the 8th.

On the year Moncada has hit .284 with 30 home runs, 92 RBIs and scored 93 runs.
