Reynolds Reaches 300 Mark
Saturday, August 29th, 2020
Kansas City's outstanding first baseman Mark Reynolds joined the 300 home run club today at Oracle Park with a booming blast in the Royals win over the Giants. They topped San Francisco 7-1.

On his milestone day Reynolds had 1 hit in 4 at-bats with 1 run batted in and 1 run scored.

Tongue-in-cheek he told the press, "Don't tell me when I get close to the next milestone. Just tell me after I reach it. It will be easier to hit that way."

In his 1692-game career Reynolds is batting .236 with 254 doubles, 14 triples, 300 home runs and 876 RBIs. He has stolen 64 bases and scored 796 times.
