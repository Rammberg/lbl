AL's #1 Batter Announced
Wednesday, July 1st, 2020
Even though he is a newcomer to Major League Baseball, Kyle Tucker put up some good numbers in June and earned the AL Batter of the Month award.

He was one of Houston's offensive leaders with a .465 average in 28 games. He also racked up 13 home runs, 37 RBIs, and 50 runs scored.

This year Tucker is hitting .387 with 27 home runs and 78 RBIs to his credit. He has gotten on base at a .446 clip and scored 91 runs.
