Hits Galore: Lopez Drills 6 Hits
Monday, June 22nd, 2020
It's not something you read about in the sports pages every day -- a 6-for-7 day. For many, it might come around only once in a career -- and for Nicky Lopez, it came around today.

The 25-year-old second baseman scattered hits all over Colorado Field to pace the Kansas City Royals to a 22-7 victory over the Colorado Rockies.

Colorado skipper Bud Black told reporters in the press room that "Lopez deserves a hat-tip."

"He hit some good pitches against us," he said. "In fact, according to our scouting reports, Lopez hit several that he is not supposed to be able to hit."

Nicky Lopez singled in the 1st, hit an RBI triple in the 3rd, singled in the 4th, hit an RBI single in the 5th, walked in a run in the 6th, grounded out in the 7th, hit a two-run single in the 8th and hit an RBI single in the 9th.

Lopez has collected 92 hits for the Royals this year, 4 of them home runs. He has also netted 40 RBIs and scored 47 runs while batting .336.
