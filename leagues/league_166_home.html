APP's Top Player Named
Monday, August 31st, 2020
It was a week to remember for Rankin Woley. After wrapping up a torrid week at the plate, the Danville Braves second baseman was named the Appalachian League Player of the Week.

Woley collected 14 hits in 33 at-bats for a .424 average. He also accounted for 5 RBIs, 3 home runs and scored 8 runs.

Looking at his stats this year, Woley has compiled a .297 batting average, while hitting 5 home runs and driving in 21 runs. He has played in 41 games and scored 22 runs.
