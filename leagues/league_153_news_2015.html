Gallo Smashes 3 Homers for Texas
Monday, September 7th, 2020
Joey Gallo brutalized the Seattle Mariners pitching staff, launching three home runs in today's 15-1 victory at Globe Life Field. The Texas Rangers right fielder was red-hot, the ball just exploding off his bat all day.

"I don't try to hit homers," he said later. "I try to go out there and put a good swing on the ball, and sometimes it is going to go out of the park. I didn't try to hit the first one or the second one or the third one."

For the game, he went 4-for-5 with 7 RBIs and 3 runs scored.

"Too much Joey Gallo today," Mariners manager Scott Servais said. "That's my assessment of the game. That's what our problems boiled down to."

Joey Gallo struck out in the 1st, singled in the 3rd, hit a two-run home run off Zack Godley in the 5th, hit a three-run home run off Drew Rasmussen in the 6th and hit a two-run home run off Brandon Brennan in the 8th.

Presently Gallo is batting .232 and has collected 34 home runs, 100 RBIs, .390 OBP and scored 91 runs.
