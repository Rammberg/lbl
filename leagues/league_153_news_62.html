Moncada Rips Kansas City for 5 Hits
Tuesday, April 21st, 2020
A jubilant Yoan Moncada joined the postgame press conference at Guaranteed Rate Field today to offer some unusual words of encouragement -- for the Kansas City Royals pitching staff he had just vanquished.

"It may not have looked like it," said the Chicago White Sox third baseman, "but I saw some awful good pitches today. If luck hadn't been on my side it might have turned out a lot different."

That would not have been the conclusion of anyone who saw his dominant 5-for-5 hitting performance, which led the White Sox to a 22-17 win.

"He made it look easy," said Mike Matheny, manager of the Royals. "We never figured out what to do with him. Obviously."

Yoan Moncada hit an RBI single in the 1st, singled in the 2nd, singled in the 4th, hit a two-run home run off Glenn Sparkman in the 5th, hit a solo-shot off Ian Kennedy in the 6th and walked in the 8th.

This season Moncada is swinging the bat at a .286 pace. He has hit 3 home runs and scored 8 times, while driving in 6. His on-base percentage is .324 and he has played in 8 games.
