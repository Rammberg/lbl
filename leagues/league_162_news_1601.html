Injury Sidelines Goddard for Season
Monday, August 17th, 2020
Jackson Goddard had hoped for the best, but expected the worst. Today, he learned from the Kane County medical staff that he would miss the rest of the season. The Kane County starting pitcher suffered bone chips in his elbow in an earlier ballgame. He admitted to being discouraged, but said he plans to come back strong next year.

Goddard has gone 5-0 this season with an ERA of 3.08 and 65 strikeouts in 61.1 innings pitched. Other clubs are hitting .228 against him.
