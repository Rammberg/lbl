Berberet Wins NWL Batter of the Month
Saturday, August 1st, 2020
Reese Berberet put together a good July to earn the Northwest League Batter of the Month prize. A virtual unknown, he made the most of his chance to prove to Salem-Keizer officials that he belongs on the big league roster.

The Volcanoes first baseman posted a .351 batting average with 2 home runs, 17 RBIs and 11 runs scored.

This year Berberet is batting .324 with 3 home runs and 19 RBIs.
