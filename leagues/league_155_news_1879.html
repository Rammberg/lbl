Albuquerque's Gerber Bags PC Top Batter for August
Tuesday, September 1st, 2020
Mike Gerber has a lot going for him. The Albuquerque center fielder just broke into the majors this year and now the Pacific Conference has just picked him as its top hitter for August.

In 27 games last month this outstanding rookie batted .306 with 7 home runs, 23 RBIs and 22 runs scored.

Thus far this season Gerber is hitting .292 with 24 home runs, 93 RBIs and 85 runs scored. He has 135 hits in 462 at-bats and has played in 118 games.
