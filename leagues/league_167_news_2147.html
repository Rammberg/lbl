Gillian Grabs AZL Reliever of the Year
Saturday, September 19th, 2020
Gage Gillian said he thinks hitters often underestimate him, as he's only 21 years old.

But he'll certainly be getting a lot more respect now, as the Texas (AZL) Rangers rising star has won the Arizona Rookie League Reliever of the Year for 2020.

He captured the award on the strength of his 1.42 ERA over 31.2 innings. He also had 12 saves in 26 relief appearances, 26 strikeouts and only 9 walks.

He received 10 first place votes out of a possible 21. Joey Yancey of the San Diego (AZL) Padres 1 finished second in voting with 10 first place votes, while Nick Robertson of the Los Angeles N (AZL) Dodgers finished third.
