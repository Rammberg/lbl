Williams Sensational, 5-for-5
Friday, July 3rd, 2020
Lehigh Valley IronPigs right fielder Nick Williams had a day to remember at Coca-Cola Park today, ripping apart the Rochester Red Wings and sparking the IronPigs to a 7-6 victory.

Williams wrote his name in the record books with a 5-hit barrage, finishing his special day 5-for-5.

"I'm usually a first ball hitter," Williams told BNN reporters in the postgame press conference. "I think pitchers think batters are going to let the first one go by. I never do. I've always found that it's better to pull the trigger early in the count. Pitchers get real nasty when they get ahead of you, so I try to avoid it."

Nick Williams singled in the 1st, singled in the 4th, singled in the 6th, hit an RBI single in the 7th and hit a three-run home run off Fernando Romero in the 9th.

Williams has collected 71 hits for the IronPigs this year, 13 of them home runs. He has also netted 45 RBIs and scored 42 runs while batting .287.
