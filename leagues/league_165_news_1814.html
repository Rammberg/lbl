Star of the Week Picked in NWL
Monday, August 31st, 2020
This week Jason Pineda, first baseman for the Tri-City Dust Devils, slashed, mashed and bashed his way to the Northwest League Player of the Week trophy.

Pineda put up a .387 average as he pounded out 12 hits, 4 home runs, 11 RBIs and scored 10 runs.

This season Pineda has delivered 80 hits in 269 at-bats for a .297 average with 10 home runs. He is getting on base at a .342 clip and his totals also include 34 RBIs and 47 runs scored. This year Pineda has played in 65 games.
