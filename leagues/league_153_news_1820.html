Seattle's Fans Crushed After Mariners Narrowly Miss Playoffs
Monday, August 31st, 2020
After the Seattle Mariners were mathematically pushed out of a playoff spot today, angry fans of the team bashed the team on social media.

"The team's GM put together all of the pieces needed and we had a great team but management simply blew it," said one local.

"The Seattle Mariners said they would turn the franchise around - I guess they meant 360 degrees," wrote one fan.

"Seattle needs to go back to school and study their chemistry - they obviously don't have any," blasted another fan.

The Seattle Mariners, are expected to again have a very competitive squad in next year's American League West Division playoff race.
