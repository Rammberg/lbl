Week's Best Player Honored by IL
Monday, May 11th, 2020
The premier performer in the International League this week was Daniel Robertson of the Durham Bulls.

The hot-hitting 26-year-old third baseman put up a .750 average and .750 on-base percentage, piling up 6 hits in 8 at-bats, 1 home run, 4 RBIs and 2 runs scored.

That kind of hitting got him the IL Player of the Week honors.

So far this year Robertson is hitting .296 with 3 home runs.
