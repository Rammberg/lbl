League Punishes Pair for Fighting
Friday, August 21st, 2020
Kyle Tucker of the Houston Astros and Mike Minor of the Texas Rangers received notification from the league commissioner's office that they had been suspended for 4 and 8 games respectively for their part in the on-field ruckus in a recent game at Minute Maid Park.

So far this year Tucker is hitting .377 with 40 home runs.

So far this season, Minor has logged a 7-9 record with a 5.08 ERA. He has started 23 games, hurled 117 innings and registered 103 strikeouts and 39 walks.
