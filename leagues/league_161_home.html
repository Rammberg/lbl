Flying Tigers, Baez Buzz Cardinals, 1-0
Monday, May 18th, 2020
Sandy Baez of the Lakeland Flying Tigers humiliated the Palm Beach Cardinals at Joker Marchant Stadium today, spinning a complete game shutout and holding the visitors to 8 hits.

"He stayed aggressive and his ball was really moving," said Palm Beach manager Dann Bilardello, after the game.

Baez whiffed 6 and walked 0 in the 1-0 win.

"If I make good pitches, I'm going to get some outs," Baez said. "I was making good pitches the entire game."

To date this season Baez has compiled a 2-2 record with a 2.95 ERA in 6 starts.
