League Suspends Tortugas Acosta 2 Games
Tuesday, May 5th, 2020
The Daytona Tortugas were not very happy with the home plate umpire in today's loss to St. Lucie at Jackie Robinson Ballpark. One in particular -- Jose Acosta. Upon being called out on strikes, the Tortugas second baseman gave the ump quite an earful. For that, the man in blue gave him the thumb and sent him to an early shower.

But the disgruntled Acosta lingered on the field for another five minutes, berating the umpire. Finally his manager got him off the field and into the clubhouse.

But it got worse after the game, when Acosta told reporters, "He was blind today. They played 'Three Blind Mice' for him. If he doesn't know the words to the song, I'm sure we can get it in Braille, so he can follow along."

For his escapades and after-the-game oration, the league office suspended Acosta for 2 games.

For the season, Acosta is hitting at a .196 clip with no home runs, 3 RBIs and has scored 2 runs. He has 10 hits, 5 walks and a .305 on-base percentage.
