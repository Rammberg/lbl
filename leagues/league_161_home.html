Early Season End for Brettell
Monday, August 3rd, 2020
Clearly dejected, Michael Brettell left the Palm Beach clubhouse nearly in tears this afternoon. He acknowledged that his season is over after sustaining a torn flexor tendon in his elbow in a ballgame recently. It brings an early end to the Palm Beach reliever's season.

So far this year Brettell has no saves in 20 relief appearances with a 2-1 record and 2.65 ERA.
