Florida State League Announces June's Top Pitcher
Wednesday, July 1st, 2020
The Pitcher of the Month has been picked and the Florida State League trophy went to Tampa hurler Albert Abreu for his strong performance in June.

During the month, Abreu was impressive with a 1.45 ERA and 3 wins and no losses. In 31 innings he struck out 29 and gave up 17 hits and 14 walks.

Thus far this season, Abreu has worked 52 innings with 49 strikeouts en route to a 5-2 record and a 3.29 ERA.
Florida State League Announces June's Best Batter
Wednesday, July 1st, 2020
Not known for his offensive prowess, Zac Shepherd set the league afire in June and took home the Florida State League Batter of the Month award.

The Lakeland third baseman posted a steady .300 batting average, 3 home runs, 18 RBIs and 9 runs.

Hitting .300 to date, Shepherd has 3 home runs and 18 RBIs. He has played in 21 games.
