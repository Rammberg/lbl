Tampa Takes Round 1 in Deciding Game
Wednesday, September 16th, 2020
The Round 1 went the distance before it all was said and done.

The Tampa Tarpons and the Clearwater Threshers went at it tooth-and-nail and when it was over at Spectrum Field, the winner was the Tarpons.

They put away Clearwater 3-2 to cinch the series and won the right to move onto the Round 2.

For his fine play, even in a losing effort, Clearwater second baseman Daniel Brito was given the series Most Valuable Player Award. He hit .345, scored 2 runs and batted in 6 to win the honor.

"It's nice that someone noticed my numbers, but I'd trade it in a heartbeat for a win," he said.

The Tampa Tarpons will play against the St. Lucie Mets in the next round. St. Lucie won their series against the Charlotte Stone Crabs 4 games to 1. The Round 2 schedule will be announced after the Round 1 finishes.
