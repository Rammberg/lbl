Shepherd Chosen #1 Player
Monday, June 8th, 2020
His teammates, fans, peers and even the BNN sportswriters all agreed that Zac Shepherd was worthy of the Florida State League Player of the Week Award.

Shepherd is batting .393 this season in 7 games with 2 home runs, 8 RBIs and 5 runs scored. He has 11 hits in 28 at-bats.
