FSL's Star Player Named
Monday, July 27th, 2020
It was a week to remember for Andy Sugilio. After wrapping up a torrid week at the plate, the Daytona Tortugas left fielder was named the Florida State League Player of the Week.

Sugilio collected 10 hits in 22 at-bats for a .455 average. He also accounted for 3 RBIs, 1 home run and scored 3 runs.

In the current season, Sugilio has put up a .291 batting average with 102 hits, 7 home runs, 36 RBI and scored 43 runs.
