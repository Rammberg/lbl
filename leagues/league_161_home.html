Florida State League Picks Top Player
Monday, July 6th, 2020
When it came to hitting the baseball, Cal Mitchell had few peers in the Florida State League this past week.

The talented right fielder for Bradenton tagged opposing pitchers for a .478 average and was voted the Player of the Week Award. Included in his stats were 11 hits in 23 at-bats, 1 homers, 5 runs scored and 6 RBIs.

On the year Mitchell has hit .241 with 13 home runs, 38 RBIs and scored 31 runs.
