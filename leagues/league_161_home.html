Week's Best Player Honored by FSL
Monday, July 13th, 2020
Speak softly and carry a big stick and you could win the Florida State League Player of the Week Award just like Griffin Conine.

He really dazzled on the diamond this week with a .474 average to take home the trophy. The Dunedin right fielder went 9-for-19, drove in 5 runs, scored 5 times and hit 2 home runs.

This year Conine has batted at a .302 pace with 68 hits, 9 home runs, 32 RBIs and scored 25 times.
Fire Frogs, Johnstone Buzz Cardinals, 2-0
Sunday, July 12th, 2020
Connor Johnstone of the Florida Fire Frogs was on fire against the Palm Beach Cardinals at Roger Dean Stadium today. He shut them out 2-0 on 3 hits, collecting 4 strikeouts and allowing 2 walks.

"The first two pitches I'm looking for an out, a ground ball or a fly ball," Johnstone said later. "I'm trying to get quick outs. I've never been a big believer in trying to strike a guy out from the very first pitch. It's too easy to end up behind in the count doing that."

In pitching stats this season Johnstone has rung up 2 wins and 2 losses in 6 games. He has an ERA of 2.83 and has fanned 25 batters in 41.1 innings.
