St. Lucie Advances to Round 2
Sunday, September 13th, 2020
By a score of 5-0, the St. Lucie Mets have sealed a victory in the Round 1 over the Charlotte Stone Crabs.

"We knew we would be in for a tough series and that's what we got," said third baseman Mark Vientos of the Mets, who was named the series MVP. "The Stone Crabs knew how to battle and gave us a good test."

The next test for St. Lucie will come in the Round 2, after winning this series 4-1.

The St. Lucie Mets don't know who they will face in the next round. They will play the winner of the Clearwater Threshers - Tampa Tarpons series. Tampa leads the series 3 games to 2. The Round 2 schedule will be announced after the Round 1 finishes.
