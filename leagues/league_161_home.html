Sensley Nabs FSL Weekly Award
Monday, August 10th, 2020
It was announced today that Tampa first baseman Steven Sensley had received the Florida State League Player of the Week Award for his impressive play last week.

All week long he was terrific at the plate and won the honor with a .417 BA (10-for-24), 2 home runs and 7 RBIs.

Currently Sensley is batting .247 with 37 hits and 4 home runs. He has knocked in 18 runs and scored 23 times.
