April's Best Hurler in FSL Is Mears
Friday, May 1st, 2020
"The kid had it all working last month," a veteran teammate told reporters. "He keeps up those kind of numbers, we might have to start inviting him out with us after the games."

Nick Mears put together a very solid month to take home the Florida State League's top pitching honor in April.

The 23-year-old reliever threw like a veteran. In 6 relief appearances he raked in 4 saves and 8 strikeouts in 7.2 innings as he put together his 2-0 record and 0.00 ERA.
Jupiter's Misner FSL #1 Batter in April
Friday, May 1st, 2020
Kameron Misner is not used to being associated with Batter of the Month-caliber players, but today the Jupiter center fielder joined that select company when the Florida State League announced the results of April's voting.

Misner totaled 3 hits, 1 walk, 2 home runs, 3 RBIs and 3 runs scored last month to put together a 1.000 batting average and 1.000 on-base percentage.
Tom Hackimer Gets Diagnosis, Out 4 Months
Friday, May 1st, 2020
The medical report is in: the injury that has kept Fort Myers closer Tom Hackimer out of action since 04/29/2020 will sideline him for another 4 months. The 25-year-old Hackimer, who has a torn labrum, said he heals quickly. "I'll be back soon," he said, "and I'll pick up where I left off. "

In 2020 Hackimer has totaled 7 relief appearances, worked 6.1 innings, struck out 10 and issued 1 walk. He has 4 saves and fashioned a 0.00 ERA and a 2-0 won-lost mark.
