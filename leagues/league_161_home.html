Stone Crabs Gray Snags FSL Player of the Week
Monday, June 29th, 2020
So far in his young career, Tristan Gray has kept a low profile, but after his performance at the plate last week, he attracted the notice of the rest of the Florida State League in a big way as he was named the Player of the Week today for his efforts.

The 24-year-old Charlotte Stone Crabs second baseman hit at a torrid .571 clip as he collected 8 hits in 14 at-bats, 1 home run, 4 RBIs and 3 runs scored.

This season Gray has hit 1 home run and posted a .471 batting average.
