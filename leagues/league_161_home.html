Hernandez on a Hot Streak, FSL Player of the Week
Monday, May 4th, 2020
It was a fantastic week for Charlotte catcher Ronaldo Hernandez and it was capped off today with the announcement that he was named the winner of the Florida State League Player of the Week Award.

Last week Hernandez hammered the ball at a torrid .474 pace, going 9-for-19 with no home runs, 8 RBIs and 5 runs scored.

Thus far this year Hernandez has hit .250 with 1 home run and 12 RBIs.
