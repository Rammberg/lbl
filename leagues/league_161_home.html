Tortugas, Johnson Buzz Fire Frogs, 2-0
Wednesday, April 29th, 2020
Jordan Johnson of the Daytona Tortugas was one tough customer on the hill today, blanking the Florida Fire Frogs 2-0 on 1 hit.

"When he's on, he's tough to hit," said Florida manager Barrett Kleinknecht after the game. "He's just a bulldog out there."

The 26-year-old Tortugas right-hander registered 5 strikeouts and allowed 2 walks and 1 hit.
Injury Sidelines Humphreys for Season
Wednesday, April 29th, 2020
In a news release, the St. Lucie Mets announced that starting pitcher Jordan Humphreys would miss the rest of the season due to a partially torn ulnar collateral ligament. Humphreys sustained the injury on 04/28/2020. He "will be sorely missed," said his manager.
