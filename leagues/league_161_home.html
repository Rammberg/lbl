Season Done for Winder
Monday, May 11th, 2020
Clearly dejected, Josh Winder left the Fort Myers clubhouse nearly in tears this afternoon. He acknowledged that his season is over after sustaining a torn labrum in a ballgame recently. It brings an early end to the Fort Myers starting pitcher's season.

In 5 starts this season Winder has chalked up a 1-2 record and a 5.24 ERA.
Pitcher Wins Week's Top Player Award
Monday, May 11th, 2020
Palm Beach's Ian Oxnevad is only 23, but last week he put up numbers that would make a seasoned veteran proud. That's why the young hurler earned the Florida State League Player of the Week award.

Oxnevad tallied 2 wins, 7 strikeouts in 11.1 innings and a 0.00 ERA for the week.

Thus far this season, Oxnevad has worked 26 innings with 16 strikeouts en route to a 3-1 record and a 2.42 ERA.
