Kernels Matt Wallner Wins MID Award
Monday, August 10th, 2020
What's in a number? In baseball, just about everything.

The numbers that Cedar Rapids's Matt Wallner put up last week were the envy of the league, as Wallner came away with Midwest League Player of the Week honors.

He belted out 13 hits in 26 at-bats, 2 home runs and 8 RBIs last week as he posted his .500 batting average.

So far this season Wallner has hit .298 with 10 home runs, 51 RBIs and scored 41 times.
