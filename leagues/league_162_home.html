MID Player of the Week Named
Monday, May 25th, 2020
The Cedar Rapids Kernels received word today that second baseman Yunior Severino was selected Midwest League Player of the Week for his stellar performance in last week's games.

Severino took full advantage of all 28 plate appearances last week as he hit .520 with 13 hits, 1 home run, 9 RBIs and 3 runs scored.

For the season Severino is batting .321 with 42 hits and 4 home runs. He has scored 10 times and batted in 23.
