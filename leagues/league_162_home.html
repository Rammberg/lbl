League Suspends De La Cruz and Doxakis for Fisticuffs
Monday, June 15th, 2020
Isrrael De La Cruz of West Michigan and John Doxakis of Bowling Green were the main participants in a recent bench-clearing brawl and today the league handed out its punishment. De La Cruz will have to miss 4 games for his part in the brawl, while Doxakis was given 6 games. League officials announced their suspensions today.

So far this year De La Cruz is batting .188 with 1 home run, 13 RBIs and 10 runs scored.

So far this year Doxakis has made 3 starts, tossed 32.1 innings, struck out 36 batters and walked 8. His record is 2-0 with a 2.51 ERA and he has limited opposing teams to a .191 batting average.
