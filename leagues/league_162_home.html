Injury Brings Season to End for Pucheu
Friday, May 29th, 2020
The season will end prematurely for Jacques Pucheu, the Dayton reliever. He suffered a ruptured finger tendon on 05/28/2020, an injury that is serious enough to knock him out of action for the rest of the year. Pucheu was not available for comment.

Pucheu has 1 save in 15 relief appearances this season with a 0-1 record and 1.35 ERA.
