Martinez Wins MID Best Player Award
Monday, July 20th, 2020
It was announced today that Kane County first baseman Francis Martinez was selected the Midwest League Player of the Week Award for his superb play last week.

During the week he compiled a .600 batting average (12-for-20) with 1 home run, 10 RBI and scored 4 runs.

This year Martinez has batted at a .248 pace with 38 hits, 3 home runs, 28 RBIs and scored 18 times.
