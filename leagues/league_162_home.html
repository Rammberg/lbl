Beloit-Dayton Fight Results in Suspensions
Thursday, July 16th, 2020
Max Schuemann of the Beloit Snappers and Mac Sceroler of the Dayton Dragons will be on the sidelines for a few days. They have been suspended for 4 and 6 games respectively by the league office for causing the bench-emptying free-for-all at the recent Snappers-Dragons game at Harry C. Pohlman Field.

Neither player had any comment about the suspensions. Both clubs told BNN Schuemann and Sceroler will begin serving them soon.

Currently Schuemann is batting .227 with 20 hits and 1 home run. He has knocked in 5 runs and scored 11 times.

This year Sceroler has worked 55.2 innings with 35 strikeouts en route to a 3-2 record and 4.04 ERA.
