Cedar Rapids Loses Theetge for Season
Monday, August 3rd, 2020
A blow for Steve Theetge today -- team doctors were able to determine the full extent of his recent injury. He will miss 6-7 months with a torn rotator cuff. Theetge suffered the injury on 07/31/2020. The team will likely place Theetge on the injured list today.

So far this season, in 9 relief appearances Theetge has compiled an ERA of 5.19. He has pitched 17.1 innings, struck out 13, walked 7 and registered no saves.
