Cubs Cruise to Round 2 with Sweep
Saturday, September 12th, 2020
With a 6-1 victory at Cooley Law School Stadium, the South Bend Cubs have completed a sweep of the Lansing Lugnuts in the Round 1.

"On to the Round 2 and feeling good," said Levi Jordan. "I don't think our guys expected a sweep, but we'll certainly take it and rest up a bit."

For the series, Jordan hit .727 and won series MVP honors. He had 0 home runs, drove in 2 RBI and scored 4 runs.

The South Bend Cubs don't know who they will face in the next round. They will play the winner of the Clinton LumberKings - Kane County Cougars series. Clinton leads the series 2 games to 1. The Round 2 schedule will be announced after the Round 1 finishes.
Quad Cities to Lose France for 11 Months
Wednesday, September 9th, 2020
The Quad Cities River Bandits have made it official: J.P. France is headed to the injured list. The 25-year-old reliever has been out since 09/07/2020 with a partially torn ulnar collateral ligament. He is expected to miss 11 months.

This season France has made 29 relief appearances, recorded no saves and compiled a 2-0 won-lost mark and a 4.07 ERA. In pitching 48.2 innings, he has totaled 35 strikeouts and 25 walks.
