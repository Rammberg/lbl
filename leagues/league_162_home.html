MID Announces Player of the Week
Monday, July 6th, 2020
Trey Truitt is respected by most Kane County Cougars fans for his no-frills style of play. The 24-year-old center fielder went about his business with vigor last week to grab the Midwest League Player of the Week honors.

He compiled a .417 average (10-for-24) with 3 home runs, 4 RBIs and 5 runs scored.

Hitting .283 to date, Truitt has 4 home runs and 9 RBIs. He has played in 29 games.
