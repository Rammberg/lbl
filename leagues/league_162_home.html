Thomas Tabbed as May's Starter of the Month
Monday, June 1st, 2020
When Connor Thomas takes the hill, he usually takes command of the game -- at least that's the way it was in May. The outcome was usually a Peoria victory. His fine 4-0 performance earned him the Midwest League Pitcher of the Month trophy.

The talented Chiefs hurler made 6 starts, worked 35 innings, permitted 29 base hits, and struck out 18 while putting up a 1.29 ERA.

Thomas has gone 5-1 this season with an ERA of 2.35 and 21 strikeouts in 46 innings pitched. Other clubs are hitting .234 against him.
Herbert Wins MID Prize, Named Best Hitter in May
Monday, June 1st, 2020
Lucas Herbert is not used to being associated with Batter of the Month-caliber players, but today the Kane County catcher joined that select company when the Midwest League announced the results of May's voting.

Herbert totaled 33 hits, 9 walks, 3 home runs, 13 RBIs and 17 runs scored last month to put together a .398 batting average and .447 on-base percentage.

For the season Herbert is hitting .379, batted in 18 runs with 5 home runs and 22 runs scored.
