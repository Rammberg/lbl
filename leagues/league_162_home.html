Hurtado Is April's Top Starter
Friday, May 1st, 2020
The Midwest League named the winner of the Pitcher of the Month Award.
It went to Jhenderson Hurtado of Beloit Snappers, who impressed the voters with a glittering 3-0 record in April.

The Beloit star hurler made 3 starts, allowed 14 hits in 20.1 innings, struck out 19 and had a 2.21 ERA.
Abreu MID Outstanding Batter of the Month
Friday, May 1st, 2020
It's perhaps an award he'll win many more times through his career, as at age 20 Pablo Abreu has a lot of baseball ahead of him.

But for now the young Wisconsin Timber Rattlers star can enjoy the numbers he put up in April, which have garnered him the nod as the Midwest League Batter of the Month.

His breakout month featured a .383 average with 2 home runs, 5 RBIs and 10 runs scored.
Puckett Shackles Bowling Green
Wednesday, April 29th, 2020
Brady Puckett of the Clinton LumberKings humiliated the Bowling Green Hot Rods at NelsonCorp Field today, spinning a complete game shutout and holding the visitors to 1 hit.

"He stayed aggressive and his ball was really moving," said Bowling Green manager Blake Butera, after the game.

Puckett whiffed 6 and walked 1 in the 3-0 win.

"If I make good pitches, I'm going to get some outs," Puckett said. "I was making good pitches the entire game."
