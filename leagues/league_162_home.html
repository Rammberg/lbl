Assad Out 4 Months
Wednesday, September 23rd, 2020
The South Bend Cubs have informed Javier Assad that he is likely heading to the injured list. The 23-year-old starting pitcher has been sidelined since 09/21/2020 with a torn meniscus. Recent tests have confirmed the extent of the injury. It will be at least 4 months before Assad returns to the field.

Lifetime Assad has registered a 6-9 record with a 4.26 ERA.

South Bend Cubs Are Champs
Monday, September 21st, 2020
It was all about winning for the South Bend Cubs -- and win they did.

There was nothing fancy in how they went about it, players said, they just prepared mentally and physically and then went out and played baseball.

"We did the little things right and we did the big things right," said second baseman Levi Jordan, who was named series MVP. "Now we're the league champions."

South Bend defeated the Kane County Cougars by a score of 7-2 at Northwestern Medicine Field to take the series 3-1.

"Sometimes you get lucky and sometimes you make your own luck," said South Bend manager Buddy Bailey. "We got lucky a few times, but mostly we did the right things by being prepared for each game and playing the game the way it's meant to be played."
