Carter Nets MID #1 Pitcher Award
Wednesday, July 1st, 2020
"The kid had it all working last month," a veteran teammate told reporters. "He keeps up those kind of numbers, we might have to start inviting him out with us after the games."

C.J. Carter put together a very solid month to take home the Midwest League's top pitching honor in June.

The 23-year-old reliever threw like a veteran. In 13 relief appearances he raked in 12 saves and 17 strikeouts in 13 innings as he put together his 0-1 record and 0.69 ERA.

This season Carter has 12 saves with a 0-1 mark and 0.69 ERA.
MID's Best Batter Announced
Wednesday, July 1st, 2020
It's perhaps an award he'll win many more times through his career, as at age 23 Lee Solomon has a lot of baseball ahead of him.

But for now the young Fort Wayne TinCaps star can enjoy the numbers he put up in June, which have garnered him the nod as the Midwest League Batter of the Month.

His breakout month featured a .330 average with 3 home runs, 13 RBIs and 15 runs scored.

Thus far this year Solomon is batting .282 with 3 home runs, 17 RBIs and 22 runs scored.
