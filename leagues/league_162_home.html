No Comeback This Season for Williams
Monday, August 31st, 2020
Chris Williams had hoped for the best, but expected the worst. Today, he learned from the Kane County medical staff that he would miss the rest of the season. The Kane County reliever suffered shoulder inflammation in an earlier ballgame. He admitted to being discouraged, but said he plans to come back strong next year.

To this point in the season he has had no saves. Williams has made 13 relief appearances, tossed 20 innings and struck out 15 with a record of 1 win and no losses.
