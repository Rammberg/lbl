Stubbs MID's Best of the Week
Monday, June 29th, 2020
All week long nobody could get C.J. Stubbs out in the Midwest League and that is the prime reason he was voted the Player of the Week.

The Quad Cities catcher took home the trophy by blasting 6 hits in 8 at-bats for a .750 batting average, driving in 2 runs and scoring 3 times.

Stubbs has a .229 average and a .298 on-base percentage for the year. He has picked up 33 hits, 3 home runs and driven in 12 runs.
Western Division Defeats Eastern Division 3-1 in Tight All-Star Game
Tuesday, June 23rd, 2020
Lucas Herbert cemented his status as one of the best in the game with his performance tonight.

"It's an honor to even be invited to the game. To be rewarded as the MVP of the game is extra special," the Western Division star said after his great showing. "Just playing on the same diamond as some of these guys is a pure treat."

In the game itself, Lucas Herbert certainly wasn't the only great player out there, but he did enough in the Western Division's 3-1 win over the Eastern Division All-Stars.
