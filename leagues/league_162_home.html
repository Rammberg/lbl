Midwest League Winner is Beck
Monday, July 13th, 2020
The Beloit Snappers received word today that center fielder Austin Beck was selected Midwest League Player of the Week for his stellar performance in last week's games.

Beck took full advantage of all 26 plate appearances last week as he hit .476 with 10 hits, 1 home run, 9 RBIs and 2 runs scored.

So far this season Beck has put up a .218 batting average with 45 hits, 1 home run and 24 RBIs.
