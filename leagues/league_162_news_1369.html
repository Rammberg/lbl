Trevor Craport Wins MID Trophy
Monday, August 3rd, 2020
The Midwest League has named its Top Player of the Week. The honor went to Trevor Craport, third baseman of the South Bend Cubs.

In an extraordinary hitting display, he belted out hits at a .545 pace to win the award. Craport went 12-for-22 for the week, totaled no home runs, 4 RBIs and scored 2 times.

This season Craport has hit at a .250 clip with 13 RBIs, 1 home runs and 9 runs scored. He has played in 38 games.
