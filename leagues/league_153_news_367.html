NL's Top Player, Kendrick of Washington
Monday, May 25th, 2020
Howie Kendrick had a week for the ages as he romped to the National League Player of the Week Award. The happy-go-lucky 36-year-old still plays ball like a kid, mashing and slashing as he goes.

The Washington Nationals second baseman hit .500 last week as he chalked up 22 hits in 44 at-bats, 2 home runs, 20 RBIs and 15 runs scored.

This season Kendrick has hit at a .379 clip with 33 RBIs, 3 home runs and 25 runs scored.
