June's SAL Batter of Month Award Announced
Wednesday, July 1st, 2020
Eric Wagaman is not used to being associated with Batter of the Month-caliber players, but today the Charleston first baseman joined that select company when the South Atlantic League announced the results of June's voting.

Wagaman totaled 26 hits, 8 walks, 7 home runs, 25 RBIs and 16 runs scored last month to put together a .271 batting average and .343 on-base percentage.

This year Wagaman is batting .233 with 12 home runs. He has scored 32 runs and driven in 51.
