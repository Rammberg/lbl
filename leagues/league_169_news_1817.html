Player of the Week: Ogden's De Jongh
Monday, August 31st, 2020
Aldrich De Jongh of the Ogden Raptors had an award-winning week and today was voted the Player of the Week by the Pioneer League.

For the week the 21-year-old left fielder batted .531 (17-for-32) with 2 home runs and 10 RBIs.

Thus far this season De Jongh is hitting .328 with 4 home runs, 21 RBIs and 45 runs scored. He has 83 hits in 253 at-bats and has played in 60 games.
