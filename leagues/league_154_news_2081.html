Bulls Win Round 1
Sunday, September 13th, 2020
Michael Brosseau put the Durham Bulls on his shoulders as they downed the Rochester Red Wings in the Round 1.

Durham won the deciding game by a 10-0 count at Frontier Field and wrapped up the series 3-1.

Brosseau played a key role in the victory, as he was named series MVP for his offensive output, which included a .438 batting average with a .526 on-base percentage, 2 home runs, 4 RBI and 5 runs scored.

"I told myself it was time to step it up and help the team win and I'm glad I was able to do that," he said.

The Durham Bulls will play against the Lehigh Valley IronPigs in the next round. Lehigh Valley won their series against the Indianapolis Indians 3 games to 0. The Round 2 schedule will be announced after the Round 1 finishes.
