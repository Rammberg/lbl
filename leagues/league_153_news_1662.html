Ozzie Albies of Atlanta Injured, DTD
Wednesday, August 19th, 2020
The topic du jour around Atlanta is whether Ozzie Albies of the Braves will suit up for today's contest. He was injured yesterday against the Brewers while running the bases. The standout 23-year-old second baseman has a knee sprain and didn't rule out playing, but did say that if pain persisted, he would not risk further injury.

"Ozzie will play if he can," said the team doctor. "He hasn't got a major injury, but at the same time, we need to monitor the situation and protect him as much as possible."

This season in 123 games Albies is batting .310 with 22 home runs, 98 RBIs and has scored 103 times.
