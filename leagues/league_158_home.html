Taveras Bags TEX Best Player Award
Monday, July 20th, 2020
Leody Taveras demonstrated his potential to be a star in the game last week and today he took home the Texas League Player of the Week Award.

The Frisco center fielder wreaked havoc on opposing pitchers last week, pounding them for a .625 average (15-for-24) with 3 home runs and 9 RBIs on his way to claiming the honor.

This season Taveras is hitting .329 with 91 hits, 9 home runs, 42 RBIs, scored 42 runs and played in 70 games.
