Texas League Names Raleigh Best Batter
Monday, August 31st, 2020
Cal Raleigh burst onto the Texas League stage in a big way last week. This promising rookie catcher for Arkansas garnered everyone's respect by putting up some remarkable numbers and picking up the Player of the Week trophy.

The 23-year-old haunted opposing pitchers hitting .400 (10-for-25) with 3 home runs and 5 RBIs last week.

For the year Raleigh is hitting .284 with 25 home runs. His stats include 63 runs batted in and 59 runs scored.
