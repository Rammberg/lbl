TEX Player of the Week Named
Monday, April 27th, 2020
Jarred Kelenic is respected by most Arkansas Travelers fans for his no-frills style of play. The 20-year-old center fielder went about his business with vigor last week to grab the Texas League Player of the Week honors.

He compiled a .407 average (11-for-27) with 2 home runs, 7 RBIs and 8 runs scored.

In 11 games Kelenic is hitting .375 this season with 15 hits, 3 home runs, 9 RBIs, 7 walks, .468 OBP and has scored 10 runs.
