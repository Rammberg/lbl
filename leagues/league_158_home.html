Midland-Frisco Fight Results in Suspensions
Thursday, June 18th, 2020
Luis Barrera of the Midland RockHounds and Taylor Jungmann of the Frisco RoughRiders received notification from the league commissioner's office that they had been suspended for 3 and 7 games respectively for their part in the on-field ruckus in a recent game at Security Bank Ballpark.

On the year Barrera is hitting .221 with 50 hits, 1 home run, 15 RBIs, .301 OBP and has scored 28 runs.

Jungmann has taken the mound for 11 relief appearances this season, nailing down no saves while notching a 2-0 record with a 4.70 ERA. The Frisco reliever has limited opposing batters to a .205 batting average.
