Rowdy Players Suspended by League
Wednesday, July 8th, 2020
Yesterday at Whataburger Field Bryan De La Cruz and Kutter Crawford got into each other's space and then into each other's face. They began to grapple and fell to the ground. That's when it boiled over into a full-fledged bench-clearing tussle between the Corpus Christi Hooks and Northwest Arkansas Naturals. Reason finally prevailed as the umpires restored order. After much discussion, only the two major combatants were ejected from the game.

Today the league office notified the ballclubs that De La Cruz will have to sit out 3 while Crawford would be given a 6-game suspension for their roles in the brawl.

This year De La Cruz is hitting .223, produced 19 RBIs and 5 home runs, scored 27 times and gotten on base at a .276 rate.

Ruckus Reaps 4-Game Suspensions
Monday, July 6th, 2020
The league commissioner's office announced today that Jacob Amaya of the Drillers and Ryan Meisinger of the Cardinals have both been suspended for 4 games for fighting in the recent game between Springfield and Tulsa at ONEOK Field.

Currently Amaya is batting .261 with 2 home runs and 28 RBIs.

Meisinger has taken the mound for 17 relief appearances this season, nailing down 1 save while notching a 1-0 record with a 1.38 ERA. The Springfield reliever has limited opposing batters to a .165 batting average.
