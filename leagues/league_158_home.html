Heim Picked Best Player
Monday, July 27th, 2020
Jonah Heim demonstrated his potential to be a star in the game last week and today he took home the Texas League Player of the Week Award.

The Midland catcher wreaked havoc on opposing pitchers last week, pounding them for a .556 average (10-for-18) with 1 home run and 2 RBIs on his way to claiming the honor.

Heim has collected 96 hits for the RockHounds this year, 8 of them home runs. He has also netted 46 RBIs and scored 39 runs while batting .324.
