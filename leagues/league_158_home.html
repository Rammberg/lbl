TEX Star of the Week Chosen
Monday, June 15th, 2020
Steele Walker went on a week-long batting spree against Texas League pitchers and hauled in the Player of the Week trophy.

The Frisco left fielder battered the opposition for 10 hits in 23 at-bats, 2 home runs, 7 RBIs and scored 4 runs. He hit .435 and posted a .481 on-base percentage.

So far on the season Walker is hitting .292 with 8 home runs, 37 RBIs and 32 runs scored. He has 57 hits and played in 54 games.
