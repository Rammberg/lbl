TEX's Premier Starter is Jurado
Wednesday, July 1st, 2020
Ariel Jurado of Frisco had an outstanding June with a 4-0 mark and was chosen the Pitcher of the Month in the Texas League.

He topped off the month with a 3.00 ERA in 5 starts, tossed 39 innings, fanned 27 and held opposing teams to a .187 average.

For the year Jurado has a 8-0 record with a 2.39 ERA in 11 starts.
TEX's Premier Batter Picked
Wednesday, July 1st, 2020
Jeren Kendall made his presence known as one of the best young stars in the Texas League this past month.

The Tulsa Drillers center fielder shined through the month of June and has won the Batter of the Month award.

The 24-year-old hit .319 with 7 home runs, 13 RBIs and 20 runs scored.

Presently Kendall is batting .267 with 55 hits and 11 home runs. He has knocked in 20 runs and scored 38 times.
