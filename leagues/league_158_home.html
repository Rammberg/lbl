No Comeback This Season for Sierra
Tuesday, May 26th, 2020
The season will end prematurely for Yaisel Sierra, the Tulsa reliever. He suffered a torn ulnar collateral ligament on 05/23/2020, an injury that is serious enough to knock him out of action for the rest of the year. Sierra was not available for comment.

Sierra's record so far this season is 1-and-0 with no saves and a 2.89 ERA. He has given up 11 hits in 18.2 innings with 17 strikeouts.
