Tulsa's Chiu TEX's Best
Monday, July 13th, 2020
It can be difficult for a young player to step out of the shadows and assert himself in the Texas League, but last week Marcus Chiu of the Tulsa Drillers reminded everyone just why he's here as he threw up numbers that made him an easy selection for Player of the Week. Chiu rocked opposing pitching for a .483 average (14-for-29), 1 home run and 7 RBIs to come away with the award.

So far this season Chiu is hitting .294 with 1 home run.
