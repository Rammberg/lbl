Atlanta Punchless in 14-0 Loss to Marlins
Friday, May 8th, 2020
Pablo Lopez of the Miami Marlins spun his magic on the Atlanta Braves today. He blanked them 14-0 on 4 hits, registering 4 strikeouts and 1 walk.

"The bottom line was I just kept the ball down," Lopez said. "My breaking stuff was down. Everything was down."

Talking to reporters in the postgame interview, Miami skipper Don Mattingly said the team "got solid pitching, good defense and took great team at-bats in a lot of different situations."

"Pablo pitched really well but this was a solid performance by everyone," he said.

Lopez has fashioned a 4-1 mark for the season, 0.75 ERA and thrown 36 innings in 5 starts. Opposing clubs are batting .172 against him.
