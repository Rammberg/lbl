Barons Vaughn Named SL's #1 Player
Monday, June 29th, 2020
It was an extraordinary week for Andrew Vaughn of the Birmingham Barons. First he goes on a hot hitting streak, batting .500, then he is voted the Player of the Week in the Southern League. Last week Vaughn went 7-for-14, racked up 1 home run and 3 RBIs and scored 3 runs. It was a heck of a week for the Birmingham third baseman.

Vaughn on the season is hitting .297 with 7 home runs. His stats include 37 RBIs and 33 runs scored.
