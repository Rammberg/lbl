Reno's Young Gets PC Player of the Week
Monday, August 10th, 2020
At the age of 26, Andy Young might have some potential he hasn't realized yet -- if his success at the plate last week is any indication. The Reno second baseman put up some exceptional numbers the past seven days with a .583 batting average. For his efforts Young collected the Pacific Conference Player of the Week Award.

He racked up 14 hits in 24 at-bats, 2 home runs and 5 RBIs last week in 6 games.

Presently Young is batting .314 with 113 hits and 18 home runs. He has knocked in 60 runs and scored 65 times.
