5-Hit Blitz by Foggo
Tuesday, August 18th, 2020
Eric Foggo had an answer for everything the San Diego (AZL) Padres 2 threw at him today at Peoria Sports Complex. The Milwaukee (AZL) first baseman went 5-for-5 in the game to put his name in the record book and put the Brewers Blue on top, 10-6.

Summing up his batting philosophy to BNN writers after the game, Foggo said, "I just see the ball and I hit it."

Eric Foggo singled in the 1st, doubled in the 3rd, singled in the 5th, singled in the 7th and hit an RBI single in the 8th.

In the 2020 season Foggo is hitting .247 with 3 home runs.
