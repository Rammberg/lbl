Red-Hot Rampage Reaps IL Reward
Monday, July 20th, 2020
A hot-hitting week got Jose Osuna the International League Player of the Week trophy. He really wore out his welcome with league pitchers.

The Indianapolis first baseman slammed them for a .438 average, 7 hits in 16 at-bats, 3 home runs, 6 RBIs and scored 4 runs.

In the postgame press conference, Osuna shared his hitting philosophy with the BNN scribes: "Just look for the seams and hit between them... nothin' more than that."

Presently Osuna is batting .318 with 103 hits and 10 home runs. He has knocked in 47 runs and scored 52 times.
