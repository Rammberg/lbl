Injury Shuts Down Campos for Season
Thursday, August 13th, 2020
"Not what I wanted to hear," said Detroit (DSL)'s Ulices Campos to reporters this afternoon, as he revealed that the injury he sustained on 08/11/2020 would force him to miss the rest of the season. "The doctor made it clear," Campos said. "There's no chance for me -- none at all -- to come back this year. That's the last thing I wanted to hear." Campos has been out of action since he suffered a bone spur in his elbow.

This season Campos has claimed 2 saves and registered a 0-1 record with a 1.64 ERA. He has 18 relief appearances spanning 22 innings and has struck out 10.
