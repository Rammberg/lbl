Cubs Win Tight National League Central Division Race
Sunday, September 27th, 2020
It's a banner year for the Chicago Cubs, they have won the National League Central Division.

"There's so much pride in our room and on our roster, anything but a division title wouldn't have been acceptable," said starting pitcher Jose Quintana. "In the playoffs we'll just have to take it one day at a time and keep battling."

The Cubs this year have battled to a 89-72 mark, which is good for the club's 24th National League Central Division title.

"I'm extremely proud of my players and look forward to seeing what we can do in the postseason," said manager David Ross.
