Grizzlies Williamson PC Hitter of the Week
Monday, July 20th, 2020
Mac Williamson of the Fresno Grizzlies had a super week and today was voted the Player of the Week by the Pacific Conference.

The 30-year-old right fielder put up a .389 average with a .389 on-base percentage, racking up 7 hits in 18 at-bats, 1 home run, 5 RBIs and 4 runs scored.

Williamson is hitting .251 for the season with 16 home runs, 55 RBIs and 49 runs scored. He has 74 hits in 295 at-bats and played in 80 games.
