Phillies Chill Tucker's Hot Streak
Thursday, July 9th, 2020
Kyle Tucker's 35-game hitting streak has now come and gone. It was snapped today as the Houston Astros suffered a 4-0 setback against the Philadelphia Phillies.

Tucker went without a hit in 4 trips to the plate. He was visibly upset during a postgame press conference, but it appeared that the streak had little to do with it.

"The hitting streak was one of those things where it was fun while it lasted, but I'm not going to lose any sleep about it tonight," he said. "On the other hand, I am going to lose sleep about dropping this game today."

In his 2020 season totals, Tucker has 27 home runs, .374 BA, .435 OBP, 81 RBIs and 97 runs scored.
