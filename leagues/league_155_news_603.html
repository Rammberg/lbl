Redbirds Listless in 7-0 Loss to Grizzlies
Sunday, June 14th, 2020
Austen Williams of the Fresno Grizzlies spun his magic on the Memphis Redbirds today. He blanked them 7-0 on 5 hits, registering 5 strikeouts and 1 walk.

"The bottom line was I just kept the ball down," Williams said. "My breaking stuff was down. Everything was down."

Talking to reporters in the postgame interview, Fresno skipper Randy Knorr said the team "got solid pitching, good defense and took great team at-bats in a lot of different situations."

"Austen pitched really well but this was a solid performance by everyone," he said.

This season Williams has worked 67 innings with 43 strikeouts en route to a 2-5 record and a 4.43 ERA.
