Kepler Hittin' on All Cylinders
Tuesday, June 9th, 2020
Finishing with 6 hits is a great day for any player, but Max Kepler made it even more special by hitting for the cycle at Oriole Park at Camden Yards today.

Kepler had 2 singles, 2 doubles, 1 triple and 1 home run for the Minnesota Twins and led them to a 23-3 decision over the Baltimore Orioles. The right fielder also finished with 2 runs scored and 9 runs batted in.

"Awesome," Kepler said when asked how the accomplishment felt.

Max Kepler hit a solo-shot off Alex Cobb in the 1st, hit an RBI double in the 3rd, hit an RBI triple in the 4th, singled in the 6th, hit a two-run single in the 7th, hit a sac fly in the 8th and hit a three-run double in the 9th.

In 53 games this season Kepler has batted at a .280 pace with 53 hits and 10 home runs.
