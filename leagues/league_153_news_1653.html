Arizona Southpaw Tosses 6-0 Shutout
Tuesday, August 18th, 2020
Today was Madison Bumgarner's turn in the rotation for the Arizona Diamondbacks and he made the most of it, shutting out the Kansas City Royals on 4 hits.

The 31-year-old left-hander led the Diamondbacks to an easy 6-0 victory, allowing no walks and tallying 7 strikeouts.

"I hope we miss him next time," Kansas City manager Mike Matheny said. "Bumgarner certainly pitched a strong game against us today. He was in a groove, throwing everything for strikes. Throwing hard. Changing speeds."

This season Bumgarner has worked 155 innings with 143 strikeouts en route to a 12-7 record and a 2.85 ERA.
