SAL's Star Player Named
Monday, August 10th, 2020
With some great hand-eye coordination and a sweet, compact swing, Greenville Drive right fielder Pedro Castellanos took the South Atlantic League by storm this past week to take the latest Player of the Week honors.

"The ball was looking like a beach ball all week," a happy Castellanos said to BNN reporters. "When it's that big, it doesn't matter where they throw it -- I can hit it anywhere."

The 22-year-old right fielder hit .560 (14-for-25) last week with no home runs, 4 RBIs and 5 runs scored.

Castellanos is swinging at a .308 pace on the year with 9 home runs, 62 RBIs and 65 runs scored. He has played in 99 games.
