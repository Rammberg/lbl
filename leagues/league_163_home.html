Season Over for Bilous
Monday, May 11th, 2020
"Not what I wanted to hear," said Kannapolis's Jason Bilous to reporters this afternoon, as he revealed that the injury he sustained on 05/10/2020 would force him to miss the rest of the season. "The doctor made it clear," Bilous said. "There's no chance for me -- none at all -- to come back this year. That's the last thing I wanted to hear." Bilous has been out of action since he suffered a partially torn ulnar collateral ligament.
