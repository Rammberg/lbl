RiverDogs Eric Wagaman Swings Hot Bat, SAL Winner
Monday, June 8th, 2020
When it came to hitting the baseball, Eric Wagaman had few peers in the South Atlantic League this past week.

The talented first baseman for Charleston tagged opposing pitchers for a .458 average and was voted the Player of the Week Award. Included in his stats were 11 hits in 24 at-bats, 3 homers, 4 runs scored and 10 RBIs.

This season Wagaman is batting at a .242 clip with 8 home runs and 36 RBIs.
