GreenJackets Hurler Named May's #1 Starter
Monday, June 1st, 2020
Keaton Winn of the Augusta GreenJackets strung a bunch of high quality starts together in May to earn the South Atlantic League Pitcher of the Month trophy.

For the month he finished with a superlative 3 wins and no losses and a 1.09 ERA. The Augusta star started 6 games, pitched 33 innings and struck out 28.

To date this year, Winn shows a 3-2 record, 9 starts, 45.1 innings, 37 strikeouts and a 2.98 ERA.
Massey Earns May's Top Batter Prize in SAL
Monday, June 1st, 2020
Michael Massey put together a good May to earn the South Atlantic League Batter of the Month prize. A virtual unknown, he made the most of his chance to prove to Lexington officials that he belongs on the big league roster.

The Legends second baseman posted a .379 batting average with 5 home runs, 15 RBIs and 17 runs scored.

So far this season Massey has put up a .284 batting average with 27 hits, 6 home runs and 18 RBIs.
