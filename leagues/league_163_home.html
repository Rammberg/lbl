Asheville's Koss Snaps Up Player of the Week
Monday, May 4th, 2020
It was an extraordinary week for Christian Koss of the Asheville Tourists. First he goes on a hot hitting streak, batting .467, then he is voted the Player of the Week in the South Atlantic League. Last week Koss went 14-for-30, racked up 5 home runs and 8 RBIs and scored 10 runs. It was a heck of a week for the Asheville second baseman.

This season Koss has 5 home runs and a .323 batting average.
