Red-Hot Rampage Reaps SAL Reward
Monday, May 25th, 2020
Brandon Parker is respected by most Rome Braves fans for his no-frills style of play. The 20-year-old left fielder went about his business with vigor last week to grab the South Atlantic League Player of the Week honors.

He compiled a .455 average (10-for-22) with 1 home run, 5 RBIs and 4 runs scored.

For the season Parker has hit .259 with 3 home runs and 14 RBIs. He has played in 34 games and scored 16 times.
