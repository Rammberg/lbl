Season Over for Hutchison
Friday, May 15th, 2020
"Not what I wanted to hear," said Charleston's Rodney Hutchison to reporters this afternoon, as he revealed that the injury he sustained on 05/13/2020 would force him to miss the rest of the season. "The doctor made it clear," Hutchison said. "There's no chance for me -- none at all -- to come back this year. That's the last thing I wanted to hear." Hutchison has been out of action since he suffered radial nerve compression.
Asheville Loses Nikorak for Season
Friday, May 15th, 2020
Clearly dejected, Mike Nikorak left the Asheville clubhouse nearly in tears this afternoon. He acknowledged that his season is over after sustaining a damaged elbow ligament in a ballgame recently. It brings an early end to the Asheville starting pitcher's season.

For the season Nikorak has logged no wins and 2 losses and struck out 15 batters in 21.1 innings while compiling a 3.80 ERA.
