Mendoza Snags SAL Top Hurler Trophy
Friday, May 1st, 2020
There's no doubt that Crawdads starter Abdiel Mendoza earned his South Atlantic League Pitcher of the Month award. He left absolutely no room for doubt as he mowed his way through the opposition in April.

During the month Mendoza was 2-0 with a 1.15 ERA. He made 3 starts, tossed 15.2 innings and had 16 strikeouts.
Sosa Awarded April's SAL Top Bat Prize
Friday, May 1st, 2020
For a rookie just breaking in, Lenyn Sosa certainly put together a successful month. It was good enough to win the SAL Batter of the Month award for April.

The 20-year-old shortstop for the Kannapolis Cannon Ballers hit .393 with 4 home runs, 10 RBIs and 15 runs scored.
Pries Blisters Hickory
Wednesday, April 29th, 2020
Jake Pries really sizzled at the plate in today's 4-1 win over Crawdads. The Charleston left fielder blistered Hickory for 5 hits in 5 at-bats.

"Pries was too tough to get out today," the Crawdads skipper said in the postgame interview. "No matter what we threw him, he hit it. And he hit some good pitches, too."

Jake Pries singled in the 1st, hit a solo-shot off Ricky Vanasco in the 3rd, singled in the 5th, tripled in the 8th and singled in the 9th.

In his 2020 season totals, Pries has 1 home run, .550 BA, .591 OBP, 2 RBIs and 5 runs scored.
