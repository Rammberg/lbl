Red-Hot Rampage Reaps SAL Reward
Monday, June 22nd, 2020
South Atlantic League pitchers tried and tried all week long to stop Pedro Castellanos, but to no avail.

His eyes would light up when he came to the plate and he hit everything in sight, batting .393 for the week to win the SAL Player of the Week Award. The Greenville right fielder cracked 11 hits in 28 at-bats, 3 home runs, drove in 10 runs and scored 10 times.

This year Castellanos is hitting .302 with 8 home runs.
