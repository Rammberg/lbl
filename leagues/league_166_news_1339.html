Cowan Earns APP's Best Pitcher Honor
Saturday, August 1st, 2020
"The kid had it all working last month," a veteran teammate told reporters. "He keeps up those kind of numbers, we might have to start inviting him out with us after the games."

Cam Cowan put together a very solid month to take home the Appalachian League's top pitching honor in July.

The 21-year-old reliever threw like a veteran. In 11 relief appearances he raked in 8 saves and 15 strikeouts in 11.1 innings as he put together his 1-0 record and 1.59 ERA.

This season Cowan has notched 8 saves with 15 strikeouts and 4 walks in 11.1 innings while registering a 1.59 ERA and a 1-0 won-lost record.
