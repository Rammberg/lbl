AC Honors Healy, #1 Batter in June
Wednesday, July 1st, 2020
June is a month Ryon Healy will no doubt remember for quite some time to come.

The 28-year-old San Antonio first baseman came through all month long for his club and has won honors as the American Conference Batter of the Month.

His standout month saw him put up a .324 batting average, with 9 home runs, 31 RBIs, 18 runs scored and a .368 on-base percentage.

So far this season Healy has gotten 86 hits in 277 at-bats for a .310 batting average with 21 home runs. His stats include 68 RBIs and 52 runs scored. He has played in 68 games.
