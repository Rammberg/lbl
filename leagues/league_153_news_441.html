Reliever Snags NL Top Hurler Trophy
Monday, June 1st, 2020
Tony Watson has been around the league long enough to know that relievers are often overlooked. That has never stopped him from doing what he does best -- and he did it extremely well in May and stole away with the National League Pitcher of the Month award.

The Giants bullpen ace collected 6 saves and 12 strikeouts in 12.2 innings, holding the opposition to a .077 average and posting a 0.00 ERA and a 3-0 record.

This year Watson has 11 saves. In 17 relief appearances he has hurled 18 innings, fanned 17 and given up 1 walk with a 3-0 record. The San Francisco reliever has recorded a 1.50 ERA and allowed the opposition to hit .119 when he is in the game.
