France Crowned Top PC Hitter in May
Monday, June 1st, 2020
El Paso Chihuahuas fans may just have witnessed a star player blossoming before their eyes in May.

Ty France put up a remarkable month at the age of 25 and has been named the Pacific Conference Batter of the Month.

For the month, France hit .340 with 5 home runs, 18 RBIs and 23 runs scored.

This season France is hitting .314 with 50 hits, 7 home runs, 23 RBIs, scored 29 runs and played in 43 games.
