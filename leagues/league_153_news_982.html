Miami Trades Berti for Washington's Tocci
Wednesday, July 8th, 2020
The Miami Marlins announced a deal with the Nationals today, sending 30-year-old minor league 3B Jon Berti to Washington for 26-year-old minor league LF Andrew Stevenson and 24-year-old minor league CF Carlos Tocci. It had been talked about for the past few weeks and everything came together late last night. All players are expected to be joining their respective clubs shortly.


Berti is hitting .237 in 2020 with 12 RBIs and 3 home runs.
