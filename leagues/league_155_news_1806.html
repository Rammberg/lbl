Sanchez Week's Top Player
Monday, August 31st, 2020
Yolmer Sanchez of the Sacramento River Cats was locked in all week as he bullied the pitchers he faced. Sanchez put up some great numbers and hauled in the Pacific Conference Player of the Week trophy.

He hit .478 with 1 home run and 3 RBIs. The 28-year-old third baseman also scored 3 runs and for the week went 11-for-23.

Looking at his present stats, Sanchez has compiled a .311 batting average in 114 games with a .371 on-base percentage, while hitting 5 home runs and driving in 44 runs.
