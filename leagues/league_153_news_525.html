Veteran Cespedes Hits Three Dingers
Friday, June 5th, 2020
Whatever Yoenis Cespedes ate for breakfast before his game against the Washington Nationals today, it gave him some impressive power.

The New York left fielder smacked three balls up and out of the park at Nationals Park. His home run barrage led the Mets to take the 25-6 victory over the Nationals.

"When they throw it in your wheelhouse, you've got a shot at a homer," he told BNN reporters after the game. "I got three shots today and nailed them all."

On his 3-homer day, he went 3-for-4, scored 3 times, drove in 8 and put on a display that history will remember.

Yoenis Cespedes hit a solo-shot off Stephen Strasburg in the 1st, hit a two-run home run off Sean Doolittle in the 3rd, walked in the 4th, walked in the 5th, hit a GRAND SLAM off Kevin Quackenbush in the 6th, grounded out in the 7th and walked in a run in the 8th.

Thus far this season in 50 games Cespedes is batting .209 with 9 home runs, 29 RBIs and 20 runs scored. He has 39 hits in 187 at-bats.
