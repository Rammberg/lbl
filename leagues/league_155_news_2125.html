Rainiers Cruise to Round 2 with Sweep
Tuesday, September 15th, 2020
The Tacoma Rainiers appear poised to make a serious run at the Round 2 after a convincing sweep over the Salt Lake Bees in the Round 1.

It's now on to the Round 2 for Tacoma, who won the final game 10-6 and took the series 3-0.

"Everyone's doing their part and things are falling into place," said Tacoma left fielder Kyle Lewis, who was named series MVP. "It feels like a special year."

For the series, Lewis hit .571 with a .625 on-base percentage. He had 2 home runs, drove in 9 RBI and scored 5 runs.

The Tacoma Rainiers will play against the San Antonio Missions in the next round. San Antonio won their series against the Memphis Redbirds 3 games to 0. The Round 2 schedule will be announced after the Round 1 finishes.
