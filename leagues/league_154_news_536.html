Castro Hit String Snapped at 20
Sunday, June 7th, 2020
Maybe it really is taboo to talk about a hitting streak before it's over. Hours after speaking to the press about his 20-game hitting streak, Toledo shortstop Willi Castro went hitless in 4 at-bats today at Fifth Third Field.

The sudden hitting drought came as the Mud Hens took the 2-0 win over the Pawtucket Red Sox.

"Nope, it's not magic," Castro said in his postgame press conference. "Nobody jinxed me. Streaks don't last forever. I've heard about it every day for weeks now. My roomie told me to get a hit before every game and wished me luck. I just ran out of hits."

This season Castro is batting .317 with 59 hits and 7 home runs. He has scored 30 times and has 30 runs batted in.
