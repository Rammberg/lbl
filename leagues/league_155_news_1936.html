Round Rock Loses Paredes for Season
Thursday, September 3rd, 2020
In a news release, the Round Rock Express announced that starting pitcher Enoli Paredes would miss the rest of the season due to a ruptured disc. Paredes sustained the injury on 09/02/2020. He "will be sorely missed," said his manager.

This season to date Paredes has a record of 2 wins and 4 losses with a 2.84 ERA, has appeared in 38 games and struck out 77 in 63.1 innings.
