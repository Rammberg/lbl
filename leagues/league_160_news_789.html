CAR Player of the Week Named
Monday, June 29th, 2020
It was a fantastic week for Down East third baseman Josh Jung and it was capped off today with the announcement that he was named the winner of the Carolina League Player of the Week Award.

Last week Jung hammered the ball at a torrid .524 pace, going 11-for-21 with 2 home runs, 4 RBIs and 6 runs scored.

This year Jung is hitting .272 with 67 hits, 8 home runs, 27 RBIs and has scored 25 runs.
