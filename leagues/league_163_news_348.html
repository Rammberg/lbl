Injury Brings Season to End for Barlow
Wednesday, May 20th, 2020
Team officials for the Hickory Crawdads confirmed that a stretched elbow ligament will force Joe Barlow to miss the rest of the season. Barlow sustained the injury on 05/17/2020. It remains to be seen who will replace Barlow on the active roster.

Looking at his pitching stats so far this season, Barlow has 9 relief appearances, pitched 6.1 innings and fashioned a 0-0 record with a 1.42 ERA. He has recorded 5 saves, struck out 11 and allowed opposing clubs to hit .150 when he is on the hill.
