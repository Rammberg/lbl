Pitcher Brings in NL Top Rookie Trophy
Tuesday, September 1st, 2020
Luis Patino has become a big San Diego fan favorite, especially during the last month when the pitching phenom racked up a 4-2 record, making him the easy choice for August's National League Rookie of the Month Award.

He notched 35 strikeouts in 34.2 innings as he compiled a 2.08 ERA and held opponents to a .160 batting average.

For the season Patino has logged 10 wins and 4 losses and struck out 125 batters in 118.2 innings while compiling a 2.20 ERA.
