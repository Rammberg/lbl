Pirates Stratton No-Hits Tigers
Wednesday, September 9th, 2020
Pittsburgh is abuzz in the wake of a masterful no-hitter by Chris Stratton.

The 30-year-old hurler was dominating against the Detroit Tigers, leading the Pirates to a 3-0 win at Comerica Park today.

"I could tell early on that it was going to be a long day for us," Detroit manager Ron Gardenhire said afterward. "He made us look real bad. He was that good. And still going strong at the end of the game."

Stratton had 9 strikeouts and gave up 2 walks en route to the no-hitter.

"Yeah, this was a special day for me," Stratton said. "I had my good stuff and good movement. Good location, too. Even when I made a mistake, I got away with it today."

This year Stratton has compiled a 9-11 record with a 5.48 ERA in 25 starts.
