Nick Schnell Selected August's Best Batter
Tuesday, September 1st, 2020
Nick Schnell is not used to being associated with Batter of the Month-caliber players, but today the Hudson Valley left fielder joined that select company when the New York-Penn League announced the results of August's voting.

Schnell totaled 36 hits, 15 walks, 4 home runs, 14 RBIs and 17 runs scored last month to put together a .360 batting average and .440 on-base percentage.

Schnell is currently batting .308 with 12 home runs, 48 RBIs and 46 runs scored. He has 82 hits in 266 at-bats.
