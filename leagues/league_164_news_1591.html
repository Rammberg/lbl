Perry Wins NYP Player of the Week Trophy
Monday, August 17th, 2020
Speak softly and carry a big stick and you could win the New York-Penn League Player of the Week Award just like Nathan Perry.

He really dazzled on the diamond this week with a .625 average to take home the trophy. The Tri-City catcher went 10-for-16, drove in 4 runs, scored 6 times and hit 3 home runs.

Perry on the season is batting .386 with 5 home runs. His totals include 11 RBIs and 12 runs scored. He has played in 16 games.
