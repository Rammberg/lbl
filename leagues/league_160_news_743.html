Biasi Dominates in 5-0 Win over Southern Division All-Stars
Tuesday, June 23rd, 2020
Dante Biasi said the annual all-star game is mostly for the fans to enjoy, but the players have a lot of fun too.

"It's a great time to get that much talent on one ball diamond," he said. "You feel fortunate to even be part of it."

Biasi wasn't just a part of the game, he was named the game MVP, as his Northern Division All-Stars won 5-0 over the Southern Division All-Stars.

Biasi went 1 for 1 in the game to earn the nod as the game's best.
