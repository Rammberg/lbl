Frankie Montas of the Oakland Athletics humiliated the Toronto Blue Jays at Oakland Coliseum today, spinning a complete game shutout and holding the visitors to 4 hits.

In 5 starts this year Montas has crafted a 2-3 record and a 4.71 ERA.
