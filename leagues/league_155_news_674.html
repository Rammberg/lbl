Rainiers Chill Hoerner's Hot Streak
Saturday, June 20th, 2020
The Iowa Cubs lost to the Tacoma Rainiers 7-4 today at Principal Park, but perhaps the bigger news was the end of Nico Hoerner's 21-game hitting streak. The second baseman went 0-for-3 on the day.

"We felt if we could slow down Hoerner, we would win the game," Rainiers manager Daren Brown explained. "They really seemed to feed off his streak, so we tried to put more emphasis on shutting him down."

Hoerner is batting .290 with 4 home runs and 34 RBIs this season.
