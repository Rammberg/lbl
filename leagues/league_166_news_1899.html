Spence August's Best Hurler in APP
Tuesday, September 1st, 2020
The Pitcher of the Month has been picked and the Appalachian League trophy went to Pulaski hurler Mitch Spence for his strong performance in August.

During the month, Spence was impressive with a 0.85 ERA and 4 wins and no losses. In 31.2 innings he struck out 22 and gave up 18 hits and 11 walks.

To this point in the season Spence has 6 wins and no losses and a 1.17 ERA.
