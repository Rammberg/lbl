McNamara Division Won: Renegades on Top
Thursday, September 3rd, 2020
Let loose, Hudson Valley fans, your team has won the McNamara Division.

The Hudson Valley Renegades have officially captured the crown, the 1st in franchise history.

"We've got a team that can do it all and we think do it just as well as anyone in the league," said left fielder Nick Schnell. "I hope no one is taking us lightly once the playoffs start."

Certainly the team's division crown will garner them a fair bit of respect, as the 45-26 record puts the club in position for the franchise's 1st New York-Penn League championship.

"You never get tired of being in the playoffs," said manager Rafael Valenzuela. "This is what you play for."
