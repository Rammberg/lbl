Tucker Rips Baltimore for 5 Hits
Friday, June 5th, 2020
5 times is the charm -- at least for Kyle Tucker.

The Houston Astros left fielder tore up the Baltimore Orioles to the tune of 5 hits in 6 at-bats at Oriole Park at Camden Yards today. The offensive onslaught from Tucker powered the Astros to a 14-10 win.

"Tucker was a tough out today," Baltimore manager Brandon Hyde said to the press in the postgame interview. "He hit some very good pitches with that quick bat of his. Not an easy out, that's for sure."

Kyle Tucker hit a solo-shot off Kyle Lloyd in the 1st, hit an RBI single in the 2nd, hit an RBI double in the 4th, singled in the 6th, grounded out in the 7th and hit a three-run home run off Jim Johnson in the 8th.

This season Tucker is batting .346 with 16 home runs.
