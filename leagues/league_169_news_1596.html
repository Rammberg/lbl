Star of the Week Awarded in PIO
Monday, August 17th, 2020
Sam Abbott of the Great Falls Voyagers cruised to Pioneer League Player of the Week honors after putting together one of the finest stretches in his career last week.

The first baseman batted .320 (8-for-25) with 2 home runs, 5 RBIs and 3 runs scored.

This season Abbott has hit at a .209 clip with 12 RBIs, 3 home runs and 5 runs scored.
