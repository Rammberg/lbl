Sweet Sweep for Pulaski
Sunday, September 6th, 2020
The Pulaski Yankees made it look easy today as they collected the 1st Round 1 championship in club history.

They swept the Elizabethton Twins in straight games, winning the series 3-0. The Yankees disposed of Elizabethton 4-3 in the series finale at Joe O'Brien Field.

"They were the better team this year, no doubt about it," said Twins manager Ray Smith.

During the regular season, Pulaski put up a 43-25 record and finished first in the East.
