2020 FSL South All-Stars Stars Shine
Tuesday, June 23rd, 2020
During the star-studded affair, the 2020 FSL South All-Stars defeated the FSL North All-Stars by a score of 4-0.

The fans in attendance at LECOM Park certainly enjoyed the show, seeing the best that the game had to offer.

Chief among the stars was Jordan Gore, who led the FSL South to victory.

"I will admit I was nervous out there. To share a field with such incredible players is always a humbling experience. But after the first couple pitches, you just have to ignore the talent out there and focus on your game," Gore commented as he accepted his award after the game.
