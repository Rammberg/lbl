Star CF Signs Extension with Houston
Saturday, July 11th, 2020
Houston fans celebrated as word of a signing became reality. George Springer won't be pulling up stakes anytime soon, as he has signed a 5-year extension at a pay rate of $24,480,000 per annum.

Since he is widely seen as an integral part of the club, Houston officials had to keep Springer from jumping ship.

This year Springer has hit at a .295 clip with 57 RBIs, 19 home runs and 82 runs scored.
