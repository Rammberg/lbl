AL Selects Heaney August's Top Starter
Tuesday, September 1st, 2020
Veteran Andrew Heaney put together quite a successful stretch in August for the Los Angeles Angels, earning himself the American League Pitcher of the Month award.

Heaney racked up a 3-1 record in 7 starts, posting a 1.38 ERA over 45.2 innings and striking out 58.

This season Heaney has crafted a 8-6 mark with a 3.28 ERA in 159.1 innings. Opposing clubs are batting .207 against him and he has 185 strikeouts.
