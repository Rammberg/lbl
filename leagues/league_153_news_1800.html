Great Week for Chicago's Yoan Moncada
Monday, August 31st, 2020
One of the league's most exciting young players, Yoan Moncada of the Chicago White Sox earned himself American League Player of the Week honors with a standout performance in last week's games.

For the week the 25-year-old third baseman had a lofty .467 average (14-for-30), collecting 4 home runs, 9 RBIs and 9 runs scored.

To date Moncada has chalked up a .283 average with 30 home runs, driven in 93 runs and scored 94 times.
