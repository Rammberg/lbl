Healy Week's Best in AC
Monday, June 8th, 2020
At the age of 28, Ryon Healy might have some potential he hasn't realized yet -- if his success at the plate last week is any indication. The San Antonio first baseman put up some exceptional numbers the past seven days with a .433 batting average. For his efforts Healy collected the American Conference Player of the Week Award.

He racked up 13 hits in 30 at-bats, 4 home runs and 9 RBIs last week in 7 games.

This season in 50 games, Healy has hit at a .322 clip with 64 hits, 16 home runs, 46 RBIs and 41 runs scored.
