Rickard Goes on Rampage Against Bees
Saturday, July 4th, 2020
Sacramento River Cats left fielder Joey Rickard went ballistic today with the bat. He hammered the Salt Lake Bees for 5 hits and drove Sacramento to a 10-9 win over Salt Lake at Sutter Health Park.

Joey Rickard singled in the 1st, hit a three-run double in the 2nd, singled in the 4th, grounded out in the 6th, doubled in the 8th and hit a two-run double in the 9th.

Rickard is hitting .306 for the season with 9 home runs, 46 RBIs and 46 runs scored. He has 79 hits in 258 at-bats and played in 68 games.
