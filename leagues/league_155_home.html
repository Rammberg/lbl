Shuck Pops 3 Out of the Park Against Bees
Wednesday, April 29th, 2020
JB Shuck was sending souvenirs to the sun gods all game long in the 10-7 Fresno Grizzlies triumph at Chukchansi Park today.

The 32-year-old put on a show for fans, launching not one, not two, but three home runs into the stands and powering the Grizzlies past the Salt Lake Bees.

"One homer's good, two's great, three's out of sight," Shuck told BNN reporters after the game. "I can't wait until next game. I really feel good at the plate."

The left fielder went 4-for-5, rung up 7 RBIs and scored 3 runs on his record-setting day.

JB Shuck hit an RBI single in the 2nd, hit a solo-shot off Dillon Peters in the 4th, hit a two-run home run off Dillon Peters in the 6th, struck out in the 7th and hit a three-run home run off Parker Markel in the 9th.

This season in 12 games Shuck is batting .356 with 4 home runs, 13 RBIs and has scored 10 times.
Perez Batters Round Rock for 5 Hits
Monday, April 27th, 2020
5 hits in a game is a rare thing, but Iowa's Hernan Perez made it happen today against the Express. Everything came together at the plate for the 29-year-old third baseman as he collected 5 hits in 5 at-bats -- and led the Cubs to a 5-3 win at Dell Diamond.

"I'm just out there to try and help the team win," Perez said after the game, "I had some real good at-bats today. I made good, solid contact and luckily no one caught 'em."

Hernan Perez singled in the 1st, singled in the 4th, hit an RBI double in the 6th, hit an RBI single in the 7th and singled in the 9th.

Currently Perez is hitting .381 and has collected 1 home run, 6 RBIs and scored 6 times, while playing in 10 games.
