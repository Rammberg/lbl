Goudeau Best PC Starter
Friday, May 1st, 2020
Ashton Goudeau hasn't been around long enough to know that youngsters shouldn't be winning Pacific Conference Pitcher of the Month. But when you put up numbers like his, you're going to get some notice.

In April the Albuquerque starter earned a 2-0 mark in 3 starts. He threw 17 innings, struck out 15 and compiled a solid 2.12 ERA.
Pitcher of the Month Named in AC
Friday, May 1st, 2020
In April, Tyler Ivey tore through the American Conference like a seasoned veteran -- quite impressive for a player only 23 years old.

The young Round Rock starter posted a 3-0 mark and an ERA of 2.04 to earn the Pitcher of the Month award. In 17.2 innings he collected 9 strikeouts while limiting the opposition to a .177 average.
Pacific Conference Announces April's Best Batter
Friday, May 1st, 2020
Even though he is a newcomer to Pacific Coast League, Josh Fuentes put up some good numbers in April and earned the PC Batter of the Month award.

He was one of Albuquerque's offensive leaders with a .433 average in 9 games. He also racked up 3 home runs, 12 RBIs, and 9 runs scored.
