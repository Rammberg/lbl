Hannemann's Strong Week Earns PC Trophy
Monday, July 27th, 2020
At the age of 29, Jacob Hannemann might have some potential he hasn't realized yet -- if his success at the plate last week is any indication. The Las Vegas center fielder put up some exceptional numbers the past seven days with a .500 batting average. For his efforts Hannemann collected the Pacific Conference Player of the Week Award.

He racked up 10 hits in 20 at-bats, 4 home runs and 7 RBIs last week in 5 games.

For the year Hannemann has hit at a .316 clip with 22 RBIs, 5 home runs and 30 runs scored.
