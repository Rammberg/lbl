Week's Top Player: Albuquerque's Fuentes
Monday, July 13th, 2020
Josh Fuentes burst onto the Pacific Conference stage in a big way last week. This promising rookie third baseman for Albuquerque garnered everyone's respect by putting up some remarkable numbers and picking up the Player of the Week trophy.

The 27-year-old haunted opposing pitchers hitting .467 (14-for-30) with 2 home runs and 9 RBIs last week.

This season in 28 games Fuentes is batting .390 with 6 home runs, 26 RBIs and has scored 23 times.
Morrison Collects Player of the Week Award
Monday, July 13th, 2020
Logan Morrison made quite a splash in the American Conference this past week. The San Antonio Missions first baseman drilled opposing pitchers for 7 hits in 18 at-bats to roll up a .389 batting average and the American Conference Player of the Week Award.

He also contributed 4 home runs, 10 RBIs and 5 runs scored to his club's offensive attack.

So far this season Morrison is hitting .293 with 16 home runs, 53 RBIs and 38 runs scored. He has 61 hits in 208 at-bats and a .391 OBP.
