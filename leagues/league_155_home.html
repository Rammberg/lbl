5-Hit Game for Cribbs Jr.
Tuesday, May 5th, 2020
Fans at Greater Nevada Field got to see not only a home-team victory today, but also one of the most impressive feats of hitting of the season.

The Reno Aces outplayed the Tacoma Rainiers en route to a 5-2 win, but the standout was shortstop Galli Cribbs Jr.. Cribbs Jr. netted 5 hits in 5 at-bats and finished with a dynamite game line.

"Hey, we tried everything we could think of to get him out," Rainiers manager Daren Brown said in his postgame comments. "We threw everything but the kitchen sink against him and he hit it. Maybe we should've thrown the sink, too."

Galli Cribbs Jr. singled in the 1st, doubled in the 3rd, singled in the 5th, singled in the 7th and hit an RBI double in the 8th.

In 17 games this season Cribbs Jr. is batting .328 with 22 hits and 1 home run. He has scored 12 runs and driven in 6.
Injury Derails Season for Jackson
Tuesday, May 5th, 2020
Drew Jackson had to wait several agonizing days to find out his diagnosis after suffering a torn posterior cruciate ligament on 05/03/2020. Today he received word from the Oklahoma City doctor that he would be out the rest of the year. Team officials called it a huge setback but something they would have to overcome.

This year Jackson is batting .163 with 7 hits, 1 home run, 5 RBIs, 8 walks, .327 OBP and has scored 2 runs.
