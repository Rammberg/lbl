Peters Takes PC #1 Pitcher Trophy
Monday, June 1st, 2020
Dillon Peters hasn't been around long enough to know that youngsters shouldn't be winning Pacific Conference Pitcher of the Month. But when you put up numbers like his, you're going to get some notice.

In May the Salt Lake starter earned a 4-0 mark in 6 starts. He threw 41.1 innings, struck out 24 and compiled a solid 3.05 ERA.

This year Peters has 5 wins and no losses and a 3.42 ERA.
Wilkerson Picks up AC Monthly Pitching Honor
Monday, June 1st, 2020
31-year-olds don't often go 5-0 in and haul away the best pitcher award, but Aaron Wilkerson did all that. Now, he's the toast of the American Conference. Today he was crowned the Pitcher of the Month for May.

In 6 starts he posted a 2.29 ERA and struck out 43 batters in 35.1 innings.

This year Wilkerson has worked 52.1 innings with 67 strikeouts en route to a 5-1 record and 2.58 ERA.
