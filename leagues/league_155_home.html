Wong Selected Top Player
Monday, May 25th, 2020
So far in his young career, Kean Wong has kept a low profile, but after his performance at the plate last week, he attracted the notice of the rest of the Pacific Conference in a big way as he was named the Player of the Week today for his efforts.

The 25-year-old Sacramento River Cats shortstop hit at a torrid .647 clip as he collected 11 hits in 17 at-bats, no home runs, 3 RBIs and 3 runs scored.

So far this season, Wong has recorded a .274 batting average with 34 hits, 2 home runs, 11 RBIs, 15 runs scored and a .328 OBP.
