Gerber Rockets 3 HR Against El Paso
Thursday, July 9th, 2020
Players don't hit three home runs in a game very often and when they do, it is something to celebrate. After today, Mike Gerber can celebrate.

The Albuquerque Isotopes center fielder powered three into the stands at Rio Grande Credit Union Field at Isotopes Park today against the El Paso Chihuahuas.

"It's rare that you actually try to hit a home run," Gerber told reporters after the game. "Most of the time you fail, if you really try to hit one. But today, I was really trying on that last one."

In all, he was 3-for-4 as the Isotopes toppled El Paso by a score of 10-2, knocking in 5 runs and scoring 3 times.

"He is blessed with a natural home run swing," Albuquerque manager Warren Schaeffer said. "He gets the ball up in the air."

Mike Gerber hit a two-run home run off Hunter Adkins in the 1st, grounded out in the 3rd, hit a solo-shot off Jimmy Yacabonis in the 5th and hit a two-run home run off Marco Estrada in the 7th.

Gerber is batting .291 with 13 home runs and 54 RBIs this season.
Dominguez Strokes 5 Hits
Monday, July 6th, 2020
5 hits in a game is a rare thing, but Iowa's Chris Dominguez made it happen today against the Storm Chasers. Everything came together at the plate for the 33-year-old third baseman as he collected 5 hits in 6 at-bats -- and led the Cubs to a 11-9 win at Werner Park.

"I'm just out there to try and help the team win," Dominguez said after the game, "I had some real good at-bats today. I made good, solid contact and luckily no one caught 'em."

Chris Dominguez flied out in the 2nd, hit an RBI double in the 4th, singled in the 6th, singled in the 9th, singled in the 10th and hit a two-run single in the 12th.

In 33 games this season Dominguez is batting .312 with 39 hits and 4 home runs. He has scored 15 runs and driven in 20.
