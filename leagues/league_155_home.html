Torres Announces Retirement
Thursday, June 4th, 2020
Carlos Torres made it official today: This season will be the last for the Nashville reliever.

Torres owns a 30-31 won-lost record with a 4.09 ERA for his career. In 362 games he has recorded 5 saves and the 37-year-old has 447 strikeouts in 506.2 innings of work.
Chihuahuas Get One to Win
Monday, June 1st, 2020
Jerad Eickhoff of the El Paso Chihuahuas humiliated the Albuquerque Isotopes at Southwest University park today, spinning a complete game shutout and holding the visitors to 3 hits.

"He stayed aggressive and his ball was really moving," said Albuquerque manager Warren Schaeffer, after the game.

Eickhoff whiffed 4 and walked 0 in the 1-0 win.

"If I make good pitches, I'm going to get some outs," Eickhoff said. "I was making good pitches the entire game."

In 9 starts this season Eickhoff has rung up a 4-3 record and a 2.95 ERA.
