Rickard PC's Best of the Week
Monday, July 6th, 2020
With some great hand-eye coordination and a sweet, compact swing, Sacramento River Cats left fielder Joey Rickard took the Pacific Conference by storm this past week to take the latest Player of the Week honors.

"The ball was looking like a beach ball all week," a happy Rickard said to BNN reporters. "When it's that big, it doesn't matter where they throw it -- I can hit it anywhere."

The 29-year-old left fielder hit .500 (10-for-20) last week with no home runs, 8 RBIs and 3 runs scored.

So far this season Rickard is batting .307 with 9 home runs, 46 RBIs and 47 runs scored. He has 80 hits in 261 at-bats, 30 walks and a .386 OBP. Rickard has played in 69 games.
