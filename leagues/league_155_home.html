No Comeback This Season for Shipley
Thursday, May 28th, 2020
The news was not unexpected, but that didn't make it easier to accept for the Omaha Storm Chasers: starting pitcher Braden Shipley is out for the year. Shipley suffered a sore shoulder on 05/24/2020. The team knew that the chances of seeing him return were slim to none. But today it was made official.

This year Shipley has compiled a 3-4 record with a 4.37 ERA.
