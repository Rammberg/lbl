Sacramento's Dubon Gets PC Player of the Week
Monday, June 15th, 2020
So far in his young career, Mauricio Dubon has kept a low profile, but after his performance at the plate last week, he attracted the notice of the rest of the Pacific Conference in a big way as he was named the Player of the Week today for his efforts.

The 25-year-old Sacramento River Cats second baseman hit at a torrid .500 clip as he collected 14 hits in 28 at-bats, 2 home runs, 9 RBIs and 5 runs scored.

Presently Dubon is batting .317 with 6 home runs and 30 RBIs. He has played in 53 games and scored 20 times.
