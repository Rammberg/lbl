Bad Behavior Costs Robertson and Law
Monday, May 18th, 2020
Kramer Robertson of Memphis and Derek Law of Nashville were the main participants in a recent bench-clearing brawl and today the league handed out its punishment. Robertson will have to miss 4 games for his part in the brawl, while Law was given 8 games. League officials announced their suspensions today.

Robertson on the season is hitting .263 with 1 home run. His stats include 5 RBIs and 22 runs scored.

Law has taken the mound for 13 relief appearances this season, nailing down no saves while notching a 0-1 record with a 9.26 ERA. The Nashville reliever has limited opposing batters to a .421 batting average.
