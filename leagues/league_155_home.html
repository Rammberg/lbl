Tacoma Secures Top Spot in Pacific Conference North Division
Monday, September 7th, 2020
Let loose, Tacoma fans, your team has won the Pacific Conference North Division.

The Tacoma Rainiers have officially captured the crown, the 1st in franchise history.

"We've got a team that can do it all and we think do it just as well as anyone in the league," said reliever Manny Banuelos. "I hope no one is taking us lightly once the playoffs start."

Certainly the team's division crown will garner them a fair bit of respect, as the 78-58 record puts the club in position for the franchise's 1st Pacific Coast League championship.

"You never get tired of being in the playoffs," said manager Daren Brown. "This is what you play for."
