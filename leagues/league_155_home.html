Rainiers Cruise to Round 2 with Sweep
Tuesday, September 15th, 2020
The Tacoma Rainiers appear poised to make a serious run at the Round 2 after a convincing sweep over the Salt Lake Bees in the Round 1.

It's now on to the Round 2 for Tacoma, who won the final game 10-6 and took the series 3-0.

"Everyone's doing their part and things are falling into place," said Tacoma left fielder Kyle Lewis, who was named series MVP. "It feels like a special year."

For the series, Lewis hit .571 with a .625 on-base percentage. He had 2 home runs, drove in 9 RBI and scored 5 runs.

The Tacoma Rainiers will play against the San Antonio Missions in the next round. San Antonio won their series against the Memphis Redbirds 3 games to 0. The Round 2 schedule will be announced after the Round 1 finishes.
Missions Dominate, Sweep Round 1
Tuesday, September 15th, 2020
The San Antonio Missions have swept the Memphis Redbirds and are on to the Round 2. San Antonio won 7-4 today at AutoZone Park to clinch the Round 1.

"We're happy to have put the dagger in when we had the chance," said San Antonio first baseman Logan Morrison, who was named MVP for the series. "It could have been a tough series to finish if we let them start winning."

Morrison won the award with a .500 batting average and a .643 on-base percentage. He had 4 home runs, drove in 7 RBI and scored 5 runs.

The San Antonio Missions don't know who they will face in the next round. They will play the winner of the Salt Lake Bees - Tacoma Rainiers series. Tacoma leads the series 2 games to 0. The Round 2 schedule will be announced after the Round 1 finishes.
