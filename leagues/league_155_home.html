No Comeback This Season for Pazos
Tuesday, July 21st, 2020
The Albuquerque Isotopes have made it official: James Pazos is headed to the injured list. The 29-year-old closer has been out since 07/19/2020 with a torn rotator cuff. He is expected to miss 14 months.

This season Pazos has 1 save. In 11 relief appearances he has tossed 12.2 innings, struck out 18 and issued no walks with a 3-1 record. The Albuquerque reliever has recorded a 0.71 ERA and allowed the opposition to hit .186 when he is in the game.
