Lency Delgado is July's #1 Hitter in PIO
Saturday, August 1st, 2020
Great Falls Voyagers fans may just have witnessed a star player blossoming before their eyes in July.

Lency Delgado put up a remarkable month at the age of 21 and has been named the Pioneer League Batter of the Month.

For the month, Delgado hit .368 with 4 home runs, 28 RBIs and 20 runs scored.

This year Delgado is hitting .338 with 6 home runs and 32 RBIs to his credit. He has gotten on base at a .401 clip and scored 25 runs.
