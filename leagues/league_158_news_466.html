TEX's #1 Batter Announced
Monday, June 1st, 2020
It's perhaps an award he'll win many more times through his career, as at age 24 Donovan Casey has a lot of baseball ahead of him.

But for now the young Tulsa Drillers star can enjoy the numbers he put up in May, which have garnered him the nod as the Texas League Batter of the Month.

His breakout month featured a .317 average with 7 home runs, 20 RBIs and 14 runs scored.

A look at his season totals shows Casey with 11 home runs, .290 BA, .346 OBP, 25 RBIs and 20 runs scored.
