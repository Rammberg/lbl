Central Division Title Goes to Brewers Gold
Saturday, August 22nd, 2020
Let loose, Milwaukee (AZL) fans, your team has won the Central Division.

The Milwaukee (AZL) Brewers Gold have officially captured the crown, the 1st in franchise history.

"We've got a team that can do it all and we think do it just as well as anyone in the league," said closer Billy McKay. "I hope no one is taking us lightly once the playoffs start."

Certainly the team's division crown will garner them a fair bit of respect, as the 35-17 record puts the club in position for the franchise's 1st Arizona Rookie League championship.

"You never get tired of being in the playoffs," said manager Nick Stanley. "This is what you play for."
