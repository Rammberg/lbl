Fernandez and Tamarez Fight, Get Suspended
Saturday, June 20th, 2020
Josue Fernandez of the Tampa Bay (DSL) Rays 1 and Misael Tamarez of the Houston (DSL) Astros will be on the sidelines for a few days. They have been suspended for 5 and 6 games respectively by the league office for causing the bench-emptying free-for-all at the recent Rays 1-Astros game at Tampa Bay Rays Complex.

Neither player had any comment about the suspensions. Both clubs told BNN Fernandez and Tamarez will begin serving them soon.

This season Fernandez has hit at a .375 clip with 14 RBIs, 2 home runs and 11 runs scored.
