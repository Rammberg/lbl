Drew Ellis Wins MVP in South Division Victory over North Division
Tuesday, June 23rd, 2020
Blue Wahoos Stadium was the place to be for baseball fans tonight, as the best SL players gathered for the annual all-star game.

On the field, the South Division downed the North Division by a score of 4-3. Off the field, game MVP Drew Ellis said he thinks everyone had a good night.

"You looked into the stands and it seemed like everyone was having a fantastic time," said the 24-year-old, who went 2-for-2 in the game. "A game like this is good for the sport."

Lifetime Ellis is hitting .239 with 30 doubles, no triples, 26 home runs, 96 RBIs and 89 runs scored. He has appeared in 174 games to date and delivered 143 hits.
