Hudson Valley Starter Wins July's Pitcher Award
Saturday, August 1st, 2020
Last month was a very good one for Alan Strong of Hudson Valley. So good that he has been picked as the Pitcher of the Month in the New York-Penn League.

Strong throttled league hitters the entire month, completing July with an impressive 5-0 mark and a 1.38 ERA in 6 starts with 19 strikeouts in 32.2 innings.

This season Strong has fanned 25 in 41.1 innings and collected a 6-0 record with a 2.61 ERA.
