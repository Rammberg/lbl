Luzardo's Treatment Not Going Well, Suffers Setback
Wednesday, June 10th, 2020
The Oakland Athletics announced today that Jesus Luzardo will miss an additional 4 days after the starting pitcher suffered a setback in his rehabilitation from elbow tendinitis. While the setback is believed to be minor, the Athletics want Luzardo to take his time and fully recover before coming back.

This season Luzardo has compiled a 4-1 mark with a 3.62 ERA in 5 starts.
