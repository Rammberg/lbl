Southern League Names Top Player
Monday, May 11th, 2020
Ryan Jeffers of the Pensacola Blue Wahoos has just enjoyed one of the better weeks of his career. That fact was not lost on Southern League officials, who named him Player of the Week.

Jeffers logged a .529 batting average for the Pensacola Blue Wahoos, racking up 5 RBIs and 2 home runs.

This year Jeffers has socked 21 hits in 67 at-bats for a .313 average with 3 home runs. He is getting on base at a .383 pace and his totals also include 10 RBIs and 11 runs scored.
