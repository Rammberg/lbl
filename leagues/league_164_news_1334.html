NYP's Premier Batter Picked
Saturday, August 1st, 2020
Nick Schnell is not used to being associated with Batter of the Month-caliber players, but today the Hudson Valley left fielder joined that select company when the New York-Penn League announced the results of July's voting.

Schnell totaled 31 hits, 15 walks, 7 home runs, 28 RBIs and 21 runs scored last month to put together a .261 batting average and .341 on-base percentage.

For the season Schnell has produced 46 hits in 166 at-bats for a .277 average with 8 home runs and gotten on base at a .348 clip.
