Injury Brings Season to End for Black
Sunday, July 5th, 2020
The Milwaukee Brewers have lost the services of Ray Black for the rest of this season. He suffered a ruptured ulnar collateral ligament in a recent ballgame and the follow-up report from the medical staff is not good. Black is out for the year.

Here are his stats for this season: Black has chalked up 2 saves and fashioned a record of 5 wins and 3 losses with a 12.27 ERA. He has made 51 relief appearances, thrown 36.2 innings and struck out 45.
