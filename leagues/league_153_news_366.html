Week's Top Performer Honored by AL
Monday, May 25th, 2020
At the age of 31, Mark Canha might have some potential he hasn't realized yet -- if his success at the plate last week is any indication. The Oakland center fielder put up some exceptional numbers the past seven days with a .481 batting average. For his efforts Canha collected the American League Player of the Week Award.

He racked up 13 hits in 27 at-bats, 4 home runs and 16 RBIs last week in 7 games.

In 37 games this season Canha has compiled a .256 batting average with 33 hits and 6 home runs. He has also scored 25 times and knocked in 24 runs.
