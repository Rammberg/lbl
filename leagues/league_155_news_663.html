Hoerner Boosts Hit Streak to 20 Games
Thursday, June 18th, 2020
Nico Hoerner, second baseman for the Iowa Cubs, ran his consecutive-game hitting streak to 20 games with 1 hit in his club's 6-3 victory today over the Reno Aces at Principal Park today.

"Not the outcome we wanted," Reno manager Chris Cron said. "There's never a good time to lose, and it's never fun to be on the wrong end of somebody else's accomplishment."
Nico Hoerner hit an RBI double in the 1st, struck out in the 2nd, was hit by a pitch in the 4th and flied out in the 7th.

So far this year Hoerner is batting .295 with 4 home runs, 34 RBIs and 26 runs scored.
