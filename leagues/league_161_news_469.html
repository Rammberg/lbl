Mitchell Wins FSL's Best Bat Award
Monday, June 1st, 2020
Even though he is a newcomer to Florida State League, Cal Mitchell put up some good numbers in May and earned the FSL Batter of the Month award.

He was one of Bradenton's offensive leaders with a .257 average in 28 games. He also racked up 9 home runs, 17 RBIs, and 17 runs scored.

In 2020 Mitchell has hit at a .219 clip with 32 hits, 10 home runs, 23 RBIs and has scored 19 times.
