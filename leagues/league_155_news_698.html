Barrage of Hits Gets Knizner AC Award
Monday, June 22nd, 2020
Andrew Knizner demonstrated his potential to be a star in the game last week and today he took home the American Conference Player of the Week Award.

The Memphis catcher wreaked havoc on opposing pitchers last week, pounding them for a .526 average (10-for-19) with 1 home run and 10 RBIs on his way to claiming the honor.

This year Knizner is hitting at a .329 pace with 7 home runs and 42 RBIs. He has played in 47 games and scored 22 times.
