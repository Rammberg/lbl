Brewers Listless in 14-0 Loss to Rockies
Thursday, July 9th, 2020
The Colorado Rockies rolled to an easy 14-0 win over the Milwaukee Brewers at Miller Park today. Leading the way to victory was starter Antonio Senzatela, who shackled the Milwaukee Brewers and held them scoreless on a 7-hitter.

"This is what every pitcher wants to do when they go out there," Senzatela said in his postgame press conference.

Senzatela has made 16 starts so far this year and put up a 6-9 won-lost record with a 6.62 ERA. In 87 innings he has allowed 103 hits, walked 39 and struck out 88.
