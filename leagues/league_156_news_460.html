Paredes Gets EL Batter of the Month Trophy
Monday, June 1st, 2020
Erie SeaWolves fans believe they've got a talented young star in Isaac Paredes.

He did nothing to dissuade that notion in May, as he hit .363 with 2 home runs, 22 RBIs and 13 runs scored.

The end result was the third baseman being named the Eastern League Batter of the Month.

In 41 games Paredes is hitting .327 this season with 51 hits, 2 home runs, 26 RBIs, 17 walks, .408 OBP and has scored 17 runs.
