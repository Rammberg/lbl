Yankees Haskin APP Hitter of the Week
Monday, August 10th, 2020
Pulaski fans had a lot to cheer about this week as left fielder Hudson Haskin brought home the Appalachian League Player of the Week trophy.

During his week-long batting binge he reeled off a .519 batting average, 14 hits and 1 home run. Haskin also racked up 7 RBIs and scored 6 runs.

This year Haskin has hit at a .306 pace and produced 31 RBIs, 7 home runs and scored 27 runs.
