League Gives Chiu and Magnifico 3-Game Suspensions
Tuesday, May 5th, 2020
It was an intense fight, so Marcus Chiu and Damien Magnifico were both suspended today for 3 games for their part in a brawl which happened in a game between the Rancho Cucamonga Quakes and the Visalia Rawhide at LoanMart Field.

Chiu said, "Luckily nobody was hurt. I think we both overreacted to the situation."

So far this year Chiu is hitting .214 with 1 home run.

Magnifico has taken the mound for 8 relief appearances this season, nailing down 1 save while notching a 2-0 record with a 1.80 ERA. The Visalia reliever has limited opposing batters to a .143 batting average.
