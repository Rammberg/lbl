Veteran Upton Hits Three Dingers
Friday, August 14th, 2020
Whatever Justin Upton ate for breakfast before his game against the Houston Astros today, it gave him some impressive power.

The Los Angeles left fielder smacked three balls up and out of the park at Angel Stadium of Anaheim. His home run barrage led the Angels to take the 27-24 victory over the Astros.

"When they throw it in your wheelhouse, you've got a shot at a homer," he told BNN reporters after the game. "I got three shots today and nailed them all."

On his 3-homer day, he went 4-for-4, scored 5 times, drove in 8 and put on a display that history will remember.

Justin Upton hit a solo-shot off Jose Urquidy in the 2nd, hit an RBI double in the 4th, hit a three-run home run off Brad Peacock in the 5th, hit a two-run home run off Brett Conine in the 6th, walked in a run in the 7th, walked in the 8th and walked in the 9th.

This year Upton is hitting .259 with 30 home runs.
