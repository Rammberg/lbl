NL Awards Best Batter Prize to Muncy
Monday, August 17th, 2020
Max Muncy must have thought he died and went to heaven this week. First he went on a remarkable batting rampage, hammering the ball at a .400 clip, then the Los Angeles first baseman finds out he has been voted the league's top performer and gets the NL Player of the Week Award.

For the week Muncy batted .400 (10-for-25) with 5 home runs and 9 RBIs.

Muncy is currently batting .237 with 29 home runs and 78 RBIs.
