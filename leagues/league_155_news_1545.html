Rickard Sizzles -- Smokes Cubs for 5 Hits
Thursday, August 13th, 2020
It wasn't just another day at the ballpark for Joey Rickard today. Rickard went on a tear and cracked hit after hit off Iowa pitchers. When the dust settled on the diamond at Principal Park, he had a 5-for-7 line and a day he will long remember. And the Sacramento River Cats had a 10-8 victory over the Cubs.

"Yeah, it'll be something nice to tell the grandkids, but it won't get me a hit tomorrow," he joked in the postgame interview.

Joey Rickard singled in the 2nd, singled in the 4th, hit a solo-shot off Tyson Miller in the 5th, grounded out in the 6th, singled in the 8th, grounded out in the 10th and hit an RBI single in the 12th.

Thus far this season in 102 games Rickard is batting .290 with 11 home runs, 67 RBIs and 62 runs scored. He has 115 hits in 396 at-bats.
