Orioles Headed to the Playoffs
Monday, August 31st, 2020
It still remains to be finalized whether or not it will be a division title or a wild card berth, but the Baltimore (GCL) Orioles today officially captured a playoff spot.

"One goal down, several more to go," said left fielder Cole Cabrera.
Division Remains Up for Grabs, but Tampa Bay (GCL) in Postseason
Monday, August 31st, 2020
The Tampa Bay (GCL) Rays are headed for the playoffs.

The club officially locked down a postseason spot today with their 30-25 record, although it remains to be seen whether they will officially capture the South Division. Nonetheless, the players were overjoyed and celebrated excitedly upon learning of their clinch.

"This is why you play the game, to get a chance to win a Round 3," said reliever Noah Rennard.
Astros Win Tight East Division Race
Monday, August 31st, 2020
The Houston (GCL) Astros have won their division, but have a much larger goal in mind.

"No one will look back on this season and remember the East Division champions. People will remember the Round 3 champions," said manager Ricky Rivera. "That's what this is all about, but of course we needed this first to get there."

The club's division win is the 1st for the franchise and they will be seeking their 1st Round 3.
