GCL Reliever of the Year Winner Named
Saturday, September 26th, 2020
At the age of 21, Max Cioffi says he still has a lot to learn about life in Gulf Coast Rookie League.

But he certainly seems to be a quick study, as the Detroit E (GCL) Tigers East reliever was better than all others this year and has captured the 2020 Gulf Coast Rookie League Reliever of the Year.

"I hope to win this a few more times before all is said and done in my career," the Chicago, Illinois native said.

For the season, Cioffi had 7 saves in 20 relief appearances, which totaled 23.2. He fanned 22 betters, while walking 8 and put up a record of 1-0 to go with his 0.38 ERA.

He received 12 first place votes out of a possible 18. Blake Brown of the Philadelphia W (GCL) Phillies West finished second in voting with 6 first place votes, while Aaron Barkley of the New York N (GCL) Mets finished third.
