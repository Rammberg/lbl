Rodriguez Has Season End
Thursday, July 2nd, 2020
Moises Rodriguez has to call it a season. The Detroit E (GCL) Tigers East reliever learned today that the full extent his injury suffered on 06/29/2020 is worse than hoped. He will not play again this year according to club doctors, who diagnosed the injury as shoulder inflammation and stated that he would not be able to play for 5 months. The team will likely place Rodriguez on the injured list today.
