Week's Best Player: Pittsburgh (GCL)'s Siani
Monday, July 6th, 2020
So far in his young career, Sammy Siani has kept a low profile, but after his performance at the plate last week, he attracted the notice of the rest of the Gulf Coast Rookie League in a big way as he was named the Player of the Week today for his efforts.

The 19-year-old Pittsburgh (GCL) Pirates center fielder hit at a torrid .462 clip as he collected 12 hits in 26 at-bats, 2 home runs, 6 RBIs and 4 runs scored.
