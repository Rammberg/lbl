NL Honors Chicago Shortstop
Monday, May 4th, 2020
At the age of 27, Javier Baez might have some potential he hasn't realized yet -- if his success at the plate last week is any indication. The Chicago shortstop put up some exceptional numbers the past seven days with a .412 batting average. For his efforts Baez collected the National League Player of the Week Award.

He racked up 14 hits in 34 at-bats, 5 home runs and 20 RBIs last week in 8 games.

Baez is swinging at a .342 pace on the year with 6 home runs, 24 RBIs and 22 runs scored. He has played in 18 games.
