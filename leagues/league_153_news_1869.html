August's Top Rookie Named by AL
Tuesday, September 1st, 2020
Matt Manning is beginning to make a name for himself and he did it by posting a 4-1 record and a 2.32 ERA in August. For his spectacular mound performance last month he has been named BNN's Rookie of the Month in the American League.

The 22-year-old newcomer isn't at all intimidated by his lack of experience, at least not outwardly. In fact, by the way he has pitched beyond his years, you could easily mistake him for a veteran.

In 5 starts for the Tigers Manning has struck out 27 batters in 31 innings, holding opposing hitters to a batting average of .234 during his hot streak.

This year Manning has fashioned a 10-8 mark, 4.14 ERA and pitched 132.2 innings. Opposing clubs are batting .249 against him and he has logged 122 strikeouts.
