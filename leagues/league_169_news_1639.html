Rowdy Players Suspended by League
Monday, August 17th, 2020
Yesterday at Dehler Park Caleb Van Blake and Sam Fuller got into each other's space and then into each other's face. They began to grapple and fell to the ground. That's when it boiled over into a full-fledged bench-clearing tussle between the Billings Mustangs and Orem Owlz. Reason finally prevailed as the umpires restored order. After much discussion, only the two major combatants were ejected from the game.

Today the league office notified the ballclubs that Van Blake will have to sit out 2 while Fuller would be given a 6-game suspension for their roles in the brawl.

Van Blake is hitting .250 this year with no home runs, 1 RBIs and 1 run scored. He has 6 hits in 24 at-bats, 3 walks and a .357 on-base percentage. Van Blake has played in 8 games.

For the season Fuller has logged 2 wins and 1 loss and struck out 22 batters in 25 innings while compiling a 3.24 ERA.
