Hit Parade: Polanco Pops 5 Hits
Saturday, May 30th, 2020
Today at Guaranteed Rate Field, Jorge Polanco put on an impressive display.

The Minnesota Twins shortstop tagged Chicago pitching for 5 hits in 5 at-bats, but his teammates were unable to capitalize on the spree and lost the game to the Chicago White Sox by the score of 18-16.

"The loss takes some of the joy out of it, but I'll take it," Polanco said. "5-for-5 ain't bad in any league."

Despite the terrific performance, the Twins manager was unwilling to offer his shortstop much praise, saying he holds his "whole roster" accountable for losses.

"Jorge told me 'Coach, I had 5 hits' and I said 'Well that's great, but it looks like we needed one more from you,'" manager Rocco Baldelli said.

Jorge Polanco singled in the 2nd, singled in the 3rd, walked in the 4th, singled in the 5th, hit an RBI triple in the 6th and singled in the 8th.

Polanco is presently batting .340 with 4 home runs, 31 runs scored and 17 RBIs. His OBP is .386.
