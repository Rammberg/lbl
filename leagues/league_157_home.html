SL's Top Player Named
Monday, July 20th, 2020
So far in his young career, Brandon Marsh has kept a low profile, but after his performance at the plate last week, he attracted the notice of the rest of the Southern League in a big way as he was named the Player of the Week today for his efforts.

The 22-year-old Rocket City Trash Pandas center fielder hit at a torrid .542 clip as he collected 13 hits in 24 at-bats, 2 home runs, 6 RBIs and 3 runs scored.

This season Marsh has hit at a .275 clip with 40 RBIs, 5 home runs and 44 runs scored. He has played in 84 games.
