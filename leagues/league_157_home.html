Querecuto on the Attack, Player of the Week
Monday, August 10th, 2020
A hot-hitting week got Juniel Querecuto the Southern League Player of the Week trophy. He really wore out his welcome with league pitchers.

The Jackson third baseman slammed them for a .522 average, 12 hits in 23 at-bats, 2 home runs, 9 RBIs and scored 4 runs.

In the postgame press conference, Querecuto shared his hitting philosophy with the BNN scribes: "Just look for the seams and hit between them... nothin' more than that."

Querecuto is hitting at a .276 mark this season. His stats show 3 home runs, 22 RBIs and 22 runs scored. He has played 43 games for the Generals.
