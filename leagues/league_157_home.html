This Week's Star Player is Adolfo
Monday, July 13th, 2020
23-year-old Micker Adolfo made a few Birmingham headlines during this past week as the rookie right fielder proved he could compete at the plate with his high-school idols. He captured the attention of fans, journalists and Southern League officials last week and today took home Player of the Week honors for his effort.

He cracked 10 hits in 26 at-bats to put up a .385 average. Adolfo collected 2 home runs, 6 RBIs and scored 6 runs.

Adolfo on the season is hitting .229 with 12 home runs. His stats include 38 RBIs and 34 runs scored.
