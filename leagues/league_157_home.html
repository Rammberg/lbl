Injury Sidelines Brady for Season
Friday, May 15th, 2020
The season will end prematurely for Denny Brady, the Rocket City starting pitcher. He suffered a partially torn ulnar collateral ligament on 05/11/2020, an injury that is serious enough to knock him out of action for the rest of the year. Brady was not available for comment.

This year Brady has compiled a 1-2 mark with a 2.88 ERA.
Tolbert Belts 5 Hits
Wednesday, May 13th, 2020
It's not something you read about in the sports pages every day -- a 5-for-7 day. For many, it might come around only once in a career -- and for L.T. Tolbert, it came around today.

The 23-year-old first baseman scattered hits all over AT&T Field to pace the Jackson Generals to a 7-5 victory over the Chattanooga Lookouts.

Chattanooga skipper Ricky Gutierrez told reporters in the press room that "Tolbert deserves a hat-tip."

"He hit some good pitches against us," he said. "In fact, according to our scouting reports, Tolbert hit several that he is not supposed to be able to hit."

L.T. Tolbert hit an RBI double in the 1st, hit an RBI single in the 3rd, hit a two-run double in the 5th, struck out in the 7th, walked in the 9th, struck out in the 12th, singled in the 14th and hit a two-run double in the 15th.

This year Tolbert has hit at a .316 pace and produced 15 RBIs, 1 home run and scored 14 runs.
