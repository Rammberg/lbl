Blue Wahoos Vasquez Named SL's Best Pitcher
Friday, May 1st, 2020
Any time a rookie makes a splash on the scene, it's got to be great for his career potential. Just ask Andrew Vasquez, who put together a stellar month out of the pen. The Pensacola Blue Wahoos closer, 26, kept the opposition off balance all month as he held them to a .000 average and walked away with the Southern League Pitcher of the Month award for April.

Vasquez posted a 2-0 mark and a 0.00 ERA in 7 relief appearances. In 6.1 innings, he had 4 saves, 10 strikeouts, and 5 walks.
Biloxi Right Fielder SL's Best Batter in April
Friday, May 1st, 2020
Alexander Palma put together a good April to earn the Southern League Batter of the Month prize. A virtual unknown, he made the most of his chance to prove to Biloxi officials that he belongs on the big league roster.

The Shuckers right fielder posted a .316 batting average with 4 home runs, 15 RBIs and 10 runs scored.
Injury Shuts Down Henzman for Season
Thursday, April 30th, 2020
The Birmingham Barons have lost the services of Lincoln Henzman for the rest of this season. He suffered a stretched elbow ligament in a recent ballgame and the follow-up report from the medical staff is not good. Henzman is out for the year.

This year Henzman has made 5 relief appearances with no saves. In 7.1 innings, he has permitted 7 hits, fanned 8 hitters and recorded no wins and no losses with a 3.68 ERA.
