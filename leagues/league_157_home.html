Uelmen Nabs SL #1 Pitcher Award
Wednesday, July 1st, 2020
In June, Erich Uelmen tore through the Southern League like a seasoned veteran -- quite impressive for a player only 24 years old.

The young Tennessee starter posted a 4-0 mark and an ERA of 1.50 to earn the Pitcher of the Month award. In 24 innings he collected 14 strikeouts while limiting the opposition to a .209 average.

This year Uelmen has worked 69 innings with 43 strikeouts en route to a 5-1 record and a 1.70 ERA.
Southern League Selects June's Best Batter
Wednesday, July 1st, 2020
Zack Short may be much younger than other hitters in the Southern League, but he performed like a seasoned veteran in June to win the Batter of the Month award.

The 25-year-old Tennessee shortstop hit .351 with 6 home runs, 22 RBIs and 19 runs scored.

Currently Short is batting .351 with 27 hits and 6 home runs. He has knocked in 22 runs and scored 19 times.
