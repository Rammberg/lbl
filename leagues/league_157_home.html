Palma Takes This Week's Trophy
Monday, May 25th, 2020
Alexander Palma demonstrated his potential to be a star in the game last week and today he took home the Southern League Player of the Week Award.

The Biloxi right fielder wreaked havoc on opposing pitchers last week, pounding them for a .522 average (12-for-23) with 2 home runs and 6 RBIs on his way to claiming the honor.

So far in 2020 Palma is batting .326 with 6 home runs, 23 RBIs and 19 runs scored. He has 44 hits in 135 at-bats and a .395 OBP.
