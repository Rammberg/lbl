Brujan Wins SL Player of the Week Prize
Monday, August 17th, 2020
Vidal Brujan demonstrated his potential to be a star in the game last week and today he took home the Southern League Player of the Week Award.

The Montgomery second baseman wreaked havoc on opposing pitchers last week, pounding them for a .455 average (10-for-22) with 2 home runs and 4 RBIs on his way to claiming the honor.

On the year Brujan is hitting .325 with 136 hits, 9 home runs, 74 RBIs, .393 OBP and has scored 60 runs.
