SL Names Top Player of the Week
Monday, June 8th, 2020
One of the league's most exciting young players, Drew Waters of the Mississippi Braves earned himself Southern League Player of the Week honors with a standout performance in last week's games.

For the week the 21-year-old center fielder had a lofty .500 average (12-for-24), collecting 2 home runs, 6 RBIs and 5 runs scored.

Waters is hitting .271 so far this season with 20 RBIs and 23 runs scored. He has 51 hits including 7 home runs, 18 walks and .341 OBP.
