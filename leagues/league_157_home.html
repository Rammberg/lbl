Biloxi Loses Leal for Season
Monday, July 27th, 2020
Erick Leal had hoped for the best, but expected the worst. Today, he learned from the Biloxi medical staff that he would miss the rest of the season. The Biloxi reliever suffered a torn labrum in an earlier ballgame. He admitted to being discouraged, but said he plans to come back strong next year.

Leal has no saves this season with a won-lost record of 2-1 and 3.16 ERA.
