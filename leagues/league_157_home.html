Injury Brings Season to End for Reed
Tuesday, May 26th, 2020
In a news release, the Pensacola Blue Wahoos announced that reliever Jake Reed would miss the rest of the season due to a fractured elbow. Reed sustained the injury on 05/25/2020. He "will be sorely missed," said his manager.

This year Reed has collected 2 saves in 15 relief appearances. He has also compiled a 0-2 won-lost mark with a 3.44 ERA and fanned 21 batters in 18.1 innings. Opponents are hitting him at a .268 clip in 2020.
