Season Over for Jay
Monday, July 6th, 2020
The Chattanooga Lookouts have made it official: Tyler Jay is headed to the injured list. The 26-year-old reliever has been out since 07/05/2020 with ulnar nerve entrapment. He is expected to miss 4-5 months.

This season he has made 25 relief appearances, collecting no saves. Jay has worked 32 innings, yielding 24 hits and giving out 10 free passes while striking out 37. He has a 2-2 record with a 3.94 ERA.
