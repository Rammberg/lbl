Smokies Cruise to Round 2 with Sweep
Saturday, September 12th, 2020
The Tennessee Smokies have swept the Jackson Generals and are on to the Round 2. Tennessee won 6-5 today at Smokies Stadium to clinch the Round 1.

"We're happy to have put the dagger in when we had the chance," said Tennessee catcher Jordan Procyshen, who was named MVP for the series. "It could have been a tough series to finish if we let them start winning."

Procyshen won the award with a .429 batting average and a .429 on-base percentage. He had 1 home runs, drove in 3 RBI and scored 4 runs.

The Tennessee Smokies don't know who they will face in the next round. They will play the winner of the Montgomery Biscuits - Biloxi Shuckers series. Montgomery leads the series 2 games to 1. The Round 2 schedule will be announced after the Round 1 finishes.
