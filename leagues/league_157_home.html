Hernandez Caps Great Week
Monday, August 24th, 2020
One of the league's most exciting young players, Ronaldo Hernandez of the Montgomery Biscuits earned himself Southern League Player of the Week honors with a standout performance in last week's games.

For the week the 22-year-old catcher had a lofty .533 average (8-for-15), collecting 2 home runs, 6 RBIs and 4 runs scored.

For the season Hernandez has produced 45 hits in 133 at-bats for a .338 average with 14 home runs and gotten on base at a .395 clip.
