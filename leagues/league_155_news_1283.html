On a Roll: Mundell Hits for Cycle
Tuesday, July 28th, 2020
Albuquerque's Brian Mundell accomplished what few others have done -- he hit for the cycle, collecting a single, double, triple and home run in the same game.

For the day, Mundell finished 6-for-7 with 7 RBI and 4 runs scored and helped the Isotopes knock off the Las Vegas Aviators 23-4 at Las Vegas Ballpark.

"I didn't even know I was about to hit for the cycle until after," Mundell told reporters following the game. "One of the guys mentioned it when I got back to the dugout."

Brian Mundell grounded out in the 1st, hit a three-run home run off Paul Blackburn in the 3rd, singled in the 4th, hit a two-run double in the 5th, hit an RBI triple in the 7th, hit an RBI single in the 8th and singled in the 9th.

Mundell on the season is hitting .333 with 6 home runs. His stats include 27 RBIs and 36 runs scored.
