Memphis Redbirds Clinch First in American Conference North Division
Friday, September 4th, 2020
For a team that has never won a Round 2, capturing the American Conference North Division is a good start.

That's the situation the Memphis Redbirds find themselves in as their division title today became official.

"We might not have a lot of championship experience as a franchise, but when push comes to shove, I believe in these guys," said reliever Jake Woodford. "We want to be that team to bring the title home to Memphis."

Team manager Ben Johnson said it won't be easy to capture the Round 2, but noted that "nothing worth doing ever is."
