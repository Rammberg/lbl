Oxnevad Earns May's Best Pitcher Honor
Monday, June 1st, 2020
Over the past month Ian Oxnevad has turned quite a few heads. He's young, talented and able to pitch with the established arms in the Florida State League. He proved all that and now he's been named May's Pitcher of the Month.

The rookie for the Palm Beach Cardinals posted 5-0 mark and a 0.88 ERA. In 2 starts he tossed 30.2 innings, permitted 25 hits and fanned 18 batters.

To date this season Oxnevad sports a 6-1 mark with a 1.99 ERA in 2 starting assignments. He has held the opposition to a .253 batting average and has 27 strikeouts in 45.1 innings.
