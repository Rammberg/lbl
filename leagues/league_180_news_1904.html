Leonard Brings Home DSL Batter of Month Honor
Tuesday, September 1st, 2020
A hot-hitting Eddys Leonard punished DSL pitchers in August, hitting .442 in 14 games while racking up 1 home run, 14 RBIs and 13 runs scored.

With those numbers it came as no surprise that Los Angeles (DSL)'s 19-year-old shortstop earned Dominican Rookie League Batter of the Month honors.

This season Leonard is hitting .347 with 68 hits, 4 home runs, 42 RBIs, scored 38 runs and played in 58 games.
