Marrero Sizzles -- Smokes Brewers for 5 Hits
Monday, May 25th, 2020
Deven Marrero put on a hitting clinic today as the Philadelphia Phillies dumped the Milwaukee Brewers 26-23 at Citizens Bank Park.

The Philadelphia third baseman made Milwaukee defenders earn their pay, lashing Milwaukee pitching for 5 hits in 5 at-bats in the game. He told BNN reporters after the game that his strategy was "just hit it where they pitched it."

"Too often I try too hard to pull the ball," Marrero said in his postgame press conference. "Today, I just went with the pitch."

Deven Marrero singled in the 2nd, singled in the 3rd, singled in the 5th, hit a two-run single in the 5th and doubled in the 6th.

This year Marrero has hit at a .324 clip with 35 hits, 2 home runs, 18 RBIs and 14 runs scored.
