AL Player of the Week Awarded
Monday, June 8th, 2020
Willie Calhoun demonstrated his potential to be a star in the game last week and today he took home the American League Player of the Week Award.

The Texas left fielder wreaked havoc on opposing pitchers last week, pounding them for a .459 average (17-for-37) with 6 home runs and 16 RBIs on his way to claiming the honor.

For the season Calhoun has produced 50 hits in 153 at-bats for a .327 average with 8 home runs and gotten on base at a .395 clip.
