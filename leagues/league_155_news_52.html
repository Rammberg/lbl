League Punishes Pair for Fighting
Monday, April 20th, 2020
If there wasn't a history between Greg Bird of Nashville and Joe Wieland of Wichita, then there is one now. The incident between the two sparked a bench-clearing scrum with those two being the center of attention. Now they will miss 4 and 8 games respectively as a result of their actions. The league office announced their suspensions today.

"We're both really competitive," Bird said later, "and sometimes things go a little too far because of it."

Bird is hitting .400 this year with 1 home run, 2 RBIs and 7 runs scored. He has 6 hits in 15 at-bats, 5 walks and a .591 on-base percentage. Bird has played in 5 games.
