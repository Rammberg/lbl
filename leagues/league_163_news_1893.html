August's Starter of the Month Goes to Crouse
Tuesday, September 1st, 2020
August's elite pitcher was Hans Crouse of Hickory. He silenced South Atlantic League hitters the entire month and claimed the Pitcher of the Month award with 5 wins and no losses in 5 starts.
