Hudson Valley Takes Round 1 in Deciding Game
Thursday, September 17th, 2020
The Hudson Valley Renegades and Brooklyn Cyclones didn't hold back anything in the Round 1, with each team pulling out all the stops in an effort to advance to the Round 2.

Hudson Valley won the final game by a score of 5-1 at Dutchess Stadium and that concluded the series, 3-2.

"Relief is the biggest thing," Hudson Valley left fielder Nick Schnell said when asked how he felt. "It's a grind both mentally and physically to have a long series like this, but our guys stuck with it and Brooklyn deserves credit for the hard fight."

Schnell paced the Hudson Valley offence and earned MVP honors. For the series, he hit .500 with a .636 on-base percentage, 0 home runs, 2 RBI and scored 3 runs.

The Hudson Valley Renegades will play against the Batavia Muckdogs in the next round. Batavia won their series against the Lowell Spinners 3 games to 1. The Round 2 schedule will be announced after the Round 1 finishes.
