Mario Garcia Hurls No-No Against State College
Monday, August 10th, 2020
It was a fantastic day for West Virginia Black Bears starter Mario Garcia and a lousy day for the State College Spikes.

The West Virginia hurler's pitching artistry completely befuddled and bewildered the Spikes, as the Black Bears cruised to a 5-0 win. When it was all over, Garcia was credited with one of the rarer feats in baseball -- a no-hitter.

"The ball was moving and darting, and best of all, going where I wanted it to go," Garcia said later.

He rang up 6 strikeouts and allowed 1 walk while shutting State College down at Monongalia County Ballpark.

This year Garcia has compiled a 1-6 record with a 3.58 ERA in 7 starts.
