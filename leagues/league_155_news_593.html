Andriese Shines in 4-0 Gem
Thursday, June 11th, 2020
Matt Andriese of the Salt Lake Bees humiliated the Omaha Storm Chasers at Smith's Ballpark today, spinning a complete game shutout and holding the visitors to 5 hits.

"He stayed aggressive and his ball was really moving," said Omaha manager Brian Poldberg, after the game.

Andriese whiffed 5 and walked 1 in the 4-0 win.

"If I make good pitches, I'm going to get some outs," Andriese said. "I was making good pitches the entire game."

In 10 starts this year Andriese has crafted a 4-3 record and a 3.30 ERA.
