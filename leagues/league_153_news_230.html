Tigers Torpedoed by Carrasco in 3-0 Loss to Cleveland
Friday, May 8th, 2020
Carlos Carrasco of the Cleveland Indians was masterful today, pitching a 3-hit shutout, 3-0, against the Detroit Tigers.

By going the distance, he was at the same time able to give his bullpen a rest, a fact appreciated by Cleveland manager Terry Francona.

"It's nice to have someone who can do that from time to time," he said. "He had command of every ball and every pitch. Great location, too."

Carrasco had 13 strikeouts and no walks in the outing.

To date this season Carrasco has compiled a 5-0 record with a 1.04 ERA in 5 starts.
