AL's #1 Batter Chosen

At only 23 years of age, Kyle Tucker could very well be having many more months like the last this in his career.

That would be great news for fans of the Houston Astros, as August was one to remember for the left fielder and he has captured the American League Batter of the Month award.

On the month he hit .404 with 12 home runs, 20 RBIs and 39 runs scored.

This year Tucker has hit at a .376 clip with 113 RBIs, 43 home runs and 154 runs scored.
