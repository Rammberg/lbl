Muckdogs Top Renegades
Friday, September 25th, 2020
It was a hard-fought battle for both teams to make it this far. But as the saying goes, "to the victors go the spoils." For the victorious Batavia Muckdogs, that meant the New York-Penn League Round 2 trophy.

The Muckdogs took a 2-0 victory over the Hudson Valley Renegades at Dutchess Stadium, winning their 1st championship after taking the series 3-2.

"We've got grit," said Muckdogs first baseman J.D. Osborne, who was named MVP of the series. "We know how to win when we have to."

The new titleholders completed the regular season with a first-place finish in the Pinckney Division and a 45-31 record.
