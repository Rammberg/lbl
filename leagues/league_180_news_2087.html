Martinez Wins Award for DSL Dominance
Monday, September 14th, 2020
At the young age of only 19, Justin Martinez has been named the Dominican Rookie League Reliever of the Year winner for 2020.

The future appears bright for the Arizona (DSL) Diamondbacks 1 relief ace, who captured the award with 6 saves in 14 relief appearances, 43 strikeouts in 45 innings and a record of 3-0 to go with his 0.60 ERA.

He received 46 first place votes, as a unanimous winner. Wilfry Urena of the Tampa Bay (DSL) Rays 2 finished second in voting, while Alex Martinez of the Los Angeles (DSL) Angels finished third.
