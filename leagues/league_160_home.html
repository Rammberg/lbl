Amaya on the Attack, Player of the Week
Monday, July 13th, 2020
So far in his young career, Miguel Amaya has kept a low profile, but after his performance at the plate last week, he attracted the notice of the rest of the Carolina League in a big way as he was named the Player of the Week today for his efforts.

The 21-year-old Myrtle Beach Pelicans catcher hit at a torrid .429 clip as he collected 9 hits in 21 at-bats, 3 home runs, 13 RBIs and 6 runs scored.

So far on the season Amaya is hitting .233 with 14 home runs, 46 RBIs and 33 runs scored. He has 54 hits and played in 70 games.
