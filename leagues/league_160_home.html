Dash Cavanerio Named CAR's Best Pitcher
Monday, June 1st, 2020
Jorgan Cavanerio has made fans throughout Carolina League take notice over the past month with an exceptional 4-1 record. No pitcher in the Carolina League threw better in May, garnering him the Pitcher of the Month award today.

In 6 starts Cavanerio compiled a 0.96 ERA, putting up a 4-1 record, striking out 19 and tossing 37.2 innings.

On the year Cavanerio has compiled a 4-3 mark with a 1.86 ERA.
Frederick's Palmeiro Bags CAR Batting Award
Monday, June 1st, 2020
For a rookie just breaking in, Preston Palmeiro certainly put together a successful month. It was good enough to win the CAR Batter of the Month award for May.

The 25-year-old first baseman for the Frederick Keys hit .346 with 4 home runs, 16 RBIs and 12 runs scored.

Looking at his stats this year, Palmeiro has compiled a .335 batting average, while hitting 7 home runs and driving in 21 runs. He has played in 42 games and scored 18 runs.
