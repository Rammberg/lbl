Season Over for Sanchez
Monday, August 3rd, 2020
Miguel Sanchez had to wait several agonizing days to find out his diagnosis after suffering a torn labrum on 07/31/2020. Today he received word from the Carolina doctor that he would be out the rest of the year. Team officials called it a huge setback but something they would have to overcome.

This season Sanchez has accumulated 4 saves, registered a 2.41 ERA and struck out 37 in 37.1 innings. He also has a 4-1 record in 36 relief appearances.
