Myrtle Beach Takes Round 1
Sunday, September 13th, 2020
Fans of the Winston-Salem Dash groaned when D.J. Wilson stepped up to the plate in the Round 1.

And for good reason too, as the Myrtle Beach Pelicans player was instrumental in guiding his team to a 3-1 series win over the Dash.

The series was clinched tonight at BB&T Ballpark, with Myrtle Beach coming out on top 2-1 in the deciding game.

Wilson's strong series included a .312 batting average, with a .353 on-base percentage. He had 1 home runs, drove in 5 RBI and scored 2 runs.

The Myrtle Beach Pelicans will play against the Salem Red Sox in the next round. Salem won their series against the Fredericksburg Nationals 3 games to 0. The Round 2 schedule will be announced after the Round 1 finishes.
Red Sox Dominate, Sweep Round 1
Saturday, September 12th, 2020
The Salem Red Sox appear poised to make a serious run at the Round 2 after a convincing sweep over the Fredericksburg Nationals in the Round 1.

It's now on to the Round 2 for Salem, who won the final game 9-5 and took the series 3-0.

"Everyone's doing their part and things are falling into place," said Salem right fielder Jarren Duran, who was named series MVP. "It feels like a special year."

For the series, Duran hit .727 with a .812 on-base percentage. He had 0 home runs, drove in 1 RBI and scored 7 runs.

The Salem Red Sox don't know who they will face in the next round. They will play the winner of the Winston-Salem Dash - Myrtle Beach Pelicans series. That series is tied at 1 game apiece. The Round 2 schedule will be announced after the Round 1 finishes.
