Melendez Carolina League's Hottest Hitter
Monday, May 18th, 2020
So far in his young career, MJ Melendez has kept a low profile, but after his performance at the plate last week, he attracted the notice of the rest of the Carolina League in a big way as he was named the Player of the Week today for his efforts.

The 21-year-old Wilmington Blue Rocks catcher hit at a torrid .389 clip as he collected 7 hits in 18 at-bats, 5 home runs, 8 RBIs and 5 runs scored.

In the 2020 season Melendez is hitting .163 with 5 home runs.
