Great Week for Salem's Brett Netzer
Monday, September 7th, 2020
So far in his young career, Brett Netzer has kept a low profile, but after his performance at the plate last week, he attracted the notice of the rest of the Carolina League in a big way as he was named the Player of the Week today for his efforts.

The 24-year-old Salem Red Sox second baseman hit at a torrid .538 clip as he collected 14 hits in 26 at-bats, 1 home run, 8 RBIs and 3 runs scored.

For the year Netzer has hit at a .260 clip with 42 RBIs, 6 home runs and 33 runs scored.
