Carter Week's Best in CAR
Monday, August 31st, 2020
So far in his young career, Jodd Carter has kept a low profile, but after his performance at the plate last week, he attracted the notice of the rest of the Carolina League in a big way as he was named the Player of the Week today for his efforts.

The 24-year-old Lynchburg Hillcats right fielder hit at a torrid .526 clip as he collected 10 hits in 19 at-bats, 1 home run, 7 RBIs and 5 runs scored.

During the current season Carter is batting at a .277 clip with 16 home runs and 60 RBIs. He has played in 106 games and scored 72 times.
