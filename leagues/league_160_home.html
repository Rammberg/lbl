Burger Suspended, Out 2 Games
Tuesday, May 19th, 2020
The league commissioner's office has notified the Winston-Salem Dash that first baseman Jake Burger has been given a 2-game suspension for his involvement in an incident with the home plate umpire in yesterday's Winston-Salem-Lynchburg game at Calvin Falwell Field. After being called out on a pitch that he deemed a ball, Burger aggressively expressed his displeasure and was promptly ejected from the game.

In the league suspension notice, it stated that he was suspended for making an obscene gesture toward the umpires prior to entering the dugout. The league said that type of behavior would not be tolerated.

Burger and the Dash were unavailable for comment.

Currently Burger is batting .256 with 5 home runs, 13 RBIs and 18 runs scored.
