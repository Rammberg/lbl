Burlington's Alvarado Gets MID Player of the Week
Monday, August 17th, 2020
The Midwest League made it official today: Luis Alvarado is the Player of the Week.

He really glistened on the diamond this week with a .556 average to take home the honor. The Burlington third baseman went 5-for-9, drove in 7 runs, scored 3 times and hit 3 home runs.

This year Alvarado is hitting .212 with 31 hits, 6 home runs, 13 RBIs and has scored 18 runs.
