Lee Acclaimed Top Pitcher in June
Wednesday, July 1st, 2020
Any time a rookie makes a splash on the scene, it's got to be great for his career potential. Just ask Chris Lee, who put together a stellar month out of the pen. The San Antonio Missions reliever, 27, kept the opposition off balance all month as he held them to a .238 average and walked away with the American Conference Pitcher of the Month award for June.

Lee posted a 3-0 mark and a 0.79 ERA in 10 relief appearances. In 11.1 innings, he had no saves, 14 strikeouts, and 3 walks.

To date, Lee has collected no saves in 13 relief appearances. He has rung up 17 strikeouts and 6 walks in 14.1 innings while registering a 1.26 ERA and a 3-1 won-lost record.
