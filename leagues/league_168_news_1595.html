Aviles Wins GCL Player of the Week Honors
Monday, August 17th, 2020
A red-hot bat made Luis Aviles the obvious choice for this week's Gulf Coast Rookie League Player of the Week.

The Detroit E (GCL) catcher laid down some numbers that no one else could match, including a .588 average on 10 hits in 17 at-bats, 1 home run, 8 RBIs, and 3 runs scored. Say no more ... he got the award.

Looking at his present stats, Aviles has compiled a .353 batting average in 24 games with a .427 on-base percentage, while hitting 2 home runs and driving in 14 runs.
