Diamondbacks Celebrate Wild Card Win
Monday, September 21st, 2020
While you set out to win a division each year, members of the Arizona Diamondbacks seemed plenty satisfied with their place as National League wild card winners.

"The National League West Division crown would have been great, but we're still in a better spot than all the teams that are going home after the regular season is over," said closer Archie Bradley. "You can't complain."

There was certainly no complaining going on as the Diamondbacks clubhouse was filed with loud music and even louder celebrations following the postseason clinch.
