Devers Selected AL Player of the Week
Monday, August 24th, 2020
It's always a thrill for fans of the team and students of the game alike when a superstar in the making shows what he's got. Rafael Devers, the Boston Red Sox 23-year-old third baseman, put himself in the spotlight last week making him the clear-cut choice for American League Player of the Week honors.

He ripped 10 hits in 25 at-bats, compiling a .400 average with 7 home runs and 10 RBIs.

To date, Devers has collected 161 hits for a .333 average. He has posted 32 home runs, scored 91 runs and driven in 82 runs.
