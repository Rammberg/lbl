Rochester Clinches Wild Card
Tuesday, September 8th, 2020
The Rochester Red Wings certainly made things interesting.

The North Division club have battled to the bitter end and have come up with a wild card berth.

"It's satisfying to clinch the playoffs so late in the season," said starting pitcher Devin Smeltzer. "You start to doubt yourself and wonder if you can even make it."

But the Red Wings have in fact made it, with their 77-63 record holding up to now give them a chance at the franchise's 1st International League championship.
