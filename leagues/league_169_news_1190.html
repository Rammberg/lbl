Rivera Has 20-Game Hitting Streak
Wednesday, July 22nd, 2020
Joshua Rivera's consecutive-game hitting streak has now climbed to 20 games. The Great Falls shortstop smacked 1 hit against the Ogden Raptors in today's game at Centene Stadium, but he expressed mixed emotions about the streak after watching the Voyagers drop the game, 3-1.

"I feel good about the streak, but don't feel good when we lose," he told reporters. "I'd love to get a hit as well as a win next time so I can celebrate properly."
Joshua Rivera grounded out in the 1st, grounded out in the 4th, singled in the 6th and struck out in the 8th.

Rivera is hitting .304 this season with 3 home runs, 19 RBIs and 20 runs scored. He has 38 hits in 125 at-bats.
