Torres Slams 3 Homers for Yankees
Friday, July 3rd, 2020
For Gleyber Torres, it was not an average day at the ballpark.

Fans at Rogers Centre today watched Torres power the New York Yankees to a 5-0 win over the Toronto Blue Jays, blasting a trifecta of home runs.

"I've always loved hitting in Rogers Centre," the shortstop told reporters following his big day. "You can see the ball so well."

For the game, he collected 3 hits in 3 at-bats, scored 3 runs and had 3 RBIs.

Gleyber Torres hit a solo-shot off Nate Pearson in the 2nd, hit a solo-shot off Nate Pearson in the 4th, walked in the 7th and hit a solo-shot off Justin Miller in the 9th.

So far this season Torres has gotten 98 hits in 311 at-bats for a .315 batting average with 25 home runs. His stats include 74 RBIs and 69 runs scored. He has played in 78 games.
