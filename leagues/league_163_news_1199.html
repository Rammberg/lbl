League Suspends Hernandez
Friday, July 24th, 2020
After receiving his suspension notification from the league office, Brayan Hernandez of the West Virginia Power told reporters, "Do the crime, do the time. I lost it yesterday and will accept the punishment."

The Power center fielder lost his temper when he was called out on strikes in the game yesterday with Columbia. The consequence was a 3-game suspension.

So far this season Hernandez is batting .242 with 2 home runs, 20 RBIs and 37 runs scored. He has 81 hits in 335 at-bats, 27 walks and a .301 OBP. Hernandez has played in 84 games.
