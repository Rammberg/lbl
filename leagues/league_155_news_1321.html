Pacific Conference Premier Pitcher is Banuelos

In July Tacoma starter Manny Banuelos mystified Pacific Conference hitters and carried off the Pitcher of the Month honors by chalking up a 4-0 record in 5 starts.

The 29-year-old Banuelos added 27 strikeouts in 23.2 innings and 11 walks to his season record and compiled a 1.52 ERA. Opposing batters hit .160 against him.

To date this year Banuelos has compiled a 7-0 mark with a 2.69 ERA in 9 starts.
