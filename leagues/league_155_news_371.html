AC Star of the Week Announced
Monday, May 25th, 2020
The American Conference has selected its Player of the Week and the honor went to Adolis Garcia, Nashville's fine center fielder.

For the week he batted .542 (13-for-24) with 3 home runs, 8 runs and 6 RBIs.

A look at his season totals shows Garcia with 7 home runs, .293 BA, .352 OBP, 20 RBIs and 21 runs scored.
