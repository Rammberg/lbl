Reliever Snags DSL Top Hurler Trophy
Saturday, August 1st, 2020
When Jeremy Mijares strolls in from the bullpen, he is a good bet to nail down the victory for the Oakland (DSL) Athletics. That's the way it was last month as he performed his job in an almost flawless fashion. Today he was honored as July's Pitcher of the Month by the Dominican Rookie League.

The Oakland (DSL) pitcher compiled a 3-0 record, 7 saves, 11 strikeouts and a 1.08 ERA in 16.2 innings over 14 relief appearances.

Mijares has collected 12 saves for the year with 19 strikeouts and 8 walks in 29 innings while registering a 3.10 ERA and a 4-2 won-lost record.
