Round Rock-Iowa Fight Results in Suspensions
Friday, April 24th, 2020
Taylor Sparks of the Round Rock Express and Duncan Robinson of the Iowa Cubs will be on the sidelines for a few days. They have been suspended for 3 and 7 games respectively by the league office for causing the bench-emptying free-for-all at the recent Express-Cubs game at Dell Diamond.

Neither player had any comment about the suspensions. Both clubs told BNN Sparks and Robinson will begin serving them soon.
