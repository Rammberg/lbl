McWilliams Wins Best Bat Trophy in May
Monday, June 1st, 2020
When Sam McWilliams stepped up to the plate in May he did it with confidence, and today he was honored with the California League Batter of the Month award.

The young Rancho Cucamonga left fielder posted a .255 batting average with 5 home runs, 11 RBIs and scored 22 runs. McWilliams also worked pitchers for 29 walks to post a .434 on-base percentage.

McWilliams has collected 36 hits for the Quakes this year, 5 of them home runs. He has also netted 18 RBIs and scored 27 runs while batting .265.
