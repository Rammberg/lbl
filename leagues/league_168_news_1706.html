Best Player of the Week Goes to Priester
Monday, August 24th, 2020
Quinn Priester drew on his youthful energy and pitching skill to win Gulf Coast Rookie League Player of the Week honors last week.

The Pittsburgh (GCL) Pirates pitcher registered 2 wins and no losses, 0.90 ERA and 6 strikeouts in 10 innings to sew up the award. The 19-year-old also held opposing hitters to 5 hits and a .161 batting average.

Season-to-date Priester has rung up 4 wins and 1 loss in 8 appearances. He has an ERA of 2.88 and has fanned 26 batters in 40.2 innings.
