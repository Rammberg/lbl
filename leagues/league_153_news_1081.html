Judge wins HR Challenge trophy
Monday, July 13th, 2020
The 2020 Home Run Challenge provided thrills for the capacity crowd at Yankee Stadium. In the end, Aaron Judge, the New York Yankees right fielder, outclassed the competition. He beat the last challenger, Gleyber Torres, in the final round, 11-10.

"I spent the last two weeks preparing for this event," said Judge. "The hard work paid off."

Judge has a .281 career batting average and has hit a total of 145 home runs.
