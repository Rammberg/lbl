Merrifield Bashes 5 Base Hits
Wednesday, June 3rd, 2020
It doesn't happen very often, but it did for Whit Merrifield today -- a 5-hit effort. He went on a rampage at Kauffman Stadium and paced the Royals to a 21-20 win over the Chicago White Sox.

After his dynamite batting performance, the Kansas City right fielder told reporters that he "just tried to get in a good count and have a good at-bat. That's all I can control."

His philosophy paid dividends today. He slammed 5 hits in 6 tries and put his name in the record book. Whit Merrifield doubled in the 1st, singled in the 3rd, doubled in the 4th, singled in the 6th, singled in the 7th, walked in the 8th and flied out in the 9th.

This season in 49 games, Merrifield has hit at a .345 clip with 76 hits, 5 home runs, 38 RBIs and 48 runs scored.
