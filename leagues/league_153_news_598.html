Injury Sidelines Clevinger for Season
Friday, June 12th, 2020
"Not what I wanted to hear," said Cleveland's Mike Clevinger to reporters this afternoon, as he revealed that the injury he sustained on 06/10/2020 would force him to miss the rest of the season. "The doctor made it clear," Clevinger said. "There's no chance for me -- none at all -- to come back this year. That's the last thing I wanted to hear." Clevinger has been out of action since he suffered a torn labrum.

So far this year Clevinger has made 11 starts, tossed 70.1 innings, struck out 61 batters and walked 27. His record is 6-4 with a 3.33 ERA and he has limited opposing teams to a .203 batting average.
