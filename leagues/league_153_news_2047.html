Cody Bellinger out with DTD Injury
Wednesday, September 9th, 2020
Dodgers star center fielder Cody Bellinger might be sitting it out a few days after he picked up an injury in yesterday's game against the Chicago White Sox. He sustained a strained oblique while making a defensive play. Bellinger isn't expected to miss a lot of time. However, doctors want to be cautious. They said he would be day-to-day for about 2 weeks.

A look at his season totals shows Bellinger with 31 home runs, .290 BA, .421 OBP, 124 RBIs and 117 runs scored.
