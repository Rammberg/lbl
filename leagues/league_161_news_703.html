Clemens Nabs FSL Weekly Award
Monday, June 22nd, 2020
The Florida State League made it official today: Kacy Clemens is the Player of the Week.

He really glistened on the diamond this week with a .375 average to take home the honor. The Dunedin first baseman went 9-for-24, drove in 8 runs, scored 5 times and hit 3 home runs.

Posting a .191 batting average to date, Clemens has 6 home runs and 24 RBIs.
