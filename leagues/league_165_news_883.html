Kevin Watson Jr. Bashes Three over the Wall
Thursday, July 2nd, 2020
Whatever Kevin Watson Jr. ate for breakfast before his game against the Boise Hawks today, it gave him some impressive power.

The Hillsboro right fielder smacked three balls up and out of the park at Memorial Stadium. His home run barrage led the Hops to take the 7-2 victory over the Hawks.

"When they throw it in your wheelhouse, you've got a shot at a homer," he told BNN reporters after the game. "I got three shots today and nailed them all."

On his 3-homer day, he went 3-for-3, scored 4 times, drove in 4 and put on a display that history will remember.

Kevin Watson Jr. hit a two-run home run off Nick Bush in the 1st, hit a solo-shot off Nick Bush in the 3rd, walked in the 5th, walked in the 7th and hit a solo-shot off Javier Medina in the 9th.

For the year Watson Jr. is batting .233 with 14 hits and 5 home runs. He has scored 9 times and driven in 12.
