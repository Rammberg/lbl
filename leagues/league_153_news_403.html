Segura Socks it to Brewers, Cracks 5 Hits
Monday, May 25th, 2020
It seemed that Philadelphia second baseman Jean Segura could do no wrong as the Phillies KO'd the Brewers, 26-23 today. Segura rapped out 5 hits in 8 at-bats and seemed to know which pitches Brewers hurlers were throwing.

"I think the Phillies were stealing signs," growled one unhappy Milwaukee fan after the game.

"I just felt good today. I saw the ball well and just wanted to make good contact and put the ball in play," said the jubilant Segura in the postgame press interview.

Jean Segura struck out in the 2nd, hit a two-run double in the 3rd, singled in the 4th, singled in the 5th, hit a two-run home run off Blake Wood in the 6th, hit an RBI single in the 6th, grounded out in the 8th and grounded out in the 9th.

Segura is hitting .297 this year with 3 home runs and 23 RBIs. He also has an OBP of .353, 11 walks and scored 22 times.
