Star RP Giles Signs for 3 years
Tuesday, August 4th, 2020
Happy times in New York as the Yankees and star reliever Ken Giles put the finishing touches on a $34,800,000 contract extension that will keep the 29-year-old in town 3 years longer.

The signing is great news for New York because players of this caliber are hard to come by.

So far this season, Giles has compiled a 1-1 won-lost mark with 16 saves and a 5.87 ERA. In 38.1 innings he has fanned 51 and opponents are hitting him at a .267 clip.
