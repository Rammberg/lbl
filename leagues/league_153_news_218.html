MLB News: Draft Pool Revealed
Tuesday, May 5th, 2020
With the 2020 rookie draft only 30 days away, teams across Major League Baseball are preparing their scouting missions, trying to assess their long-term needs and hoping that they land the prospect that will positively define their club for the next few years. Most of the young men in the draft pool will have gone as far as they can go, but then, a few will surprise and pan out as quality -- or even great -- professional ballplayers.

For a complete listing of those entered in the draft, consult the newly created draft section on the Transactions page, which will be available for 30 days prior to draft day.

The early favorites to be high picks are the following:
1. Austin Martin, 21 year-old SS out of college.
2. Spencer Torkelson, 20 year-old LF out of college.
3. Emerson Hancock, 20 year-old RHP out of college.
4. Asa Lacy, 20 year-old LHP out of college.
5. Nick Bitsko, 17 year-old RHP out of high-school.
6. Nick Gonzales, 20 year-old 2B out of college.
7. Mick Abel, 18 year-old RHP out of high-school.
8. Ed Howard, 18 year-old SS out of high-school.
9. Garrett Mitchell, 21 year-old CF out of college.
10. Jordan Walker, 17 year-old 3B out of high-school.

The official OSA Mock Draft will be released on the day of the draft, or you can review the draft pool on the draft page.
