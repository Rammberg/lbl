NYP's #1 Player Named
Monday, August 3rd, 2020
It was a fantastic week for State College first baseman Pedro Pages and it was capped off today with the announcement that he was named the winner of the New York-Penn League Player of the Week Award.

Last week Pages hammered the ball at a torrid .500 pace, going 10-for-20 with 1 home run, 6 RBIs and 5 runs scored.

Pages has collected 55 hits for the Spikes this year, 2 of them home runs. He has also netted 25 RBIs and scored 26 runs while batting .342.
