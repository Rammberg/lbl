It was a fantastic week for Batavia catcher Pablo Garcia and it was capped off today with the announcement that he was named the winner of the New York-Penn League Player of the Week Award.

Last week Garcia hammered the ball at a torrid .533 pace, going 8-for-15 with 1 home run, 7 RBIs and 4 runs scored.
