5-Hit Barrage by Gentry
Sunday, June 21st, 2020
All game long, Williamsport's Tyler Gentry was rattling the walls at Bowman Field and banging out base hits against the West Virginia Black Bears. When the dust finally settled on the diamond, he had a 5-hit day and the Crosscutters had a 9-6 victory.

Speaking with reporters after his standout performance, the 21-year-old right fielder said, "Get 5 hits, win a ballgame, it's nice. I could get used to this."

Tyler Gentry hit a three-run home run off Ramon Guzman in the 1st, hit a two-run single in the 1st, doubled in the 4th, singled in the 6th and singled in the 8th.
