NYP Awards Player of the Week Trophy
Monday, July 13th, 2020
Mike Antico of the Auburn Doubledays had a super week and today was voted the Player of the Week by the New York-Penn League.

The 22-year-old left fielder put up a .480 average with a .552 on-base percentage, racking up 12 hits in 25 at-bats, 1 home run, 9 RBIs and 3 runs scored.

Thus far this season Antico is hitting .405 with 1 home run, 9 RBIs and 7 runs scored. He has 17 hits in 42 at-bats and has played in 12 games.
