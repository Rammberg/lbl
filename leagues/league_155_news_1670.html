San Antonio Cruises to American Conference South Division Crown
Friday, August 21st, 2020
The San Antonio Missions said they expected to win the American Conference South Division from day one.

It is now closer to the end than it is to day one, but that brash attitude has paid off, as the team has in fact clinched the division.

"We believe in each other and we think we're by far the best team in the division. We proved that this year," said reliever Dylan File. "We're sitting at 79-41 right now, well ahead of the rest of the division, it feels good. This is a statement for us."

Whether the confidence carries over into the team's 1st Round 2 remains to be seen, but this is the 1st time they have won the division.
