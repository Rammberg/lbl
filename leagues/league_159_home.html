Star of the Week Awarded in CAL
Monday, May 4th, 2020
So far in his young career, Gersel Pitre has kept a low profile, but after his performance at the plate last week, he attracted the notice of the rest of the California League in a big way as he was named the Player of the Week today for his efforts.

The 23-year-old Rancho Cucamonga Quakes catcher hit at a torrid .526 clip as he collected 10 hits in 19 at-bats, 1 home run, 6 RBIs and 5 runs scored.

On the year Pitre has hit .298 with 1 home run, 10 RBIs and scored 7 runs.
