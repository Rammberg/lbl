Murray CAL Winner, Top Hurler in June
Wednesday, July 1st, 2020
Colton Murray was the anchor in the Stockton bullpen last month and snatched up the California League Pitcher of the Month honors for June. The 30-year-old is in the prime of his career and pitching like he intends to stay around baseball for quite some time to come.

He threw 13.2 innings over 10 relief appearances and collected a 2-0 record with 23 strikeouts and 5 saves while etching his 0.66 ERA.

So far this season, Murray has a 2-0 won-lost record with 5 saves. He has made 10 relief appearances, logged 13.2 innings and fanned 23 batters while fashioning a 0.66 ERA and allowing the opposition a .200 batting average.
June's #1 Batter Announced
Wednesday, July 1st, 2020
When Willie Abreu stepped up to the plate in June he did it with confidence, and today he was honored with the California League Batter of the Month award.

The young Lancaster right fielder posted a .273 batting average with 7 home runs, 20 RBIs and scored 17 runs. Abreu also worked pitchers for 13 walks to post a .379 on-base percentage.

Thus far in 2020 Abreu is hitting .278 with 7 home runs, 20 RBIs, 17 runs scored and has compiled a .376 OBP.
