Michael Busch Wins CAL Trophy
Monday, May 25th, 2020
22-year-old Michael Busch made a few Rancho Cucamonga headlines during this past week as the rookie second baseman proved he could compete at the plate with his high-school idols. He captured the attention of fans, journalists and California League officials last week and today took home Player of the Week honors for his effort.

He cracked 11 hits in 23 at-bats to put up a .478 average. Busch collected no home runs, 2 RBIs and scored 2 runs.

This year Busch is batting .328 with no home runs. He has scored 23 runs and driven in 12.
