Season-Ending Injury for Willeman
Wednesday, May 13th, 2020
Zach Willeman had hoped for the best, but expected the worst. Today, he learned from the Rancho Cucamonga medical staff that he would miss the rest of the season. The Rancho Cucamonga starting pitcher suffered a damaged elbow ligament in an earlier ballgame. He admitted to being discouraged, but said he plans to come back strong next year.

This year Willeman has fashioned a 1-1 mark, 4.33 ERA and pitched 27 innings. Opposing clubs are batting .219 against him and he has logged 21 strikeouts.
