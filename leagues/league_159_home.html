Week's Top Player: Visalia's Karaviotis
Monday, August 24th, 2020
So far in his young career, Mark Karaviotis has kept a low profile, but after his performance at the plate last week, he attracted the notice of the rest of the California League in a big way as he was named the Player of the Week today for his efforts.

The 24-year-old Visalia Rawhide first baseman hit at a torrid .448 clip as he collected 13 hits in 29 at-bats, 2 home runs, 7 RBIs and 2 runs scored.

Presently Karaviotis is batting .280 with 109 hits and 6 home runs. He has knocked in 48 runs and scored 51 times.
