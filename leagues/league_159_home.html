Deichmann Caps Great Week
Monday, July 6th, 2020
Greg Deichmann of the Stockton Ports had a super week and today was voted the Player of the Week by the California League.

The 25-year-old right fielder put up a .481 average with a .533 on-base percentage, racking up 13 hits in 27 at-bats, 4 home runs, 6 RBIs and 8 runs scored.

Deichmann's current batting average stands at .247 and he has chalked up 14 home runs and 36 RBIs.
