CAL Player of the Week Awarded
Monday, June 22nd, 2020
His hard work and hustle paid off this week. The hard-working and hard-playing Frankie Rios was rewarded with the California League Player of the Week honors.

The scrappy Stockton Ports third baseman hit .550 while collecting 11 hits in 20 at-bats, no home runs and 3 RBIs.

Looking at his stats this year, Rios has compiled a .433 batting average, while hitting no home runs and driving in 3 runs. He has played in 9 games and scored 5 runs.
