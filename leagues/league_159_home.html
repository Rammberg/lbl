Damron Stands Out, Choice for Best Pitcher
Friday, May 1st, 2020
Ty Damron took a much-deserved turn in the spotlight earlier today as he accepted the California League Pitcher of the Month award for April.

The 25-year-old Ports starter is a relative newcomer to the league, but nonetheless racked up an impressive 3-0 won-lost record in 3 starts. This month he fanned 13 batters in 18.1 innings and compiled a 2.45 ERA.
Rancho Cucamonga's Vargas Snaps up April Batting Award
Friday, May 1st, 2020
Rancho Cucamonga Quakes fans believe they've got a talented young star in Miguel Vargas.

He did nothing to dissuade that notion in April, as he hit .268 with 3 home runs, 10 RBIs and 9 runs scored.

The end result was the first baseman being named the California League Batter of the Month.
