CAL Star of the Week Announced
Monday, August 10th, 2020
20-year-old Jordyn Adams made a few Inland Empire headlines during this past week as the rookie center fielder proved he could compete at the plate with his high-school idols. He captured the attention of fans, journalists and California League officials last week and today took home Player of the Week honors for his effort.

He cracked 11 hits in 22 at-bats to put up a .500 average. Adams collected 1 home run, 5 RBIs and scored 7 runs.

This year Adams is batting .235 with 66 hits and 3 home runs. He has scored 31 times and has batted in 25 runs.
