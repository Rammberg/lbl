Week's Premier Player: Lake Elsinore's Suwinski
Monday, July 27th, 2020
It can be difficult for a young player to step out of the shadows and assert himself in the California League, but last week Jack Suwinski of the Lake Elsinore Storm reminded everyone just why he's here as he threw up numbers that made him an easy selection for Player of the Week. Suwinski rocked opposing pitching for a .417 average (10-for-24), no home runs and 2 RBIs to come away with the award.

This year Suwinski is batting .266 with 69 hits and 6 home runs. He has scored 27 times and has batted in 34 runs.
