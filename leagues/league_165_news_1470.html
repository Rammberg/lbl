NWL Announces Player of the Week
Monday, August 10th, 2020
Today the Northwest League announced the Player of the Week Award. This week's honoree is Boise third baseman Julio Carreras. When he steps into the batter's box, it's usually bad news for pitchers.

For the week he batted .389 (7-for-18) with 1 home run and 5 RBIs.

For the season Carreras is hitting .246, batted in 17 runs with 4 home runs and 16 runs scored.
