Week's Premier Player: Boston's Martinez
Monday, August 10th, 2020
J.D. Martinez is respected by most Boston Red Sox fans for his no-frills style of play. The 32-year-old right fielder went about his business with vigor last week to grab the American League Player of the Week honors.

He compiled a .500 average (15-for-30) with 3 home runs, 10 RBIs and 11 runs scored.

This year Martinez is batting .311 with 28 home runs and 89 RBIs.
