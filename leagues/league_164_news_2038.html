Doubledays Captures Wild Card
Wednesday, September 9th, 2020
The Auburn Doubledays certainly made things interesting.

The Pinckney Division club have battled to the bitter end and have come up with a wild card berth.

"It's satisfying to clinch the playoffs so late in the season," said reliever Alfonso Hernandez. "You start to doubt yourself and wonder if you can even make it."

But the Doubledays have in fact made it, with their 41-36 record holding up to now give them a chance at the franchise's 1st New York-Penn League championship.
