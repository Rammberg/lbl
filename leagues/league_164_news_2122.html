Muckdogs Win Round 1
Tuesday, September 15th, 2020
The Batavia Muckdogs are riding high after knocking off the Lowell Spinners in the Round 1.

"We're feeling confident now and that's half the battle," said Pablo Garcia, who was named series MVP. "You play confidently, you play well and it shows up with results like this."

Batavia won the final game by a 6-3 count at Edward A. LeLacheur Park and the series 3-1.

For the series, Garcia hit .438 with a .500 on-base percentage. He had 1 home runs, drove in 5 RBI and scored 3 runs.

The Batavia Muckdogs don't know who they will face in the next round. They will play the winner of the Brooklyn Cyclones - Hudson Valley Renegades series. Hudson Valley leads the series 2 game to 1. The Round 2 schedule will be announced after the Round 1 finishes.
