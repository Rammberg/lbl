DSL Player of the Week, Garcia of New York A (DSL)
Monday, June 29th, 2020
Anthony Garcia stepped up to the plate in a big way last week and today he took home the Dominican Rookie League Player of the Week honors.

The New York A (DSL) right fielder ripped opposing pitchers for 11 hits in 19 at-bats and 1 home run. He put together a sizzling .579 batting average as he knocked in 7 runs and scored 8 times.

Garcia is hitting .306 this season with 3 home runs, 15 RBIs and 20 runs scored. He has 26 hits in 85 at-bats.
