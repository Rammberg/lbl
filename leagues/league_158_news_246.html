Raleigh Chosen #1 Player
Monday, May 11th, 2020
A hot-hitting week got Cal Raleigh the Texas League Player of the Week trophy. He really wore out his welcome with league pitchers.

The Arkansas catcher slammed them for a .500 average, 8 hits in 16 at-bats, 2 home runs, 6 RBIs and scored 4 runs.

In the postgame press conference, Raleigh shared his hitting philosophy with the BNN scribes: "Just look for the seams and hit between them... nothin' more than that."

So far this season Raleigh has hit .343 with 4 home runs, 14 RBIs and scored 8 times.
