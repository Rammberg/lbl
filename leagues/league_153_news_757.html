Chicago-Detroit Fight Results in Suspensions
Thursday, June 25th, 2020
Edwin Encarnacion of the Chicago White Sox and Daniel Norris of the Detroit Tigers received notification from the league commissioner's office that they had been suspended for 4 and 9 games respectively for their part in the on-field ruckus in a recent game at Guaranteed Rate Field.

In 80 games this season Encarnacion is batting .296 with 85 hits and 27 home runs. He has scored 68 runs and driven in 58.

Norris has a 2-8 season record so far. In 14 starts, he has fanned 58 batters in 71 innings with a 4.94 ERA.
