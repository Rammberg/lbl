Reliever of the Year Goes to Cozart
Sunday, September 27th, 2020
The Eastern League Reliever of the Year for 2020 has been bestowed on Hartford Yard Goats relief specialist Logan Cozart.

The 27-year-old bullpen ace, out of New Philadelphia, Ohio, nailed down the award with 6 saves in 36 relief appearances, 42 strikeouts in 43.2 innings and a record of 3 wins and no losses to go with his 2.06 ERA.

He received 9 first place votes out of a possible 12. Henry Martinez of the Akron RubberDucks finished second in voting with 1 first place vote, while Kyle Nelson of the Akron RubberDucks finished third with 2 first place votes.
