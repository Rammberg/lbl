Vilade: Big Hit in the Eastern League
Monday, July 13th, 2020
Ryan Vilade demonstrated his potential to be a star in the game last week and today he took home the Eastern League Player of the Week Award.

The Hartford third baseman wreaked havoc on opposing pitchers last week, pounding them for a .522 average (12-for-23) with 1 home run and 3 RBIs on his way to claiming the honor.

Vilade is batting .290 this season in 78 games with 4 home runs, 36 RBIs and 40 runs scored. He has 93 hits in 321 at-bats.
