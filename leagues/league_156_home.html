Season Done for Schmidt
Monday, May 18th, 2020
In a news release, the Trenton Thunder announced that starting pitcher Clarke Schmidt would miss the rest of the season due to a torn rotator cuff. Schmidt sustained the injury on 05/14/2020. He "will be sorely missed," said his manager.

To date this season, Schmidt has registered 6 starts, 2-3 won-lost mark, 6.51 ERA, 27.2 IP, 23 strikeouts and 9 walks. Opposing teams have hit .304 against him.
