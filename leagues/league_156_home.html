Season Ends for Jerez
Tuesday, May 12th, 2020
Williams Jerez has to call it a season. The Altoona Curve reliever learned today that the full extent his injury suffered on 05/09/2020 is worse than hoped. He will not play again this year according to club doctors, who diagnosed the injury as a torn ulnar collateral ligament and stated that he would not be able to play for 13-14 months. The team will likely place Jerez on the injured list today.

Jerez has nailed down no saves for the year. He has also totaled 10 strikeouts and 5 walks in 12.2 innings while registering a 2.13 ERA and a 0-0 won-lost record.
