Reading Fightin Phils Capture Round 1
Wednesday, September 16th, 2020
It's party time in Reading. The confetti is flying and residents are smiling.

The Reading Fightin Phils have reached the mountaintop, capturing the 2020 Round 1 today. They defeated the Harrisburg Senators 4-3 at FirstEnergy Stadium to officially wrap up the series 3-2.

Asked how it feels to be champion, shortstop Arquimedes Gamboa was to the point.

"It feels great, like a million bucks. I'm so happy right now, not just for me but for all the guys," he said. "We put a lot of work into this and deserve to be champions."

The team is planning a victory parade and fan appreciation day later in the week. Reading finished the regular season 91-49 and claimed first place in the Eastern Division.
