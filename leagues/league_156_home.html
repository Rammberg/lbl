Ward of Harrisburg Crowned Best Player
Monday, August 17th, 2020
25-year-old Drew Ward made a few Harrisburg headlines during this past week as the rookie first baseman proved he could compete at the plate with his high-school idols. He captured the attention of fans, journalists and Eastern League officials last week and today took home Player of the Week honors for his effort.

He cracked 12 hits in 23 at-bats to put up a .522 average. Ward collected 2 home runs, 6 RBIs and scored 4 runs.

Ward is hitting .267 this year with 18 home runs and 66 RBIs. He also has an OBP of .336, 38 walks and scored 57 times.
