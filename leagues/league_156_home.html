Wiseman Week's #1 Player
Monday, May 25th, 2020
It can be difficult for a young player to step out of the shadows and assert himself in the Eastern League, but last week Rhett Wiseman of the Harrisburg Senators reminded everyone just why he's here as he threw up numbers that made him an easy selection for Player of the Week. Wiseman rocked opposing pitching for a .450 average (9-for-20), 3 home runs and 7 RBIs to come away with the award.

This season Wiseman is hitting .276 with 32 hits, 7 home runs, 21 RBIs, scored 18 runs and played in 30 games.
