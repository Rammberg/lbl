Yajure Top EL Pitcher in June
Wednesday, July 1st, 2020
Miguel Yajure hasn't been around long enough to know that youngsters shouldn't be winning Eastern League Pitcher of the Month. But when you put up numbers like his, you're going to get some notice.

In June the Trenton starter earned a 5-1 mark in 6 starts. He threw 36.1 innings, struck out 33 and compiled a solid 1.73 ERA.

To date this season Yajure has compiled a 6-4 record with a 2.62 ERA in 14 starts.
Jones Wins EL Batter of the Month
Wednesday, July 1st, 2020
Ryder Jones has a lot going for him. The Portland third baseman just broke into the majors this year and now the Eastern League has just picked him as its top hitter for June.

In 24 games last month this outstanding rookie batted .417 with 2 home runs, 23 RBIs and 17 runs scored.

For the season Jones is currently batting .385 with 4 home runs and 30 RBIs. In 35 games he has reached base at a .455 rate and scored 24 runs to date.
