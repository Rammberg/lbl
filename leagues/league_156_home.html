Reading's Bohm EL Star of the Week
Monday, June 8th, 2020
Alec Bohm has the tools to stick in this league for many years. The 23-year-old third baseman of the Reading Fightin Phils had a hot bat in the Eastern League over the past seven days and won the Player of the Week honors.

Bohm batted .448 with 13 hits in 29 at-bats, 2 home runs and 7 RBIs.

In 48 games this season Bohm has compiled a .277 batting average with 54 hits and 6 home runs. He has also scored 25 times and knocked in 27 runs.
