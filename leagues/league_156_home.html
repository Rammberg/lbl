3 Bombs for Darick Hall
Thursday, July 2nd, 2020
The "hardest feat in sports" debate is a never-ending one with no correct answer, but hitting three home runs is certainly in the conversation. That's exactly what Darick Hall did today at FirstEnergy Stadium, going deep three times and carrying his team to victory, 9-1.

"It all came together. I was seeing the ball well and I got some good pitches to hit," Hall said. "Good things happen, when that happens."

The Reading Fightin Phils first baseman blistered the Richmond Flying Squirrels for 3 hits, batted in 6 runs and scored 3 times.

"It ain't like we weren't trying," Richmond manager Jose Alguacil said. "He was hot and really torched my boys today."

Darick Hall hit a solo-shot off Luis Madero in the 2nd, hit a two-run home run off Luis Madero in the 3rd, walked in the 4th, hit a three-run home run off Carlos Sano in the 6th and struck out in the 8th.

For the season Hall has produced 65 hits in 270 at-bats for a .241 average with 24 home runs and gotten on base at a .338 clip.
Yajure Top EL Pitcher in June
Wednesday, July 1st, 2020
Miguel Yajure hasn't been around long enough to know that youngsters shouldn't be winning Eastern League Pitcher of the Month. But when you put up numbers like his, you're going to get some notice.

In June the Trenton starter earned a 5-1 mark in 6 starts. He threw 36.1 innings, struck out 33 and compiled a solid 1.73 ERA.

To date this season Yajure has compiled a 6-4 record with a 2.62 ERA in 14 starts.
Jones Wins EL Batter of the Month
Wednesday, July 1st, 2020
Ryder Jones has a lot going for him. The Portland third baseman just broke into the majors this year and now the Eastern League has just picked him as its top hitter for June.

In 24 games last month this outstanding rookie batted .417 with 2 home runs, 23 RBIs and 17 runs scored.

For the season Jones is currently batting .385 with 4 home runs and 30 RBIs. In 35 games he has reached base at a .455 rate and scored 24 runs to date.
