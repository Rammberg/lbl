EL Player of the Week Goes to Jones of Portland
Monday, June 22nd, 2020
Ryder Jones of the Portland Sea Dogs has just enjoyed one of the better weeks of his career. That fact was not lost on Eastern League officials, who named him Player of the Week.

Jones logged a .478 batting average for the Portland Sea Dogs, racking up 8 RBIs and no home runs.

So far in 2020 Jones is batting .388 with 3 home runs, 23 RBIs and 17 runs scored. He has 40 hits in 103 at-bats and a .467 OBP.
