Szapucki Electric in 11-0 Win
Monday, June 1st, 2020
Today was Thomas Szapucki's turn in the rotation for the Binghamton Rumble Ponies and he made the most of it, shutting out the Akron RubberDucks on 3 hits.

The 23-year-old left-hander led the Rumble Ponies to an easy 11-0 victory, allowing 1 walk and tallying 9 strikeouts.

"I hope we miss him next time," Akron manager Rouglas Odor said. "Szapucki certainly pitched a strong game against us today. He was in a groove, throwing everything for strikes. Throwing hard. Changing speeds."

In pitching stats this season Szapucki has rung up 6 wins and 1 loss in 9 games. He has an ERA of 1.40 and has fanned 58 batters in 57.2 innings.

Fisher Cats Murphy Chosen May's Best in EL
Monday, June 1st, 2020
In May, Patrick Murphy tore through the Eastern League like a seasoned veteran -- quite impressive for a player only 24 years old.

The young New Hampshire starter posted a 4-1 mark and an ERA of 1.47 to earn the Pitcher of the Month award. In 43 innings he collected 36 strikeouts while limiting the opposition to a .217 average.

Checking out his stats for the season, Murphy has racked up 5 wins and 2 losses in 9 starts, 2.10 ERA and struck out 53 batters in 60 innings.
