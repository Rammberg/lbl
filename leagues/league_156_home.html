Jones Has Season End
Monday, July 20th, 2020
Hunter Jones had to wait several agonizing days to find out his diagnosis after suffering a torn posterior cruciate ligament on 07/19/2020. Today he received word from the Harrisburg doctor that he would be out the rest of the year. Team officials called it a huge setback but something they would have to overcome.

On the year Jones has hit .136 with no home runs, 3 RBIs and scored 4 runs.
