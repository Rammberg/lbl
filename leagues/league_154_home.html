IL #1 Pitcher in June is Durham's O'Brien
Wednesday, July 1st, 2020
Over the past month Riley O'Brien has turned quite a few heads. He's young, talented and able to pitch with the established arms in the International League. He proved all that and now he's been named June's Pitcher of the Month.

The rookie for the Durham Bulls posted 3-1 mark and a 1.47 ERA. In 5 starts he tossed 30.2 innings, permitted 22 hits and fanned 31 batters.

This year in 13 starts, O'Brien has recorded 7 wins and absorbed 3 losses. He has pitched 75.1 innings, allowed 54 hits, struck out 77 and has a 3.11 ERA.
Brosseau Picks Up Best IL Batter Honors for June
Wednesday, July 1st, 2020
For a rookie just breaking in, Michael Brosseau certainly put together a successful month. It was good enough to win the IL Batter of the Month award for June.

The 26-year-old third baseman for the Durham Bulls hit .371 with 4 home runs, 22 RBIs and 17 runs scored.

Brosseau has collected 89 hits for the Bulls this year, 9 of them home runs. He has also netted 49 RBIs and scored 44 runs while batting .338.
