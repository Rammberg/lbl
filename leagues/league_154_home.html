Colin Moran Captures IL Award
Monday, August 31st, 2020
The Indianapolis Indians received word today that third baseman Colin Moran was selected International League Player of the Week for his stellar performance in last week's games.

Moran took full advantage of all 18 plate appearances last week as he hit .588 with 10 hits, 2 home runs, 5 RBIs and 3 runs scored.

A look at his season totals shows Moran with 6 home runs, .255 BA, .315 OBP, 39 RBIs and 42 runs scored.
