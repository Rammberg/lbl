IL Trophy Goes to Tsutsugo of Durham
Monday, September 7th, 2020
Yoshitomo Tsutsugo is respected by most Durham Bulls fans for his no-frills style of play. The 28-year-old left fielder went about his business with vigor last week to grab the International League Player of the Week honors.

He compiled a .455 average (10-for-22) with 1 home run, 6 RBIs and 2 runs scored.

Hitting .251 to date, Tsutsugo has 25 home runs and 74 RBIs. He has played in 121 games.
