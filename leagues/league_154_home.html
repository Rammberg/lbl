Honeywell Jr. Dominates Toledo in 3-0 Whitewash
Wednesday, May 6th, 2020
The Durham Bulls shut out the Toledo Mud Hens 3-0 today behind the strong pitching of Brent Honeywell Jr.. Honeywell Jr. surrendered only 1 hit in recording the complete-game victory, and struck out 9.

Following the last out, Honeywell Jr. appeared to celebrate a little too boisterously for Toledo manager Tom Prince, who could be seen scolding the famously outspoken right-hander as the clubs left the field. Honeywell Jr.'s own manager then intervened with some words for his counterpart.

The Mud Hens skipper would not comment on the exchange, but at the Bulls press conference, Honeywell Jr. confirmed that it was regarding the celebration.

"He basically told me I was being a lousy winner," Honeywell Jr. said.

"And I told him he was being a lousy loser," Durham manager Brady Williams added.
