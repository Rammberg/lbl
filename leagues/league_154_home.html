5-Hit Game for Plawecki
Monday, June 15th, 2020
It wasn't just another day at the ballpark for Kevin Plawecki today. Plawecki went on a tear and cracked hit after hit off Lehigh Valley pitchers. When the dust settled on the diamond at Coca-Cola Park, he had a 5-for-5 line and a day he will long remember. And the Pawtucket Red Sox had a 10-1 victory over the IronPigs.

"Yeah, it'll be something nice to tell the grandkids, but it won't get me a hit tomorrow," he joked in the postgame interview.

Kevin Plawecki doubled in the 2nd, hit an RBI single in the 3rd, singled in the 5th, doubled in the 7th and hit an RBI single in the 9th.

Thus far in 2020 Plawecki is hitting .305 with 4 home runs, 20 RBIs, 9 runs scored and has compiled a .357 OBP.
