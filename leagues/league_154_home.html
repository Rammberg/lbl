IL Player of the Week Awarded
Monday, July 6th, 2020
Andrew Pullin of the Pawtucket Red Sox was locked in all week as he bullied the pitchers he faced. Pullin put up some great numbers and hauled in the International League Player of the Week trophy.

He hit .458 with 3 home runs and 10 RBIs. The 26-year-old left fielder also scored 8 runs and for the week went 11-for-24.

For the season Pullin is batting .318 with 70 hits and 14 home runs. He has scored 42 times and batted in 43.
