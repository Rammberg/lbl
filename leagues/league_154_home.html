Bad Boy Brawlers Suspended 4 Games
Wednesday, June 3rd, 2020
There was a mass free-for-all yesterday at BB&T Stadium. Both dugouts emptied when Charlotte's Nick Madrigal and Rochester's Austin Adams scuffled with one another after some heated words.

In an official statement today, the league commissioner suspended both parties for 4 games.

For the season Madrigal is batting .271 with 48 hits and 1 home run. He has scored 18 times and batted in 17.

Adams has taken the mound for 12 relief appearances this season, nailing down no saves while notching a 2-0 record with a 3.52 ERA. The Rochester reliever has limited opposing batters to a .200 batting average.
Season-Ending Injury for Borucki
Wednesday, June 3rd, 2020
Clearly dejected, Ryan Borucki left the Buffalo clubhouse nearly in tears this afternoon. He acknowledged that his season is over after sustaining bone chips in his elbow in a ballgame recently. It brings an early end to the Buffalo reliever's season.

So far this year Borucki has been credited with no saves, registered a 3.26 ERA and struck out 15 in 19.1 innings. He also has a 2-1 record.
