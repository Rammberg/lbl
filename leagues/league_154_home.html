Season-Ending Injury for Borucki
Wednesday, June 3rd, 2020
Clearly dejected, Ryan Borucki left the Buffalo clubhouse nearly in tears this afternoon. He acknowledged that his season is over after sustaining bone chips in his elbow in a ballgame recently. It brings an early end to the Buffalo reliever's season.

So far this year Borucki has been credited with no saves, registered a 3.26 ERA and struck out 15 in 19.1 innings. He also has a 2-1 record.

International League Picks Best Starter in May
Monday, June 1st, 2020
There's no doubt that Bats starter Rob Wooten earned his International League Pitcher of the Month award. He left absolutely no room for doubt as he mowed his way through the opposition in May.

During the month Wooten was 4-1 with a 2.57 ERA. He made 6 starts, tossed 35 innings and had 29 strikeouts.

Checking out his stats for the season, Wooten has registered 4 wins and 1 loss in 6 starts, 2.21 ERA and struck out 35 batters in 40.2 innings.
