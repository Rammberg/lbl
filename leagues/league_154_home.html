IL Star of the Week Announced
Monday, June 22nd, 2020
Michael Brosseau burst onto the International League stage in a big way last week. This promising rookie third baseman for Durham garnered everyone's respect by putting up some remarkable numbers and picking up the Player of the Week trophy.

The 26-year-old haunted opposing pitchers hitting .520 (13-for-25) with 1 home run and 9 RBIs last week.

Brosseau is hitting .330 for the season with 8 home runs, 42 RBIs and 37 runs scored. He has 77 hits in 233 at-bats and played in 60 games.
