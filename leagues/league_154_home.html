Williams Tags Mud Hens for 5 Hits
Tuesday, May 19th, 2020
Today at Fifth Third Field, the Toledo Mud Hens topped the Norfolk Tides in spite of a standout batting performance by right fielder Mason Williams.

Williams had a very impressive day at the plate, spanking Toledo pitching for 5 base hits, but couldn't do enough for a win. Toledo whipped Norfolk by the score of 8-7.

Mason Williams hit an RBI single in the 1st, hit an RBI single in the 2nd, doubled in the 5th, singled in the 7th, walked in the 9th, singled in the 11th, flied out in the 13th and struck out in the 16th.

A look at his current stats shows Williams with a .287 average, no home runs, 8 RBIs and 10 runs scored. He has played in 26 games.
