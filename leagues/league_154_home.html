VanMeter Wins IL Player of the Week Prize
Monday, August 3rd, 2020
So far in his young career, Josh VanMeter has kept a low profile, but after his performance at the plate last week, he attracted the notice of the rest of the International League in a big way as he was named the Player of the Week today for his efforts.

The 25-year-old Louisville Bats second baseman hit at a torrid .462 clip as he collected 12 hits in 26 at-bats, 2 home runs, 8 RBIs and 6 runs scored.

VanMeter is currently hitting at a .387 pace with 2 home runs and 12 RBIs.
