Mountcastle Selected IL Player of the Week
Monday, July 27th, 2020
So far in his young career, Ryan Mountcastle has kept a low profile, but after his performance at the plate last week, he attracted the notice of the rest of the International League in a big way as he was named the Player of the Week today for his efforts.

The 23-year-old Norfolk Tides first baseman hit at a torrid .417 clip as he collected 10 hits in 24 at-bats, 3 home runs, 7 RBIs and 4 runs scored.

Mountcastle is hitting .271 in 2020 with 19 RBIs and 6 home runs.
