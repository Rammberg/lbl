RailRiders Trey Amburgey Wins IL Award
Monday, June 8th, 2020
25-year-old Trey Amburgey made a few Scranton WB headlines during this past week as the rookie left fielder proved he could compete at the plate with his high-school idols. He captured the attention of fans, journalists and International League officials last week and today took home Player of the Week honors for his effort.

He cracked 8 hits in 11 at-bats to put up a .727 average. Amburgey collected 2 home runs, 4 RBIs and scored 3 runs.

On the year Amburgey is hitting .370 with 30 hits, 3 home runs, 13 RBIs, .422 OBP and has scored 15 runs.
