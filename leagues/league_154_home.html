Season Over for Pannone
Monday, August 17th, 2020
The season will end prematurely for Thomas Pannone, the Buffalo starting pitcher. He suffered a torn labrum on 08/16/2020, an injury that is serious enough to knock him out of action for the rest of the year. Pannone was not available for comment.

Thus far this season, Pannone has tossed 86.1 innings in 15 starts, fanned 107 batters, walked 25 and put up a 5.63 ERA with 3 wins and 8 losses.
