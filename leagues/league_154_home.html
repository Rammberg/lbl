International League Names #1 Pitcher in April
Friday, May 1st, 2020
The International League announced the winner of the Pitcher of the Month Award for April.

It went to Ryan Yarbrough of Durham for his outstanding 3-0 record and 1.17 ERA.

Yarbrough also chalked up 3 starts, 23 innings and 19 strikeouts.
Yangervis Solarte is April's #1 Hitter in IL
Friday, May 1st, 2020
Seasoned by several years of professional experience, 32-year-old Yangervis Solarte put together a solid offensive month for Gwinnett and was crowned April's top batter in the International League.

He batted .451 with 8 home runs, 18 RBIs and 15 runs scored.
