SL Best Hitter Trophy Goes to Kirilloff
Monday, June 1st, 2020
Alex Kirilloff has a lot going for him. The Pensacola right fielder just broke into the majors this year and now the Southern League has just picked him as its top hitter for May.

In 27 games last month this outstanding rookie batted .382 with 6 home runs, 24 RBIs and 24 runs scored.

This year Kirilloff has hit at a .337 pace and produced 31 RBIs, 6 home runs and scored 26 runs.
