Los Angeles Wins Top Spot in National League West Division
Monday, September 21st, 2020
It's official! The Los Angeles Dodgers have secured the top spot in the National League West Division. Los Angeles's manager Dave Roberts was quick to give credit to his players.

"They believed in this team all year, and never gave up."

It won't be an easy road for Los Angeles as they try to compete for their 7th World Series victory. They have to hope that their success will translate to the post-season, which is certainly not always the case. This was the 36th division title for the team.
