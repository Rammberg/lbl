66ers Vega and Nuts Kerr Suspended for Fight
Monday, June 1st, 2020
Ryan Vega of the Inland Empire 66ers and Raymond Kerr of the Modesto Nuts received notification from the league commissioner's office that they had been suspended for 5 and 3 games respectively for their part in the on-field ruckus in a recent game at San Manuel Stadium.

To date, Vega has collected 27 hits for a .194 average. He has posted 1 home run, scored 12 runs and driven in 9 runs.

Kerr has taken the mound for 13 relief appearances this season, nailing down no saves while notching a 1-0 record with a 5.40 ERA. The Modesto reliever has limited opposing batters to a .258 batting average.
