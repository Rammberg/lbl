BP Proves Dangerous for Diaz
Friday, September 25th, 2020
Baltimore right fielder Yusniel Diaz was taken to a local hospital earlier this evening when he was struck in the temple by a wayward pitch during batting practice. Diaz, who managed to remain on his feet after the impact, complained of a headache and double-vision moments later. "He pretty well got his clock cleaned," said teammate Michael Baumann, who witnessed the incident. Diaz had a bruise on his cheekbone and complained of some blurred vision, but a CAT-scan revealed no evidence of a concussion or other injuries. However, manager Brandon Hyde said Diaz would be held out of the lineup for the Orioles next few games as a precaution.
