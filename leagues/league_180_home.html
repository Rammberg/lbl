Season Ends for Abena
Thursday, July 2nd, 2020
Deivi Abena had to wait several agonizing days to find out his diagnosis after suffering a torn rotator cuff on 06/28/2020. Today he received word from the Tampa Bay (DSL) doctor that he would be out the rest of the year. Team officials called it a huge setback but something they would have to overcome.

So far this season Abena has 1 save in 5 relief appearances with a 0-0 record and 0.96 ERA.
Perez Garners DSL #1 Pitcher Award
Wednesday, July 1st, 2020
Francisco Perez of Cleveland (DSL) had a tremendous June with a 4-0 mark and was chosen the Pitcher of the Month in the Dominican Rookie League.

Perez restricted opposing hitters to 25 base hits and a .191 batting average. He rang up 21 strikeouts in 37.1 innings of work, surrendered 4 walks, and crafted a 1.45 ERA.
DSL's Top Hitter Selected
Wednesday, July 1st, 2020
Anthony Garcia put together a good June to earn the Dominican Rookie League Batter of the Month prize. A virtual unknown, he made the most of his chance to prove to New York A (DSL) officials that he belongs on the big league roster.

The Yankees 1 right fielder posted a .315 batting average with 5 home runs, 21 RBIs and 22 runs scored.
