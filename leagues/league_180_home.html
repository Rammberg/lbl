Season Over for Lugo
Wednesday, June 17th, 2020
Americo Lugo had to wait several agonizing days to find out his diagnosis after suffering a ruptured ulnar collateral ligament on 06/15/2020. Today he received word from the St. Louis (DSL) doctor that he would be out the rest of the year. Team officials called it a huge setback but something they would have to overcome.
