Great Day for Fernandez -- 5 Hits
Monday, June 8th, 2020
Josue Fernandez said "it felt like taking batting practice" today at Baseball City Complex, and it showed in the box score. The Chicago N (DSL) left fielder had a fantastic 5-for-5 day against the Baltimore (DSL) Orioles 2, leading his club to victory, 7-3.

"I was seeing the ball very well and it was going just about anywhere I wanted," Fernandez said later."

Josue Fernandez singled in the 2nd, hit an RBI single in the 3rd, doubled in the 6th, singled in the 8th and hit an RBI single in the 9th.

Fernandez is hitting .565 in 2020 with 10 RBIs and no home runs.
