Injury Sidelines Alberto for Season
Tuesday, July 21st, 2020
In a news release, the Tampa Bay (DSL) Rays 1 announced that starting pitcher Alexander Alberto would miss the rest of the season due to a torn triceps. Alberto sustained the injury on 07/19/2020. He "will be sorely missed," said his manager.

This season Alberto has a 0-2 record with a 2.33 ERA.
Season-Ending Injury for Rivera
Tuesday, July 21st, 2020
Obviously disappointed, Francis Rivera confirmed today that his season is officially over. The Colorado (DSL) reliever offered mostly monosyllabic responses to reporters. "Nope," he said, when asked if he might possibly return this year. The 19-year-old Rivera suffered a torn flexor tendon in his elbow on 07/19/2020 and has been out of action since.

This season Rivera is 1-and-0 with no saves and a 0.75 ERA. He has given up 8 hits in 12 innings with 8 strikeouts.
