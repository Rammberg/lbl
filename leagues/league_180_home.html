Los Angeles (DSL) Takes Title
Thursday, September 3rd, 2020
It's party time in Los Angeles (DSL). The confetti is flying and residents are smiling.

The Los Angeles (DSL) Dodgers Robinson have reached the mountaintop, capturing the 2020 Round 3 today. They defeated the New York N (DSL) Mets 2 9-6 at Las Palmas Complex to officially wrap up the series 3-1.

Asked how it feels to be champion, shortstop Eddys Leonard was to the point.

"It feels great, like a million bucks. I'm so happy right now, not just for me but for all the guys," he said. "We put a lot of work into this and deserve to be champions."

The team is planning a victory parade and fan appreciation day later in the week. Los Angeles (DSL) finished the regular season 43-29 and claimed first place in the Boca Chica South Division.
