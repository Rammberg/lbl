Mountcastle of Norfolk Player of the Week
Monday, August 10th, 2020
His hard work and hustle paid off this week. The hard-working and hard-playing Ryan Mountcastle was rewarded with the International League Player of the Week honors.

The scrappy Norfolk Tides third baseman hit .500 while collecting 16 hits in 32 at-bats, 2 home runs and 5 RBIs.

This season Mountcastle has 10 home runs and a .314 batting average.
