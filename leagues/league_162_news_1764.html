5-for-5 Game for Kennedy
Wednesday, August 26th, 2020
Buddy Kennedy put on a hitting clinic today as the Kane County Cougars dumped the Wisconsin Timber Rattlers 7-3 at Northwestern Medicine Field.

The Kane County third baseman made Wisconsin defenders earn their pay, lashing Wisconsin pitching for 5 hits in 5 at-bats in the game. He told BNN reporters after the game that his strategy was "just hit it where they pitched it."

"Too often I try too hard to pull the ball," Kennedy said in his postgame press conference. "Today, I just went with the pitch."

Buddy Kennedy singled in the 1st, singled in the 3rd, hit a solo-shot off Chris Kohler in the 5th, singled in the 6th and singled in the 8th.

So far this season Kennedy is hitting .244 with 7 home runs.
