Round 1 Goes the Distance, Ogden Prevails
Sunday, September 20th, 2020
The Ogden Raptors and Grand Junction Rockies didn't hold back anything in the Round 1, with each team pulling out all the stops in an effort to advance to the Round 2.

Ogden won the final game by a score of 7-1 at Lindquist Field and that concluded the series, 2-1.

"Relief is the biggest thing," Ogden left fielder Joe Vranesh said when asked how he felt. "It's a grind both mentally and physically to have a long series like this, but our guys stuck with it and Grand Junction deserves credit for the hard fight."

Vranesh paced the Ogden offence and earned MVP honors. For the series, he hit .417 with a .462 on-base percentage, 1 home runs, 1 RBI and scored 2 runs.

The Ogden Raptors will play against the Billings Mustangs in the next round. Billings won their series against the Great Falls Voyagers 2 games to 0. The Round 2 schedule will be announced after the Round 1 finishes.
Mustangs Cruise to Round 2 with Sweep
Saturday, September 19th, 2020
The Billings Mustangs proved to be too much to handle for the Great Falls Voyagers in the Round 1, and a sweep was the end result.

Billings punched its ticket to the Round 2 on the strength of a 9-6 win in the deciding game, which wrapped up the series 2-0.

Billings second baseman Nick Biddison, named series MVP, was instrumental in the sweep, as he mashed Great Falls pitching to the tune of a .333 average, with a .333 on-base percentage. He had 1 home runs, drove in 4 RBI and scored 3 runs.

The Billings Mustangs don't know who they will face in the next round. They will play the winner of the Grand Junction Rockies - Ogden Raptors series. Grand Junction leads the series 1 game to 0. The Round 2 schedule will be announced after the Round 1 finishes.
