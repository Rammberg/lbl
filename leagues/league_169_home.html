Carrion Wins PIO Best Player Award
Monday, July 6th, 2020
21-year-old Julio Carrion made a few Ogden headlines during this past week as the rookie third baseman proved he could compete at the plate with his high-school idols. He captured the attention of fans, journalists and Pioneer League officials last week and today took home Player of the Week honors for his effort.

He cracked 11 hits in 26 at-bats to put up a .423 average. Carrion collected 2 home runs, 5 RBIs and scored 5 runs.

Carrion is swinging at a .410 pace on the year with 3 home runs, 9 RBIs and 8 runs scored. He has played in 11 games.
Early Season End for Rodriguez
Sunday, July 5th, 2020
A blow for Orlando Rodriguez today -- team doctors were able to determine the full extent of his recent injury. He will miss 11-12 months with a damaged elbow ligament. Rodriguez suffered the injury on 07/04/2020. The team will likely place Rodriguez on the injured list today.
