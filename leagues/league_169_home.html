Lanzilli of Orem Player of the Week
Monday, June 29th, 2020
22-year-old Chris Lanzilli made a few Orem headlines during this past week as the rookie first baseman proved he could compete at the plate with his high-school idols. He captured the attention of fans, journalists and Pioneer League officials last week and today took home Player of the Week honors for his effort.

He cracked 12 hits in 27 at-bats to put up a .444 average. Lanzilli collected no home runs, 3 RBIs and scored 10 runs.
