Dillard Wins PIO Honor
Monday, July 13th, 2020
Thomas Dillard must have thought he was dreaming. First he went on an exceptional week-long batting spree, bashing the ball at a .417 clip, then the Rocky Mountain right fielder finds out he has been chosen the league's best performer and gets the PIO Player of the Week Award.

He told BNN reporters in the postgame, "It was a great week for me. I could see the ball so well. Plus some of the balls seemed to have good eyes, too."

For the week Dillard batted .417 (10-for-24) with 3 home runs and 10 RBIs.

This year Dillard is batting .371 with 3 home runs and 11 RBIs.
