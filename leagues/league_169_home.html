Big Bat Pays Off, Loftin Player of the Week
Monday, August 24th, 2020
It was announced today by the Pioneer League that Orem shortstop Nick Loftin had received the Player of the Week Trophy for his superlative play last week.

During his week-long batting binge he reeled off a .476 batting average, 10 hits and 1 home run. Loftin also racked up 3 RBIs and scored 3 runs.

Loftin's current batting average stands at .344 and he has chalked up 2 home runs and 10 RBIs.
