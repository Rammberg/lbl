Injury Shuts Down McIntyre for Season
Monday, August 31st, 2020
"Not what I wanted to hear," said Ogden's Aiden McIntyre to reporters this afternoon, as he revealed that the injury he sustained on 08/29/2020 would force him to miss the rest of the season. "The doctor made it clear," McIntyre said. "There's no chance for me -- none at all -- to come back this year. That's the last thing I wanted to hear." McIntyre has been out of action since he suffered a stretched elbow ligament.

This season McIntyre has logged 7 relief appearances, 6.1 IP, 7 strikeouts, and a 2.84 ERA. His W-L mark is 1 win and no losses, and he has 1 save.
