Alvarez Wins AL Player of the Week Honors
Monday, June 29th, 2020
It can be difficult for a young player to step out of the shadows and assert himself in the American League, but last week Yordan Alvarez of the Houston Astros reminded everyone just why he's here as he threw up numbers that made him an easy selection for Player of the Week. Alvarez rocked opposing pitching for a .500 average (14-for-28), 6 home runs and 19 RBIs to come away with the award.

Alvarez is batting .364 this season in 73 games with 32 home runs, 112 RBIs and 79 runs scored. He has 110 hits in 302 at-bats.
