Injury Sidelines Murray for Season
Wednesday, September 2nd, 2020
Obviously disappointed, Jayden Murray confirmed today that his season is officially over. The Hudson Valley reliever offered mostly monosyllabic responses to reporters. "Nope," he said, when asked if he might possibly return this year. The 23-year-old Murray suffered a torn rotator cuff on 08/30/2020 and has been out of action since.

This year Murray has earned no saves, worked 41.2 innings, fanned 53 hitters and recorded a 1.30 ERA. He has also put up a 3-0 won-lost mark.
