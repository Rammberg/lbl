5-for-5 Game for Knizner
Thursday, June 18th, 2020
The 5th time was not the charm for Andrew Knizner today.

The catcher for the Memphis Redbirds hit the milestone today at Rio Grande Credit Union Field at Isotopes Park, but it was the Albuquerque Isotopes that salvaged a 7-6 victory. Knizner finished with a 5-for-5 line at the plate, and it still wasn't enough.

"He was not an easy out, that's for sure," Albuquerque manager Warren Schaeffer said to the press in the postgame interview. "I'm just glad we held everybody else in check and got the win."

Knizner declined to speak to reporters at length following the game, saying only that "no loss is a good one."

Andrew Knizner hit a solo-shot off Wes Parsons in the 2nd, hit a two-run single in the 3rd, hit an RBI single in the 4th, singled in the 6th and singled in the 8th.

Presently Knizner is batting .338 and has collected 7 home runs, 40 RBIs, .406 OBP and scored 22 runs.
