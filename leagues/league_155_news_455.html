AC Announces Best Batter Award
Monday, June 1st, 2020
San Antonio's Ryon Healy wowed sportswriters in May with an award-winning performance. For his standout play he was voted the American Conference Batter of the Month.

He rang up a .314 batting average with 8 home runs, 23 RBIs and 25 runs scored. He also had 8 walks and a .357 on-base percentage.

This year Healy is batting .302 with 51 hits and 12 home runs. He has scored 34 times and has batted in 37 runs.
