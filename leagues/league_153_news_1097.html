Astros Bregman Suffers Setback
Friday, July 17th, 2020
The Houston Astros will have to do without third baseman Alex Bregman even longer than expected. Team doctors say that Bregman's recovery from a strained oblique has not gone well and more time will be needed to treat it successfully. The Astros trainer indicated it would be at least 2 weeks before they would consider activating Bregman.

This season Bregman has banged out 38 hits in 148 at-bats for a .257 average with 5 home runs. He is getting on base at a .423 pace and his totals also include 21 RBIs and 35 runs scored.
