Dietrich and Burdi Suspended for Free-for-All
Sunday, July 12th, 2020
After an intense confrontation, both the Reds Derek Dietrich and the Pirates Nick Burdi were handed their punishments today. The league commissioner's office issued suspensions for an on-the-field conflict that occurred at a recent Cincinnati-Pittsburgh game. Derek Dietrich will miss 4 games, while Nick Burdi was suspended for 8 games.

"Sometimes we go too far and have to pay the consequences," Derek Dietrich commented later. "Hopefully cooler heads will prevail the next time we play."

Currently Dietrich has 8 home runs and a .257 batting average.

In 15 relief appearances this year, Burdi has thrown 14 innings, struck out 20 and collected 7 saves. His won-lost record is 0-2 with a 1.29 ERA.
