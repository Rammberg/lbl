Rodriguez Grabs NWL Reliever of the Year
Saturday, September 26th, 2020
At the young age of only 23, Hansel Rodriguez has been named the Northwest League Reliever of the Year winner for 2020.

The future appears bright for the Tri-City Dust Devils relief ace, who captured the award with 17 saves in 33 relief appearances, 31 strikeouts in 37.1 innings and a record of 1-2 to go with his 2.41 ERA.

He received 7 first place votes out of a possible 8. Jake Mulholland of the Hillsboro Hops finished second in voting with 1 first place vote, while Justin Slaten of the Spokane Indians finished third.
