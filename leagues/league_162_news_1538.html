Lopez Batters Lake County for 5 Hits
Wednesday, August 12th, 2020
It doesn't happen very often, but it did for Jesus Lopez today -- a 5-hit effort. He went on a rampage at Cooley Law School Stadium and paced the Lugnuts to a 12-11 win over the Lake County Captains.

After his dynamite batting performance, the Lansing third baseman told reporters that he "just tried to get in a good count and have a good at-bat. That's all I can control."

His philosophy paid dividends today. He slammed 5 hits in 7 tries and put his name in the record book. Jesus Lopez grounded out in the 2nd, singled in the 4th, hit a three-run home run off Zach Hart in the 5th, grounded out in the 7th, singled in the 9th, tripled in the 11th and hit an RBI single in the 13th.

This year Lopez has batted at a .333 pace with 16 hits, 2 home runs, 11 RBIs and scored 7 times.
