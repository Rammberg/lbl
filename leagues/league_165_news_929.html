Schwecke Selected Player of the Week
Monday, July 6th, 2020
All week long Trevor Schwecke was the toughest out in the Northwest League and that is the prime reason he was voted the Player of the Week.

The Vancouver shortstop rapped out 8 hits in 19 at-bats for a .421 batting average, drove in 8 runs and scored 4 times.

Schwecke is hitting at a .241 mark this season. His stats show 1 home run, 9 RBIs and 9 runs scored. He has played 16 games for the Canadians.
