Rays Suffer 13-0 Drubbing from Blue Jays
Sunday, September 6th, 2020
Chase Anderson of the Toronto Blue Jays spun his magic on the Tampa Bay Rays today. He blanked them 13-0 on 2 hits, registering 9 strikeouts and 1 walk.

"The bottom line was I just kept the ball down," Anderson said. "My breaking stuff was down. Everything was down."

Talking to reporters in the postgame interview, Toronto skipper Charlie Montoyo said the team "got solid pitching, good defense and took great team at-bats in a lot of different situations."

"Chase pitched really well but this was a solid performance by everyone," he said.

Presently Anderson has a 9-9 mark in 30 starts with a 3.97 ERA. He has given up 147 hits in 147.1 innings, struck out 124 and issued 45 walks. Opposing clubs have compiled a .257 batting average against him.
