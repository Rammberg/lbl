Oakland Athletics Ship Hendriks to New York
Tuesday, July 28th, 2020
After a week of negotiations, the Athletics and Mets have come to terms on a deal that will send 31-year-old RHP Liam Hendriks and 17-year-old minor league SS Robert Puason to New York for 25-year-old 2B Luis Guillorme.


This season Hendriks has worked 35.2 innings in 35 relief appearances and picked up 21 saves. He has dished out 34 strikeouts and walked 10 with a 1-4 mark and a 4.04 ERA.

Guillorme on the season is batting .260 with 4 home runs. His totals include 30 RBIs and 35 runs scored. He has played in 98 games.
