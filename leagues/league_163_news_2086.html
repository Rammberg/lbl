Drive Heading to Round 2
Sunday, September 13th, 2020
While it wasn't a complete mismatch, the Greenville Drive showed themselves to be the superior team in the Round 1, downing the Charleston RiverDogs 3-1.

"Nothing is ever easy in the South Atlantic League playoffs. Whether you're a favorite or an underdog, you can't take anyone lightly or you'll get burned," said Roldani Baldwin, who was named series MVP after a standout offensive performance.

Greenville won the final game by a 8-5 count at Joseph P. Riley, Jr. Park to clinch its spot in the next round.

They will hope to once again rely on Baldwin there, as he hit .636 with 0 home runs, 2 RBI 3 runs scored during the series.

The Greenville Drive will play against the Hickory Crawdads in the next round. Hickory won their series against the Kannapolis Cannon Ballers 3 games to 1. The Round 2 schedule will be announced after the Round 1 finishes.
