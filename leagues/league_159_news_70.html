Visalia-Lancaster Brawl, Rose and Parra Suspended
Thursday, April 23rd, 2020
After an intense confrontation, both the Rawhide Joey Rose and the JetHawks Frederis Parra were handed their punishments today. The league commissioner's office issued suspensions for an on-the-field conflict that occurred at a recent Visalia-Lancaster game. Joey Rose will miss 4 games, while Frederis Parra was suspended for 8 games.

"Sometimes we go too far and have to pay the consequences," Joey Rose commented later. "Hopefully cooler heads will prevail the next time we play."

This year Rose has socked 5 hits in 19 at-bats for a .263 average with no home runs. He is getting on base at a .417 pace and his totals also include 1 RBIs and 3 runs scored.
