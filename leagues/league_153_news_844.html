NL Selects Rookie Star of the Month
Wednesday, July 1st, 2020
Carter Kieboom is still a bit shy, but then, who could blame the newest National League Rookie of the Month honoree? He hasn't been around the league long enough to fully know the ropes yet, despite the fact that he's putting up veteran-like numbers.

In June the Washington hitter tagged opposing pitching for a .308 average with 37 hits, 5 home runs and 18 RBIs.

For the year Kieboom has hit at a .275 clip with 46 RBIs, 7 home runs and 57 runs scored.
