August's Best Hurler in AZL is Andrews
Tuesday, September 1st, 2020
Tanner Andrews really strutted his stuff in August with 3 wins and no losses and walked off with the Pitcher of the Month Award in the Arizona Rookie League. The Milwaukee (AZL) starter was good news for his club, but bad news for the opposition.

In 26.1 innings of work, Andrews limited opposing batters to 12 base hits and a .133 batting average. He totaled 15 strikeouts, issued 8 walks and had a 0.34 ERA.

This year Andrews has compiled a 4-0 mark with a 1.09 ERA in 6 starts.
Lugo Gets AZL Batter of the Month Trophy
Tuesday, September 1st, 2020
August's Batter of the Month has been announced by the Arizona Rookie League. This month's trophy was presented to the Chicago A (AZL) shortstop, Matthew Lugo.

He starred by hitting .379 with a .432 on-base percentage, totaled 25 hits in 66 at-bats, 2 home runs, 13 RBIs and scored 13 runs.

This season Lugo is batting .350 with 28 hits and 2 home runs. He has scored 14 times and has 15 runs batted in.
