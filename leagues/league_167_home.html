Bautista of Oakland (AZL) Crowned Best Player
Monday, August 3rd, 2020
Not every ball that came off Danny Bautista's bat last week was a line drive or a towering shot -- it just seemed that way to the teams that played the Oakland (AZL) Athletics Green. For his exploits, Bautista was named this week's Arizona Rookie League Player of the Week award.

The Oakland (AZL) right fielder put up 12 hits in 19 at-bats and no home runs while hitting .632 with 3 RBIs and 9 runs scored.

To date this season Bautista is hitting .315 with 2 home runs.
