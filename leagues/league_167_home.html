Season Ends for Floyd
Wednesday, July 8th, 2020
Taylor Floyd has to call it a season. The Milwaukee (AZL) Brewers Gold reliever learned today that the full extent his injury suffered on 07/03/2020 is worse than hoped. He will not play again this year according to club doctors, who diagnosed the injury as a damaged elbow ligament and stated that he would not be able to play for 12-13 months. The team will likely place Floyd on the injured list today.
