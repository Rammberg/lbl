Sosa Awarded April's SAL Top Bat Prize
Friday, May 1st, 2020
For a rookie just breaking in, Lenyn Sosa certainly put together a successful month. It was good enough to win the SAL Batter of the Month award for April.

The 20-year-old shortstop for the Kannapolis Cannon Ballers hit .393 with 4 home runs, 10 RBIs and 15 runs scored.
