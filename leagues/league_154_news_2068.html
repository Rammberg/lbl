Lehigh Valley Sweeps into the Round 2
Saturday, September 12th, 2020
It was a sweet sweep for the Lehigh Valley IronPigs as they eased past the Indianapolis Indians to advance to the Round 2.

Lehigh Valley won the final game by a 5-0 count and the series 3-0.

"We're just fortunate to get through it quickly and be able to rest up for the next series," said Rhys Hoskins, who was named series MVP.

For the series, Hoskins hit .333 with a .500 on-base percentage. He had 2 home runs, drove in 3 RBI and scored 2 runs.

The Lehigh Valley IronPigs don't know who they will face in the next round. They will play the winner of the Rochester Red Wings - Durham Bulls series. Durham leads the series 2 game to 1. The Round 2 schedule will be announced after the Round 1 finishes.
