Nunez Dominican Rookie League's Hottest Hitter
Monday, June 22nd, 2020
The Player of the Week has been chosen by the Dominican Rookie League. The award went to Malcom Nunez, first baseman of the St. Louis (DSL) Cardinals Blue.

In a sensational hitting performance, he wrecked league pitchers with a .536 average to win the honor. Nunez went 15-for-28 for the week, totaled 1 home run and 8 RBIs and scored 5 times.

So far on the season Nunez is hitting .324 with 2 home runs, 9 RBIs and 7 runs scored. He has 23 hits and played in 19 games.
