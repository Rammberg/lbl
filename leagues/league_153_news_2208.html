American League East Division Champions Crowned
Friday, September 25th, 2020
The Tampa Bay Rays have taken a first big step in winning the franchise's first World Series.

"We're champions of the American League East Division and now it's time to get that championship," said catcher Victor Caratini. "The fans deserve it and all the guys in this room want to bring that title home."

It is the club's 3rd division title and although they have never won the league championship, manager Kevin Cash said the club will "certainly be prepared" once playoffs begin.
