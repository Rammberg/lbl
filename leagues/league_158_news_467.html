Phillips Garners TEX #1 Pitcher Award
Monday, June 1st, 2020
Tyler Phillips took a much-deserved turn in the spotlight earlier today as he accepted the Texas League Pitcher of the Month award for May.

The 22-year-old RoughRiders starter is a relative newcomer to the league, but nonetheless racked up an impressive 5-0 won-lost record in 6 starts. This month he fanned 24 batters in 43 innings and compiled a 0.84 ERA.

On the year Phillips has worked 62.2 innings with 40 strikeouts en route to a 7-0 record and 1.15 ERA.
