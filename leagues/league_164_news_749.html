Fletcher Slams 5 Hits for State College
Tuesday, June 23rd, 2020
It's not something you read about in the sports pages every day -- a 5-for-5 day. For many, it might come around only once in a career -- and for Trejyn Fletcher, it came around today.

The 19-year-old right fielder scattered hits all over Falcon Park to pace the State College Spikes to a 7-4 victory over the Auburn Doubledays.

Auburn skipper Patrick Anderson told reporters in the press room that "Fletcher deserves a hat-tip."

"He hit some good pitches against us," he said. "In fact, according to our scouting reports, Fletcher hit several that he is not supposed to be able to hit."

Trejyn Fletcher singled in the 1st, singled in the 2nd, hit an RBI single in the 3rd, tripled in the 5th and doubled in the 8th.

For the year Fletcher is hitting .429 with 1 home run. His stats include 2 RBI and 4 runs scored.
