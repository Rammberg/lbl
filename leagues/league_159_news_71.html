JetHawks-Rawhide Fight Nets 4-Game Suspensions
Thursday, April 23rd, 2020
Tempers were flaring yesterday at The Hangar. Both the JetHawks and Rawhide were defending their turf. A few punches were thrown, but fortunately very few landed -- there was more wrestling than fighting. When the umpires sorted everything out, Lancaster's Terrin Vavra and Visalia's West Tunnell were ejected from the game.

Today the league office suspended Vavra and Tunnell for 4 games each.

Vavra is currently batting .118 with no home runs and 2 RBIs.

This year Tunnell has made 5 relief appearances, pitched 3.1 innings, and fashioned a 2-0 record with a 2.70 ERA. He has recorded no saves, struck out 4, and allowed opposing clubs to hit .308 when he is on the hill.
