Griffin Picks up TEX Monthly Pitching Honor
Friday, May 1st, 2020
Foster Griffin hated to see April turn to May.
And who could blame him?
The 24-year-old hurler piled up 3 wins and no losses for the Naturals last month and won the Texas League Pitcher of the Month award for his efforts.

Griffin stymied opposing batters on 18 base hits and a .225 batting average. He rang up 9 strikeouts in 21 innings of work, surrendered 6 walks and fashioned a sparkling 1.29 ERA.
