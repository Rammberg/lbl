Red-Hot July Lands Garcia Batting Prize in AC
Saturday, August 1st, 2020
Even though he is a newcomer to Pacific Coast League, Adolis Garcia put up some good numbers in July and earned the AC Batter of the Month award.

He was one of Nashville's offensive leaders with a .308 average in 19 games. He also racked up 7 home runs, 17 RBIs, and 14 runs scored.

Looking at his season stats, Garcia has compiled a .293 batting average and a .357 on-base percentage, while hitting 16 home runs and driving in 44 runs.
