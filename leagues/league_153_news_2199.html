Astros Sit Atop American League West Division
Wednesday, September 23rd, 2020
The Houston Astros have won their division, but have a much larger goal in mind.

"No one will look back on this season and remember the American League West Division champions. People will remember the World Series champions," said manager Dusty Baker. "That's what this is all about, but of course we needed this first to get there."

The club's division win is the 10th for the franchise and they will be seeking their 2nd World Series.
