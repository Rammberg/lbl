Mahtook: Big Hit in the International League
Monday, June 1st, 2020
Lehigh Valley IronPigs left fielder Mikie Mahtook, at the top of his game all last week, was a consensus choice today for the International League Player of the Week.

He victimized opposing pitchers for a .464 batting average on 13 hits in 28 at-bats, piling up 5 home runs, 8 RBIs and 5 runs scored.

This season Mahtook is batting .283 with 12 home runs. His totals include 26 RBIs and 22 runs scored.
