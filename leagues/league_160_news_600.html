Bad Behavior Costs Neustrom and Skoglund
Saturday, June 13th, 2020
After an intense confrontation, both the Keys Robert Neustrom and the Blue Rocks Eric Skoglund were handed their punishments today. The league commissioner's office issued suspensions for an on-the-field conflict that occurred at a recent Frederick-Wilmington game. Robert Neustrom will miss 4 games, while Eric Skoglund was suspended for 6 games.

"Sometimes we go too far and have to pay the consequences," Robert Neustrom commented later. "Hopefully cooler heads will prevail the next time we play."

This season Neustrom is batting at a .222 clip with 6 home runs and 21 RBIs.

Checking out his stats for the season, Skoglund has registered 1 win and 5 losses in 7 starts, 3.23 ERA and struck out 28 batters in 61.1 innings.
