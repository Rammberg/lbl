Great Week for Houston's Kyle Tucker
Monday, July 27th, 2020
23-year-old Kyle Tucker sent shock waves through Major League Baseball last week with an eye-popping performance that brought him the American League Player of the Week Award.

A newcomer to the league, Tucker made the establishment sit up and take notice, punishing opposing pitchers with a .426 average, 20 hits in 47 at-bats, 3 home runs and 11 RBI.

Where the Houston Astros left fielder goes from here, only the baseball gods know for sure, but Tucker certainly made his mark last week.

This year Tucker is hitting .374, produced 92 RBIs and 30 home runs, scored 114 times and gotten on base at a .438 rate.
