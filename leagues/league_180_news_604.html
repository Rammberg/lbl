Star of the Week Selected in DSL
Monday, June 15th, 2020
18-year-old Keithron Moss made a few Texas (DSL) headlines during this past week as the rookie third baseman proved he could compete at the plate with his high-school idols. He captured the attention of fans, journalists and Dominican Rookie League officials last week and today took home Player of the Week honors for his effort.

He cracked 12 hits in 23 at-bats to put up a .522 average. Moss collected no home runs, 17 RBIs and scored 4 runs.
