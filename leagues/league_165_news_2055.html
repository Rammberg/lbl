Dust Devils Win Tight North Division Race
Thursday, September 10th, 2020
The Tri-City Dust Devils are champions of the North Division, but don't plan the parade quite yet.

"It's a nice accomplishment, but we haven't done anything until we win the Round 1," said starting pitcher Omar Fernandez. "We're going to celebrate this for sure, but the real celebration is still to come for fans in Tri-City."

The club's record currently sits at 37-39. It is the team's 1st division title and they will be seeking the franchise's 1st Northwest League championship.

Team manager Vinny Lopez said he'll take some time to celebrate the division title, but then it's "back to work" for members of the team and coaching staff.
