Crawdads Win Round 1
Sunday, September 13th, 2020
The championship aspirations of the Hickory Crawdads are still alive and well, while the Kannapolis Cannon Ballers are left to wait until next year.

Hickory won the clinching game of the Round 1 6-4 over Kannapolis at Atrium Health Ballpark, to win the series 3-1.

"You feel like a little kid again, celebrating something like this," said third baseman Braden Comeaux, who paced the Hickory offense and was named series MVP. "We take everything real seriously when we're on the field, but after a series is over you can finally relax a little bit."

Comeaux claimed the MVP by racking up 7 hits for a .467 average, with a .579 on-base percentage. He had 0 home runs, 2 RBI and scored 3 runs.

The Hickory Crawdads don't know who they will face in the next round. They will play the winner of the Greenville Drive - Charleston RiverDogs series. Greenville leads the series 2 games to 1. The Round 2 schedule will be announced after the Round 1 finishes.
