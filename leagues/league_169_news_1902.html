PIO's Best Batter Announced
Tuesday, September 1st, 2020
Ashton McGee feasted on Pioneer League pitching in August and today he walked off with the Batter of the Month award.

In 62 games this season McGee has batted at a .284 pace with 66 hits and 7 home runs.
