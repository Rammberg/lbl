AL Player of the Week Awarded
Monday, September 7th, 2020
His hard work and hustle paid off this week. The hard-working and hard-playing Aaron Judge was rewarded with the American League Player of the Week honors.

The scrappy New York Yankees right fielder hit .385 while collecting 10 hits in 26 at-bats, 5 home runs and 12 RBIs.

During the current season Judge is batting at a .284 clip with 49 home runs and 131 RBIs. He has played in 134 games and scored 123 times.
