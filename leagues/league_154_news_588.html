League Suspends India and Mazza for Brawl
Wednesday, June 10th, 2020
Baseball officials have suspended two players for "inappropriate and aggressive conduct" in yesterday's Louisville-Pawtucket contest at Louisville Slugger Field. The Bats Jonathan India and the Red Sox Chris Mazza will have to sit out 5 and 10 games respectively for their parts in the brawl.

Thus far this season India is hitting .269 with 7 home runs, 19 RBIs and 24 runs scored. He has 49 hits in 182 at-bats and has played in 50 games.

To date this season Mazza sports a 1-0 mark with a 3.52 ERA in 4 starting assignments. He has held the opposition to a .267 batting average and has 37 strikeouts in 38.1 innings.
