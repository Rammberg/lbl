Star of the Week Honored in APP
Monday, July 20th, 2020
Alex Toral kept his eye on the ball last week and now all eyes are on him. The Greeneville Reds left fielder easily swung his way to the Appalachian League Player of the Week Award with 12 hits in 21 at-bats.

Toral hit .571 last week with 1 home run, 7 RBIs and 7 runs scored.

This season in 22 games, Toral has hit at a .405 clip with 32 hits, 3 home runs, 15 RBIs and 15 runs scored.
