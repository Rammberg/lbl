Reliever May's Best Hurler in CAL
Monday, June 1st, 2020
"The kid had it all working last month," a veteran teammate told reporters. "He keeps up those kind of numbers, we might have to start inviting him out with us after the games."

Junior Garcia put together a very solid month to take home the California League's top pitching honor in May.

The 24-year-old reliever threw like a veteran. In 13 relief appearances he raked in no saves and 17 strikeouts in 13.1 innings as he put together his 6-0 record and 4.05 ERA.

So far this season Garcia has worked 20.1 innings in 19 relief appearances with 1 saves. He has dished out 25 strikeouts and walked 10 with a 7-0 mark and a 2.66 ERA.
