Vargas and Beck Fight, Get Suspended
Wednesday, August 12th, 2020
Yesterday at Fifth Third Field Kennys Vargas and Landon Beck got into each other's space and then into each other's face. They began to grapple and fell to the ground. That's when it boiled over into a full-fledged bench-clearing tussle between the Toledo Mud Hens and Gwinnett Stripers. Reason finally prevailed as the umpires restored order. After much discussion, only the two major combatants were ejected from the game.

Today the league office notified the ballclubs that Vargas will have to sit out 5 while Beck would be given a 6-game suspension for their roles in the brawl.

Vargas is currently hitting at a .214 pace with 17 home runs, 43 RBIs and has scored 42 times.

Beck has fashioned a 5-0 mark for the season, 6.75 ERA and thrown 45.1 innings in 1 starts. Opposing clubs are batting .281 against him.
