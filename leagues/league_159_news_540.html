Week's Premier Performer Honored by CAL
Monday, June 8th, 2020
So far in his young career, Deacon Liput has kept a low profile, but after his performance at the plate last week, he attracted the notice of the rest of the California League in a big way as he was named the Player of the Week today for his efforts.

The 23-year-old Rancho Cucamonga Quakes shortstop hit at a torrid .500 clip as he collected 10 hits in 20 at-bats, no home runs, 2 RBIs and 1 run scored.

For the season Liput is hitting .297, batted in 15 runs with 1 home run and 20 runs scored.
