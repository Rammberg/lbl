GCL Top Batter Trophy Won by Pichman
Saturday, August 1st, 2020
At only 22 years of age, John Pichman could very well be having many more months like the last this in his career.

That would be great news for fans of the Philadelphia E (GCL) Phillies East, as July was one to remember for the first baseman and he has captured the Gulf Coast Rookie League Batter of the Month award.

On the month he hit .349 with 4 home runs, 15 RBIs and 15 runs scored.
