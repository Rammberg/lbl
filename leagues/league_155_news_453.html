AC Player of the Week Goes to Ramos of Nashville
Monday, June 1st, 2020
A hot-hitting week got Henry Ramos the American Conference Player of the Week trophy. He really wore out his welcome with league pitchers.

The Nashville right fielder slammed them for a .478 average, 11 hits in 23 at-bats, 2 home runs, 9 RBIs and scored 4 runs.

In the postgame press conference, Ramos shared his hitting philosophy with the BNN scribes: "Just look for the seams and hit between them... nothin' more than that."

This year Ramos has hit 7 home runs and posted a .256 batting average.
