Maeda Hears from Medical Staff
Tuesday, June 2nd, 2020
The Minnesota Twins have made it official: Kenta Maeda is headed to the injured list. The 32-year-old starting pitcher has been out since 05/29/2020 with shoulder inflammation. He is expected to miss 4-5 months.

So far this year Maeda has made 9 starts, tossed 50.1 innings, struck out 52 batters and walked 13. His record is 4-2 with a 4.11 ERA and he has limited opposing teams to a .277 batting average.
Medical Diagnosis Updated on Gibson
Tuesday, June 2nd, 2020
A blow for Kyle Gibson today -- team doctors were able to determine the full extent of his recent injury. He will miss 4-5 months with a torn meniscus. Gibson suffered the injury on 06/01/2020. The team will likely place Gibson on the injured list today.

This season Gibson has compiled a 0-6 record with a 7.71 ERA.
5-Hit Game for Ozuna
Monday, June 1st, 2020
Even as a kid, Marcell Ozuna said his coaches told him the key to hitting was making contact, and letting everything else flow from there. At Dodger Stadium today, Ozuna showed how well he learned that lesson.

The 29-year-old left fielder had 5 hits in 5 at-bats for the Atlanta Braves, helping his team knock off the Los Angeles Dodgers by a score of 10-5.

"They always said, just keep your eye on the ball, don't overswing, make contact and you never know what will happen," he said. "That's all I did today."

Marcell Ozuna singled in the 1st, singled in the 2nd, singled in the 5th, singled in the 7th and hit an RBI double in the 9th.

Currently Ozuna has 7 home runs and a .288 batting average.
