Los Angeles-Houston Trade: Barnes for Pressly
Monday, July 6th, 2020
The Los Angeles Dodgers addressed a concern today, coming to terms with the Houston Astros on a trade that will send 30-year-old C Austin Barnes from the Dodgers to the Astros in exchange for 31-year-old RHP Ryan Pressly and 23-year-old minor league RHP Jose Alberto Rivera.


Barnes is currently hitting at a .241 pace with 3 home runs, 10 RBIs and has scored 16 times.

Pressly has taken the mound for 26 relief appearances this season, nailing down 2 saves while notching a 2-1 record with a 9.43 ERA. The Los Angeles reliever has limited opposing batters to a .342 batting average.
