Cleveland Indians Ship Santana to Arizona
Tuesday, July 21st, 2020
A trade between Cleveland and Arizona was made official this morning. The Indians have sent 34-year-old 1B Carlos Santana to the Diamondbacks in exchange for 32-year-old LF David Peralta.


This year Santana is batting .185 with 53 hits, 6 home runs, 30 RBIs, 76 walks, .359 OBP and has scored 46 runs.

Currently Peralta has 12 home runs and a .268 batting average.
Chicago Trades Gonzalez for Los Angeles's Procopio
Tuesday, July 21st, 2020
Sometimes a change of scenery can be a beneficial to a baseball player. That is the hope in Chicago and Los Angeles as the teams came to an agreement today which sends 34-year-old LHP Gio Gonzalez to the Angels and 24-year-old minor league RHP Daniel Procopio to the White Sox. "Only time will tell if this is the right move for everyone," stated the Los Angeles GM.


Currently Gonzalez has a record of 7-3 with 71 strikeouts and 45 walks in 89.2 innings and a 5.12 ERA.
Seattle-Philadelphia Trade: Fraley for Medina
Tuesday, July 21st, 2020
A trade between Seattle and Philadelphia was made official this morning. The Mariners have sent 25-year-old CF Jake Fraley to the Phillies in exchange for 23-year-old minor league RHP Adonis Medina.
