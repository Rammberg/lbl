Castillo Gets 5 Hits
Tuesday, April 28th, 2020
Boston Red Sox center fielder Rusney Castillo banged out 5 hits today, but it was not enough to keep his club from succumbing to the Toronto Blue Jays, 15-12. He went 5-for-5 and raised his batting average a few points.

"If the hits keep coming," an upbeat Castillo said after the game, "the runs will too. And, more importantly, so will the wins."

Rusney Castillo singled in the 3rd, hit a solo-shot off Rafael Dolis in the 5th, singled in the 6th, hit a three-run double in the 7th and singled in the 9th.

This season Castillo is batting .500 with 6 hits, 1 home run, 4 RBIs, no walks, .500 OBP and has scored 3 runs. He has played in 5 games.
