National League Names Rookie of Month
Monday, June 1st, 2020
Carter Kieboom earned May's Rookie of the Month honors in the National League for last month's performance. The boyish Washington third baseman might be wet behind the ears, but he's proving that he has what it takes to play at this level.

Kieboom hit .286 with 2 home runs, 25 RBIs and 22 runs scored this past month on his way to winning the award.

Kieboom is currently batting .253 with 2 home runs, 28 RBIs and 32 runs scored. He has 45 hits in 178 at-bats.
May's Premier Rookie Named by AL
Monday, June 1st, 2020
Though he's only 25, Jose Urquidy really put together a spectacular May to capture the American League Rookie of the Month honor.

The Houston starter spun 4 wins and no losses as he posted a 3.44 ERA and 37 strikeouts in 34 innings.

So far in 8 appearances this season, Urquidy has recorded 5 wins and sustained no losses. He has pitched 43 innings, yielded 43 hits, fanned 46 and has a 3.35 ERA.
