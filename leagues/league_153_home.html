Miami-Cleveland Trade: Aguilar for Monasterio
Monday, July 27th, 2020
A trade between Miami and Cleveland was made official this morning. The Marlins have sent 30-year-old 1B Jesus Aguilar and 23-year-old minor league 1B Lewin Diaz to the Indians in exchange for 23-year-old minor league 2B Andruw Monasterio and 20-year-old minor league 1B Jeikol Contreras.


Aguilar is batting .265 this year with 5 home runs, 16 RBIs and 19 runs scored. He has 35 hits in 132 at-bats.
Tampa Bay Rays Ship Choi to Texas
Monday, July 27th, 2020
Sometimes a change of scenery can be a beneficial to a baseball player. That is the hope in Tampa Bay and Texas as the teams came to an agreement today which sends 29-year-old 1B Ji-man Choi to the Rangers and 26-year-old RHP Jose Leclerc and 17-year-old minor league SS Maximo Acosta to the Rays. "Only time will tell if this is the right move for everyone," stated the Texas GM.


Thus far this year Choi is batting .255 with 8 home runs, 39 RBIs and 43 runs scored.

This year Leclerc is 3-and-6 with 7 saves and a 14.37 ERA. He has given up 61 hits in 41.1 innings with 41 strikeouts.
Chicago Trades Encarnacion for Boston's Davis
Monday, July 27th, 2020
A trade between Chicago and Boston was made official this morning. The White Sox have sent 37-year-old 1B Edwin Encarnacion to the Red Sox in exchange for 19-year-old minor league 2B Matthew Lugo and 23-year-old minor league 1B Joe Davis.


This season Encarnacion has hit at a .294 clip with 67 RBIs, 30 home runs and 84 runs scored.
