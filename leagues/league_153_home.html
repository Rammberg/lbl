Kansas City Royals Ship Rosenthal to Boston
Friday, July 3rd, 2020
Everybody is overjoyed with a trade consummated today by the Kansas City Royals and the Boston Red Sox. In the deal Kansas City got 19-year-old minor league 2B Gregori Custodio and 24-year-old minor league RHP Kutter Crawford from Boston and dealt 30-year-old RHP Trevor Rosenthal and 24-year-old minor league C Chris Hudgins to the Red Sox. The general managers that made the swap are happy and the players are happy, too. One of the GMs commented, "This was a good swap for all concerned."


Rosenthal has taken the mound for 42 relief appearances this season, nailing down 2 saves while notching a 4-2 record with a 8.41 ERA. The Boston reliever has limited opposing batters to a .291 batting average.
Season Ends for Vincent
Friday, July 3rd, 2020
Nick Vincent had hoped for the best, but expected the worst. Today, he learned from the San Francisco medical staff that he would miss the rest of the season. The San Francisco reliever suffered a torn flexor tendon in his elbow in an earlier ballgame. He admitted to being discouraged, but said he plans to come back strong next year.

Taking a look at this year, Nick Vincent has made 13 relief appearances, recorded no saves and compiled a 1-1 won-lost mark and a 6.75 ERA. In pitching 16 innings, he has totaled 10 strikeouts and 3 walks.
Seattle-Cincinnati Trade: Magill for Tamares
Thursday, July 2nd, 2020
Seattle and Cincinnati made a trade today. In this move, the Mariners shipped 30-year-old RHP Matt Magill to the Reds for 23-year-old minor league RHP Michael Byrne and 18-year-old minor league CF Junior Tamares. The Seattle GM told reporters, "I think this trade is beneficial to everyone involved, including the players."


Magill has taken the mound for 39 relief appearances this season, nailing down 1 save while notching a 3-0 record with a 3.63 ERA. The Cincinnati reliever has limited opposing batters to a .266 batting average.
