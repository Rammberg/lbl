Reynolds 5-for-8 Against Cubs
Thursday, May 21st, 2020
It doesn't happen very often, but it did for Bryan Reynolds today -- a 5-hit day. He went on a rampage and sparkled on the diamond at PNC Park, but the performance lost a little luster when the Pirates were downed 27-26 by the Chicago Cubs.

After his glittering performance, the Pittsburgh left fielder told the press that he "just tried to get myself in a good count and have a good at-bat."

His batting theory paid dividends today as he slammed 5 hits in 8 tries and put his name in the record book.

"I just wish we had added a win in the record book today, too," Reynolds said.

Bryan Reynolds grounded out in the 1st, singled in the 3rd, grounded out in the 4th, hit a two-run single in the 5th, struck out in the 7th, singled in the 8th, hit an RBI double in the 9th, singled in the 10th and walked in a run in the 10th.

Presently Reynolds is batting .327 with 52 hits and 2 home runs. He has knocked in 28 runs and scored 33 times.

Bell Blisters Chicago
Thursday, May 21st, 2020
Josh Bell did all he could with his bat, but it just wasn't enough for the Pittsburgh Pirates to pull out a victory.

The 27-year-old first baseman rapped out 5 hits in the game, but the Pirates could not overcome the Chicago Cubs. Pittsburgh lost the game 27-26 today at PNC Park.

Martinez Rips Boston for 5 Hits
Wednesday, May 20th, 2020
Jose Martinez had an answer for everything the Boston Red Sox threw at him today at Fenway Park. The Tampa Bay first baseman went 5-for-7 in the game to put his name in the record book and put the Rays on top, 35-9.

Summing up his batting philosophy to BNN writers after the game, Martinez said, "I just see the ball and I hit it."

Jose Martinez struck out in the 2nd, hit a two-run home run off Josh Taylor in the 4th, hit an RBI single in the 5th, hit a two-run single in the 5th, flied out in the 6th, doubled in the 8th, walked in the 8th and hit a solo-shot off Christian Vazquez in the 9th.

This season Martinez is batting .302 with 22 RBIs, 22 runs scored and 6 home runs.
