NL's Top Player Named
Monday, June 8th, 2020
It can be difficult for a young player to step out of the shadows and assert himself in the National League, but last week Pete Alonso of the New York Mets reminded everyone just why he's here as he threw up numbers that made him an easy selection for Player of the Week. Alonso rocked opposing pitching for a .385 average (15-for-39), 6 home runs and 23 RBIs to come away with the award.

So far this season Alonso is hitting .298 with 14 home runs, 45 RBIs and 39 runs scored. He has 61 hits in 205 at-bats and a .405 OBP.
