NL Selects Rookie Star of the Month
Wednesday, July 1st, 2020
Carter Kieboom is still a bit shy, but then, who could blame the newest National League Rookie of the Month honoree? He hasn't been around the league long enough to fully know the ropes yet, despite the fact that he's putting up veteran-like numbers.

In June the Washington hitter tagged opposing pitching for a .308 average with 37 hits, 5 home runs and 18 RBIs.

For the year Kieboom has hit at a .275 clip with 46 RBIs, 7 home runs and 57 runs scored.
American League Names Rookie of Month
Wednesday, July 1st, 2020
Youngster Austin Hays emerged as the latest American League Rookie of the Month today. The young man from Baltimore deserved the honor, batting .274 with 11 home runs and 23 RBI. Is the month of June a harbinger of things to come or just a fluke? We'll find out someday, but for now, we'll just let Hays enjoy the limelight.

For the season Hays is batting .288 with 86 hits and 19 home runs. He has scored 45 times and batted in 47.
Bradley Earns NL's Best Pitcher Honor
Wednesday, July 1st, 2020
Archie Bradley is at the zenith of his career and today he was selected as the National League Pitcher of the Month for June.

He made 14 relief appearances for Arizona last month, throwing 14.2 innings and collecting 9 saves, a 2-0 record, 17 strikeouts and a 2.45 ERA.

Bradley has 30 relief appearances this year with 20 saves. Bradley has also compiled a 3-0 won-lost mark with a 1.44 ERA. In 31.1 innings he has fanned 41.
