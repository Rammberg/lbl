NL Awards Player of the Week
Monday, July 20th, 2020
At the age of 27, Mookie Betts might have some potential he hasn't realized yet -- if his success at the plate last week is any indication. The Los Angeles right fielder put up some exceptional numbers the past seven days with a .483 batting average. For his efforts Betts collected the National League Player of the Week Award.

He racked up 14 hits in 29 at-bats, 1 home run and 6 RBIs last week in 8 games.

This year Betts has hit at a .335 pace and produced 86 RBIs, 22 home runs and scored 89 runs.
