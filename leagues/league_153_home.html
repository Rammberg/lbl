Atlanta Loses Minter for 4 Months
Thursday, June 18th, 2020
A blow for A.J. Minter today -- team doctors were able to determine the full extent of his recent injury. He will miss 4 months with shoulder inflammation. Minter suffered the injury on 06/14/2020. The team will likely place Minter on the injured list today.

In 31 relief appearances in 2020, Minter has nailed down the victory 1 times for his club. In 32 innings he has fanned 36 batters and walked 25. His won-lost record is 3-0 with a 8.16 ERA.
Bregman Receives Injury Update
Wednesday, June 17th, 2020
A blow for Alex Bregman today -- team doctors were able to determine the full extent of his recent injury. He will miss 5 weeks with a strained oblique. Bregman suffered the injury on 06/12/2020. The team will likely place Bregman on the injured list today.

This year Bregman has socked 38 hits in 148 at-bats for a .257 average with 5 home runs. He is getting on base at a .423 pace and his totals also include 21 RBIs and 35 runs scored.
Season Ends for Hamels
Wednesday, June 17th, 2020
Cole Hamels has to call it a season. The Atlanta Braves starting pitcher learned today that the full extent his injury suffered on 06/15/2020 is worse than hoped. He will not play again this year according to club doctors, who diagnosed the injury as a torn labrum and stated that he would not be able to play for 7-8 months. The team will likely place Hamels on the injured list today.

This season Hamels has a 6-1 record with a 2.92 ERA in 11 starts.
