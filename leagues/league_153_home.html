Season Ends for Schlitter
Monday, August 24th, 2020
The Oakland Athletics have made it official: Brian Schlitter is headed to the injured list. The 34-year-old reliever has been out since 08/22/2020 with a torn back muscle. He is expected to miss 4 months.

This season Schlitter has logged no saves. He has thrown 39.2 innings, dished out 19 strikeouts and walked 34 while putting up a 2-2 mark and a 9.53 ERA.
