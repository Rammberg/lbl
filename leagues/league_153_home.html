Okert Hears from Medical Staff
Wednesday, May 27th, 2020
The Philadelphia Phillies have made it official: Steven Okert is headed to the injured list. The 28-year-old reliever has been out since 05/25/2020 with a torn meniscus. He is expected to miss 4-5 months.

Okert's record this season is 1-and-0 with no saves and a 6.06 ERA. He has given up 18 hits in 16.1 innings with 13 strikeouts.
No Comeback This Season for Davies
Wednesday, May 27th, 2020
After several days of waiting for test results, bad news was delivered to Zach Davies today. The San Diego starting pitcher will be out the rest of the year due to a torn flexor tendon in his elbow he suffered on 05/26/2020.

This year Davies has compiled a 2-2 record with a 3.64 ERA in 8 starts.
Latest Injury News for Sanchez
Wednesday, May 27th, 2020
A blow for Gary Sanchez today -- team doctors were able to determine the full extent of his recent injury. He will miss 6 weeks with a broken collarbone. Sanchez suffered the injury on 05/24/2020. The team will likely place Sanchez on the injured list today.

For the year Sanchez has hit .168 with 4 home runs, 13 RBIs and scored 16 runs.
