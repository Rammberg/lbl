Injury Brings Season to End for Hader
Monday, September 7th, 2020
The news was not unexpected, but that didn't make it easier to accept for the Milwaukee Brewers: closer Josh Hader is out for the year. Hader suffered a bone spur in his elbow on 09/05/2020. The team knew that the chances of seeing him return were slim to none. But today it was made official.

Hader has 24 saves with a 2-5 season record and a 6.18 ERA. He also has 62 relief appearances, thrown 62.2 innings and struck out 91.
