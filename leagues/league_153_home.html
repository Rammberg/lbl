Cardinals, Wainwright Buzz Pirates, 2-0
Tuesday, May 12th, 2020
Adam Wainwright of the St. Louis Cardinals was on fire against the Pittsburgh Pirates at PNC Park today. He shut them out 2-0 on 5 hits, collecting 5 strikeouts and allowing 1 walk.

"The first two pitches I'm looking for an out, a ground ball or a fly ball," Wainwright said later. "I'm trying to get quick outs. I've never been a big believer in trying to strike a guy out from the very first pitch. It's too easy to end up behind in the count doing that."

On his stats sheet for the year Wainwright shows a 2-2 record, 6 starts, 32.2 innings, 24 strikeouts and a 3.86 ERA.

San Francisco Southpaw Tosses 8-0 Shutout
Tuesday, May 12th, 2020
Tyler Anderson of the San Francisco Giants spun his magic on the Colorado Rockies today. He blanked them 8-0 on 3 hits, registering 12 strikeouts and 2 walks.

"The bottom line was I just kept the ball down," Anderson said. "My breaking stuff was down. Everything was down."

Talking to reporters in the postgame interview, San Francisco skipper Gabe Kapler said the team "got solid pitching, good defense and took great team at-bats in a lot of different situations."

"Tyler pitched really well but this was a solid performance by everyone," he said.

In season stats Anderson shows a 2-1 record in 5 games, 37.2 innings, 44 strikeouts and a 2.15 ERA.

Doolittle Signs for 2 years
Tuesday, May 12th, 2020
Flanked by team officials and his agent, Sean Doolittle announced that he'd agreed to terms with the Nationals earlier today.

Officials declined to specify a salary publicly, but in private say the figure is $6,900,000 per year.

Doolittle has taken the hill for 12 relief appearances this season, nailing down 1 save while notching a 2-1 record with a 4.91 ERA. The Washington reliever has limited opposing batters to a .275 batting average.
