James Electric in 22-0 Houston Win
Wednesday, May 6th, 2020
The Houston Astros rolled to an easy 22-0 win over the Kansas City Royals at Kauffman Stadium today. Leading the way to victory was starter Josh James, who shackled the Kansas City Royals and held them scoreless on a 5-hitter.

"This is what every pitcher wants to do when they go out there," James said in his postgame press conference.

So far this season James has 2 wins and 2 losses and a 4.50 ERA.

Bad News For DeSclafani and Cincinnati
Wednesday, May 6th, 2020
Just when it looked like Anthony DeSclafani would soon return to the Cincinnati Reds, the reliever suffered yet another setback in his recovery from a sprained ankle. According to his doctors, treatment was progressing well until yesterday, when DeSclafani experienced quite a bit of pain during a workout. Further examination revealed he had caused more damage and would now be disabled for at least 3 weeks.

DeSclafani's record up to now is 0-and-0 with no saves and a 5.87 ERA. He has given up 5 hits in 7.2 innings with 13 strikeouts.
