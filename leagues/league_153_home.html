Arizona Southpaw Tosses 2-0 Shutout
Friday, May 15th, 2020
It wasn't easy but the Arizona Diamondbacks made it look that way.

Starter Madison Bumgarner shut out the Washington Nationals despite having what he later called "sub-par" stuff, and the Arizona Diamondbacks cruised to victory, 2-0. Bumgarner allowed only 6 hits and rang up 5 strikeouts but insisted later that he did not deserve primary credit for the win.

"It didn't feel quite right today, but I got a lot of help to get through," he said. "I trusted my catcher and the guys behind me bailed me out more than once when I screwed up."

This year Bumgarner has made 6 starts, thrown 36.2 innings, fanned 29 batters and walked 14. His won-lost record is 2-2 with a 3.68 ERA.

Will Marlins Trade Cervelli?
Wednesday, May 13th, 2020
"No comment," was the word from Miami Marlins catcher Francisco Cervelli when asked about the trade rumors swirling around baseball circles.

The Marlins have been hush on the topic of a possible trade, but several unnamed sources have mentioned scenarios involving Cervelli being traded to a few different teams.
Cardinals, Wainwright Buzz Pirates, 2-0
Tuesday, May 12th, 2020
Adam Wainwright of the St. Louis Cardinals was on fire against the Pittsburgh Pirates at PNC Park today. He shut them out 2-0 on 5 hits, collecting 5 strikeouts and allowing 1 walk.

"The first two pitches I'm looking for an out, a ground ball or a fly ball," Wainwright said later. "I'm trying to get quick outs. I've never been a big believer in trying to strike a guy out from the very first pitch. It's too easy to end up behind in the count doing that."

On his stats sheet for the year Wainwright shows a 2-2 record, 6 starts, 32.2 innings, 24 strikeouts and a 3.86 ERA.
