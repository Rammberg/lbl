Moustakas Wins NL Player of the Week Prize
Monday, May 18th, 2020
At the age of 31, Mike Moustakas might have some potential he hasn't realized yet -- if his success at the plate last week is any indication. The Cincinnati second baseman put up some exceptional numbers the past seven days with a .361 batting average. For his efforts Moustakas collected the National League Player of the Week Award.

He racked up 13 hits in 36 at-bats, 6 home runs and 23 RBIs last week in 9 games.

So far this year Moustakas is hitting .325 with 13 home runs.
