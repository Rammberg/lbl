Hits Galore: Grichuk Drills 5 Hits
Thursday, June 18th, 2020
Today at Rogers Centre, the Tampa Bay Rays topped the Toronto Blue Jays in spite of a standout batting performance by center fielder Randal Grichuk.

Grichuk had a very impressive day at the plate, spanking Tampa Bay pitching for 5 base hits, but couldn't do enough for a win. Tampa Bay whipped Toronto by the score of 10-6.

Randal Grichuk hit an RBI double in the 1st, hit a three-run double in the 3rd, singled in the 5th, hit an RBI single in the 7th and singled in the 9th.

For the season Grichuk has produced 64 hits in 235 at-bats for a .272 average with 17 home runs and gotten on base at a .344 clip.
