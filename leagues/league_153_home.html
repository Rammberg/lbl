Atlanta Punchless in 14-0 Loss to Marlins
Friday, May 8th, 2020
Pablo Lopez of the Miami Marlins spun his magic on the Atlanta Braves today. He blanked them 14-0 on 4 hits, registering 4 strikeouts and 1 walk.

"The bottom line was I just kept the ball down," Lopez said. "My breaking stuff was down. Everything was down."

Talking to reporters in the postgame interview, Miami skipper Don Mattingly said the team "got solid pitching, good defense and took great team at-bats in a lot of different situations."

"Pablo pitched really well but this was a solid performance by everyone," he said.

Lopez has fashioned a 4-1 mark for the season, 0.75 ERA and thrown 36 innings in 5 starts. Opposing clubs are batting .172 against him.

Tigers Torpedoed by Carrasco in 3-0 Loss to Cleveland
Friday, May 8th, 2020
Carlos Carrasco of the Cleveland Indians was masterful today, pitching a 3-hit shutout, 3-0, against the Detroit Tigers.

By going the distance, he was at the same time able to give his bullpen a rest, a fact appreciated by Cleveland manager Terry Francona.

"It's nice to have someone who can do that from time to time," he said. "He had command of every ball and every pitch. Great location, too."

Carrasco had 13 strikeouts and no walks in the outing.

To date this season Carrasco has compiled a 5-0 record with a 1.04 ERA in 5 starts.

Chicago Loses Brothers for Season
Friday, May 8th, 2020
A blow for Rex Brothers today -- team doctors were able to determine the full extent of his recent injury. He will miss 13-14 months with a torn rotator cuff. Brothers suffered the injury on 05/05/2020. The team will likely place Brothers on the injured list today.

This season Brothers has chalked up a 7.71 ERA while ringing up no saves with no wins and no losses. He has made 17 relief appearances, pitching 11.2 innings, giving up 16 hits, and holding opponents to a .381 batting average. Brothers has also recorded 11 strikeouts and allowed 18 walks.
