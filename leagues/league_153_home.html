Miami Trades Villar for Cleveland's Clement
Friday, July 10th, 2020
The Miami Marlins have finalized a deal with the Cleveland Indians for the services of 24-year-old minor league 2B Ernie Clement and 21-year-old minor league 2B Jose Fermin. In exchange, the Indians will receive 29-year-old minor league 2B Jonathan Villar and $3,340,000 in cash.


Currently Villar has 1 home run and a .257 batting average.
Minnesota-St. Louis Trade: Cruz for Kim
Friday, July 10th, 2020
Signed ... sealed ... and delivered.

Minnesota has worked out terms of a deal to send 40-year-old LF Nelson Cruz to St. Louis for 32-year-old LHP Kwang-hyun Kim. Both teams expect to introduce their new players at press conferences later in the week.


So far this season, Cruz has recorded a .269 batting average with 83 hits, 16 home runs, 56 RBIs, 57 runs scored and a .350 OBP.

He has made 43 relief appearances this year with 2 saves. Kim has achieved a 2-4 record while holding opposing hitters to a .256 batting average and posting a 7.07 ERA. He has tossed 42 innings, given up 40 base hits, fanned 39 and issued 17 walks.
Sano Catches up on Yard Work -- Slams 4 Homers
Thursday, July 9th, 2020
Minnesota Twins first baseman Miguel Sano totally decimated the Toronto Blue Jays today, finishing with 4 home runs in a 17-4 Minnesota win.

The Blue Jays had no answer for Sano, who finished the day 4-for-6 with 6 RBIs and 4 runs scored.

"What can I say? My guys could've shot the ball out of a rifle and he'd probably hit them out anyway," said Toronto skipper Charlie Montoyo. "There's nothing you can do when a guy gets in that kind of zone, you just hope he cools down some before you have to face him again."

When asked about his record performance, Sano responded, "After the first two I just tried to do everything the same way and it worked."

Miguel Sano struck out in the 1st, hit a solo-shot off Shun Yamaguchi in the 4th, hit a two-run home run off Dany Jimenez in the 5th, struck out in the 5th, hit a solo-shot off Rowdy Tellez in the 7th and hit a two-run home run off Mitch Walding in the 8th.

For the year Sano is batting .232 with 20 home runs, 66 RBIs and 57 runs scored.
