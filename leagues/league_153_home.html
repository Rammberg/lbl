Injury Brings Season to End for Nova
Monday, June 22nd, 2020
The news was not unexpected, but that didn't make it easier to accept for the Tampa Bay Rays: reliever Ivan Nova is out for the year. Nova suffered a partially torn ulnar collateral ligament on 06/20/2020. The team knew that the chances of seeing him return were slim to none. But today it was made official.

This season Nova has nailed down no saves with 37 strikeouts in 44.1 innings. He is credited with 1 win and 1 loss and has a 3.65 earned run average.
