Monday, August 31st, 2020

Orioles Out of Time, Out of Playoffs
Monday, August 31st, 2020
Not even a late-season surge can save the Baltimore Orioles playoff hopes this season. The team was mathematically eliminated from postseason contention today.

The Orioles have missed the playoffs 135 times in their 145 years as a Major League Baseball franchise. This playoff miss is the club's 4th in a row, with their last postseason appearance coming in 2015. In the last 10 seasons, the Orioles have missed the playoffs 8 times. Baltimore has won the World Series 3 times in their history.
