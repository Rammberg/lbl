More Injury Problems For Atlanta's Corbin Clouse
Wednesday, August 19th, 2020
Yesterday Atlanta reliever Corbin Clouse sustained a setback in his recovery from a sore wrist. The Braves medical staff told him it would be at least 4 days before he will be able to return to action. Clouse told reporters, "The healing process has gone extremely well. This is just a little hiccup and a little setback." Apparently Clouse worked a little too hard in a recent workout and caused more damage. His doctors have advised him to do only light exercise and give the injury time to heal.

This season Clouse is 0-and-0 with no saves and a 2.79 ERA. He has given up 7 hits in 9.2 innings with 8 strikeouts.
