NL Choice for Batter of the Month is Acuna Jr.
Wednesday, July 1st, 2020
Atlanta Braves fans may just have witnessed a star player blossoming before their eyes in June.

Ronald Acuna Jr. put up a remarkable month at the age of 22 and has been named the National League Batter of the Month.

For the month, Acuna Jr. hit .381 with 10 home runs, 29 RBIs and 41 runs scored.

So far this season Acuna Jr. is batting .338 with 22 home runs, 67 RBIs and 76 runs scored. He has 102 hits in 302 at-bats, 57 walks and a .442 OBP. Acuna Jr. has played in 83 games.
