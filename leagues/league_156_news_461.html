Fisher Cats Murphy Chosen May's Best in EL
Monday, June 1st, 2020
In May, Patrick Murphy tore through the Eastern League like a seasoned veteran -- quite impressive for a player only 24 years old.

The young New Hampshire starter posted a 4-1 mark and an ERA of 1.47 to earn the Pitcher of the Month award. In 43 innings he collected 36 strikeouts while limiting the opposition to a .217 average.

Checking out his stats for the season, Murphy has racked up 5 wins and 2 losses in 9 starts, 2.10 ERA and struck out 53 batters in 60 innings.
