Torres Announces Retirement

Carlos Torres made it official today: This season will be the last for the Nashville reliever.

Torres owns a 30-31 won-lost record with a 4.09 ERA for his career. In 362 games he has recorded 5 saves and the 37-year-old has 447 strikeouts in 506.2 innings of work.
