Rockies Pitching Ravaged by Bellinger
Friday, June 5th, 2020
Getting 5 hits in a game has always been memorable feat in baseball. In the Los Angeles's 20-1 win over Colorado today, center fielder Cody Bellinger was red-hot, scorching them for hit after hit at Dodger Stadium.

"To hit the ball consistently well, you have to relax," Bellinger said afterward. "I was relaxed for the first four hits, but I have to admit, I got a little tight when I realized I had a chance for five. On the last at-bat, I was ready to swing at anything close."

Cody Bellinger singled in the 1st, singled in the 2nd, hit a three-run home run off Scott Oberg in the 3rd, hit a two-run single in the 4th, walked in the 5th and singled in the 7th.

Thus far this year Bellinger has hit .330 with 14 home runs and 51 RBIs.
