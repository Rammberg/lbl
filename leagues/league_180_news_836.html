Nationals Chill Bell's Hot Streak
Monday, June 29th, 2020
Lady Luck was not on the side of George Bell or his teammates today at Las Américas Complex.

Everything went the way of the Washington (DSL) Nationals, who not only came away with the 4-3 victory over San Francisco (DSL), but also held Bell hitless in 2 at-bats to put an end to his 24-game hitting streak.

"It was a good run," Bell said afterward. "It would be much easier to take right now if we had won."

So far this season Bell is batting .365 with no home runs, 11 RBIs and 9 runs scored. He has 38 hits in 104 at-bats, 6 walks and a .400 OBP. Bell has played in 26 games.
