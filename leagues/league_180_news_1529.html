Hitting Streak Still Alive for Andujar
Tuesday, August 11th, 2020
Luis Andujar of the St. Louis (DSL) Cardinals Red extended his hitting streak to 20 straight games with 1 hit in today's 4-2 triumph over the Atlanta (DSL) Braves at Las Américas Complex.

"Luis has been making it easy for me to pencil his name in the lineup every day," Cardinals Red manager Estuar Ruiz joked after the game.
Luis Andujar was hit by a pitch in the 1st, grounded out in the 2nd, singled in the 5th and flied out in the 7th.

This season Andujar has delivered 74 hits in 236 at-bats for a .314 average with 3 home runs. He is getting on base at a .378 clip and his totals also include 23 RBIs and 40 runs scored. This year Andujar has played in 61 games.
