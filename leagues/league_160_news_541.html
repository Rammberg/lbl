Player of the Week: Fayetteville's Brewer
Monday, June 8th, 2020
His hard work and hustle paid off this week. The hard-working and hard-playing Jordan Brewer was rewarded with the Carolina League Player of the Week honors.

The scrappy Fayetteville Woodpeckers right fielder hit .500 while collecting 11 hits in 22 at-bats, 2 home runs and 5 RBIs.

This year Brewer is batting .229 with 35 hits, 5 home runs, 14 RBIs, 16 walks, .306 OBP and has scored 21 runs.
