5-Hit Game for Ozuna
Monday, June 1st, 2020
Even as a kid, Marcell Ozuna said his coaches told him the key to hitting was making contact, and letting everything else flow from there. At Dodger Stadium today, Ozuna showed how well he learned that lesson.

The 29-year-old left fielder had 5 hits in 5 at-bats for the Atlanta Braves, helping his team knock off the Los Angeles Dodgers by a score of 10-5.

"They always said, just keep your eye on the ball, don't overswing, make contact and you never know what will happen," he said. "That's all I did today."

Marcell Ozuna singled in the 1st, singled in the 2nd, singled in the 5th, singled in the 7th and hit an RBI double in the 9th.

Currently Ozuna has 7 home runs and a .288 batting average.
