Detroit Tigers Ship Godley to Seattle
Wednesday, July 29th, 2020
Detroit and Seattle have agreed in principle to a trade. The Tigers will send 30-year-old RHP Zack Godley to the Mariners for 19-year-old minor league SS Miguel Perez and 23-year-old minor league LF Jhonny Santos. The deal is expected to be finalized shortly and the clubs will be introducing their new players to the press when the paperwork is complete.


Godley has taken the mound for 20 relief appearances this season, nailing down no saves while notching a 3-4 record with a 4.17 ERA. The Seattle reliever has limited opposing batters to a .246 batting average.
