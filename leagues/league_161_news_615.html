Cespedes Wins FSL Best Player Award
Monday, June 15th, 2020
This week he let his bat do the talking. That's why Ricardo Cespedes of the Clearwater Threshers was named the Florida State League Player of the Week.

For the week the 22-year-old center fielder batted .462 (12-for-26) with no home runs, 4 runs and 5 RBIs.

This year Cespedes is hitting at a .293 pace with no home runs and 14 RBIs. He has played in 38 games and scored 15 times.
