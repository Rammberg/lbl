Lopes Sensational, 5-for-6
Thursday, June 11th, 2020
Fans at Riverfront Stadium got to see not only a home-team victory today, but also one of the most impressive feats of hitting of the season.

The Wichita Wind Surge outplayed the El Paso Chihuahuas en route to a 5-4 win, but the standout was second baseman Christian Lopes. Lopes netted 5 hits in 6 at-bats and finished with a dynamite game line.

"Hey, we tried everything we could think of to get him out," Chihuahuas manager Edwin Rodriguez said in his postgame comments. "We threw everything but the kitchen sink against him and he hit it. Maybe we should've thrown the sink, too."

Christian Lopes grounded out in the 1st, singled in the 3rd, singled in the 5th, singled in the 7th, singled in the 9th and singled in the 11th.

Thus far this season Lopes is hitting .274 with 2 home runs, 22 RBIs and 29 runs scored. He has 49 hits in 179 at-bats and has played in 47 games.
