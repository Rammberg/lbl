Nashville's Ibanez Gets AC Player of the Week
Monday, April 20th, 2020
At the age of 27, Andy Ibanez might have some potential he hasn't realized yet -- if his success at the plate last week is any indication. The Nashville second baseman put up some exceptional numbers the past seven days with a .529 batting average. For his efforts Ibanez collected the American Conference Player of the Week Award.

He racked up 9 hits in 17 at-bats, 2 home runs and 8 RBIs last week in 4 games.
