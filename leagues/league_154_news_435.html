Mets, Lockett Buzz RailRiders, 2-0
Sunday, May 31st, 2020
"Good pitching beats good hitting" is an old baseball adage.

Well, good pitching also beats bad hitting.

Walker Lockett of the Syracuse Mets proved the point at PNC Field today, holding the Scranton WB RailRiders to 2 hits in a complete game shutout victory.

"Lockett made us look real bad today," RailRiders manager Doug Davis said in his postgame interview. "He was outstanding. He was down in the zone with all his pitches. Everything was sinking."

Lockett had 5 strikeouts and gave up no walks as the Mets took the 2-0 win.

To date this season Lockett has compiled a 3-3 record with a 3.95 ERA in 9 starts.
