Hitting Streak Still Alive for Sanchez
Thursday, June 4th, 2020
The streak is alive and well -- at least for today.

Yolbert Sanchez did it again, bashing 1 hit in the contest at Trustmark Park today. For the last 20 games in a row, the 23-year-old shortstop has collected at least one hit. His efforts helped the Birmingham Barons beat the Mississippi Braves 7-6 today.

"I'm happy I kept the streak going," Sanchez told a standing-room-only crowd of reporters gathered in the Birmingham clubhouse. "Honestly though, I'm looking forward to going home and not thinking about this for the rest of the day."
