AZL Trophy Goes to Martinez of Chicago N (AZL)
Monday, August 17th, 2020
Pedro Martinez must have thought he was dreaming. First he went on an exceptional week-long batting spree, bashing the ball at a .476 clip, then the Chicago N (AZL) third baseman finds out he has been chosen the league's best performer and gets the AZL Player of the Week Award.

He told BNN reporters in the postgame, "It was a great week for me. I could see the ball so well. Plus some of the balls seemed to have good eyes, too."

For the week Martinez batted .476 (10-for-21) with 2 home runs and 14 RBIs.

Martinez currently has a .279 batting average with 43 hits, 3 homers, 34 RBIs, 28 runs scored and a .370 OBP.
