Orioles Win Tight South Division Race
Tuesday, September 1st, 2020
The Baltimore (GCL) Orioles may not have ever won a Round 3, but you wouldn't have guessed it from looking inside their clubhouse today.

The club celebrated its South Division championship like they've been hosting such festivities for a long time.

"Our team may not have ever won a championship, but we know how to party," said reliever Michael Fowler. "This is nothing compared to what you'll see if we can win the Round 3."

The club's 31-25 record led to the franchise's 1st division crown.
