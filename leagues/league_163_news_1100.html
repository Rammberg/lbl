League Suspends Schunk and Vilera for Brawl
Friday, July 17th, 2020
After an intense confrontation, both the Tourists Aaron Schunk and the Fireflies Jaison Vilera were handed their punishments today. The league commissioner's office issued suspensions for an on-the-field conflict that occurred at a recent Asheville-Columbia game. Aaron Schunk will miss 2 games, while Jaison Vilera was suspended for 6 games.

"Sometimes we go too far and have to pay the consequences," Aaron Schunk commented later. "Hopefully cooler heads will prevail the next time we play."

So far on the season Schunk is hitting .241 with 4 home runs, 28 RBIs and 26 runs scored. He has 60 hits and played in 62 games.

This year Vilera has 6 wins and 3 losses and a 3.09 ERA.
