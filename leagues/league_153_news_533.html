Great Stroke: Calhoun Gets 5 Hits
Sunday, June 7th, 2020
Willie Calhoun put on a hitting clinic today as the Texas Rangers dumped the Toronto Blue Jays 20-16 at Rogers Centre.

The Texas left fielder made Toronto defenders earn their pay, lashing Toronto pitching for 5 hits in 6 at-bats in the game. He told BNN reporters after the game that his strategy was "just hit it where they pitched it."

"Too often I try too hard to pull the ball," Calhoun said in his postgame press conference. "Today, I just went with the pitch."

Willie Calhoun singled in the 1st, singled in the 2nd, struck out in the 4th, singled in the 6th, doubled in the 7th, singled in the 9th and walked in a run in the 9th.

Calhoun is hitting .327 this year with 8 home runs, 34 RBIs and 31 runs scored. He has 50 hits in 153 at-bats, 18 walks and a .395 on-base percentage. Calhoun has played in 39 games.
