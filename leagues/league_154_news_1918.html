Players Suspended in Stripers-Bulls Donnybrook
Tuesday, September 1st, 2020
Baseball officials have suspended two players for "inappropriate and aggressive conduct" in yesterday's Gwinnett-Durham contest at Coolray Field. The Stripers Yonder Alonso and the Bulls Joe Ryan will have to sit out 4 and 9 games respectively for their parts in the brawl.

Alonso is hitting .300 so far this season with 96 RBIs and 80 runs scored. He has 140 hits including 31 home runs, 70 walks and .389 OBP.

So far this season Ryan has a 2-6 record, 15 starts, 69 innings, 97 strikeouts and a 6.00 ERA.
