Los Angeles (DSL) Secures Top Spot in Boca Chica South Division
Wednesday, August 12th, 2020
While it remains to be seen what the playoffs will bring for the Los Angeles (DSL) Dodgers Robinson, the club took the first step by clinching the Boca Chica South Division title.

"It's exactly where we wanted to be and where we talked about being during spring training," said third baseman Alex De Jesus. "There's not a guy in the room that doesn't believe in this team. We're all pulling in the same direction right now."

It was the team's 1st division title and they will attempt to capture the franchise's 1st Dominican Rookie League championship.

"The end goal hasn't changed - win the Round 3," said manager Cordell Hipolito. "We think we've got the team to do it."
