Toronto Trades Giles for New York's Volpe
Wednesday, July 29th, 2020
Officials for Toronto confirmed that they have traded 29-year-old RHP Ken Giles to the New York Yankees. In return, the Blue Jays will receive 19-year-old minor league 2B Anthony Volpe, 20-year-old minor league RHP Yoendrys Gomez and 24-year-old minor league RF Clark Scolamiero.


This season Giles has 16 saves. In 35 relief appearances he has hurled 36.1 innings, struck out 49 and given up 20 walks with a 1-1 record. The New York reliever has recorded a 5.70 ERA and allowed the opposition to hit .262 when he is on the hill.
