SAL Awards Best Batter Prize to Martin
Monday, September 7th, 2020
Greensboro Grasshoppers first baseman Mason Martin walked away with the South Atlantic League Player of the Week Award today after putting up some impressive numbers over the past seven days.

"The Grasshoppers fans are really behind me right now," said the 21-year-old. "That helps. They give you the confidence to go out there and do better."

Martin batted .522 (12-for-23) with 2 home runs and 12 RBIs this week on his way to the award.

Martin is batting .281 this year with 9 home runs, 40 RBIs and 30 runs scored. He has 54 hits in 192 at-bats.
