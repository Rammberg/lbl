Big Day with Bat: Blackmon 5-for-6
Saturday, June 20th, 2020
It doesn't happen very often, but it did for Charlie Blackmon today -- a 5-hit effort. He went on a rampage at Colorado Field and paced the Rockies to a 14-13 win over the San Diego Padres.

After his dynamite batting performance, the Colorado right fielder told reporters that he "just tried to get in a good count and have a good at-bat. That's all I can control."

His philosophy paid dividends today. He slammed 5 hits in 6 tries and put his name in the record book. Charlie Blackmon flied out in the 1st, hit an RBI single in the 3rd, hit a two-run double in the 5th, hit an RBI single in the 6th, singled in the 7th and singled in the 9th.

So far this season Blackmon is hitting .284 with 7 home runs.
