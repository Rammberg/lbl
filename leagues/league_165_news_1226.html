Northwest League Honors Week's #1 Player
Monday, July 27th, 2020
Eugene's Brendon Little is only 23, but last week he put up numbers that would make a seasoned veteran proud. That's why the young hurler earned the Northwest League Player of the Week award.

Little tallied 2 wins, 10 strikeouts in 12.1 innings and a 0.00 ERA for the week.

This year Little has recorded a 3-2 mark. In 6 starts he has fanned 26 batters in 36 innings with a 1.50 ERA.
