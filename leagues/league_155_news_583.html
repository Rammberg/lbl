Straw 5-for-5 for Round Rock
Tuesday, June 9th, 2020
Round Rock center fielder Myles Straw went down swinging at Greater Nevada Field today, knocking out 5 hits even as his Express fell, 4-3.

"It was like I was hitting Little League pitching," Straw said after his 5-for-5 performance against the Reno Aces. "No offense meant to Reno, but it was like I was back in little league again -- I could see the ball that well."

After the game, his manager elaborated on the center fielder's improvements, saying Straw spent the offseason trying to shorten his swing and stay inside the ball.

"That's what I'm trying to do," Straw agreed. "Be quick to the ball and let it travel a little farther so I get a better look at it."

Myles Straw singled in the 1st, singled in the 3rd, hit an RBI single in the 5th, singled in the 7th and singled in the 9th.

So far this year in 37 games Straw is hitting .311 with no home runs, 8 RBIs and 21 runs scored.
