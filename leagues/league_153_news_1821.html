Rangers Mathematically Out of Playoffs
Monday, August 31st, 2020
Many Texas Rangers fans have already turned their attention to next season, and with good reason. Even with a good chunk of the schedule still left to play, the club was mathematically eliminated from playoff contention today.

Texas last appeared in the postseason in 2012. This is their 8th consecutive year missing the playoffs, and in total they have not advanced past the regular season 56 times in their 59-year history. In the last 10 seasons, the Rangers have missed the playoffs 7 times.
