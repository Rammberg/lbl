Arizona Rookie League Announces Best Manager
Monday, September 21st, 2020
Nick Stanley stepped before the microphone today and accepted his Arizona Rookie League Manager of the Year award, thanking the whole Milwaukee (AZL) Brewers Gold organization for making the 2020 season such a success.

Stanley struggled to find the right words. "My father was a drill instructor in the military. He taught me that you have to work hard, set goals and accept responsibility for your actions. That's what the entire Milwaukee (AZL) organization did this year. My dedicated owner, coaching staff, hard-working and talented players and loyal front office all made this award possible. I thank them for it."

And what a year it was. Stanley sparked the Brewers Gold to a 38-18 record and a first in the Central Division standings.
