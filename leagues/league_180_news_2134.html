Brewers Ace DSL's Best Pitcher
Thursday, September 17th, 2020
The Milwaukee (DSL) clubhouse staff referred to him as "the kid" this season. Next year Alexander Rosario will have to have a new nickname. He earned it after winning the 2020 Dominican Rookie League Outstanding Pitcher Award.

Rosario made 11 starts this year, winning 10 and losing 1. He threw 81.2 innings, fanned 49, allowed 53 hits and 16 walks, while holding opposing teams to a .185 average as he logged an impressive 1.43 ERA.

He received 36 first place votes out of a possible 46. Hector Guance of the Washington (DSL) Nationals finished second in voting, while Petter Caty of the Cleveland (DSL) Indians 1 finished third with 5 first place votes.
