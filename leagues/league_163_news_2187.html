Greenville Drive Are Champs
Monday, September 21st, 2020
A celebratory mood filled the air as the Greenville Drive closed out the season with a 10-5 victory over the Hickory Crawdads at Fluor Field at the West End to capture the Round 2, 3-2.

Giving the Crawdads credit for a hard-fought series, Drive players said they believed throughout the postseason that their "destiny" was to be crowned South Atlantic League champions.

"We felt like this was our year all season long," said second baseman Korby Batesole, who was named series MVP. "It was our destiny to win it and we did."

It was the 1st in franchise history. During the Southern Division regular season Greenville put up a 86-54 record and finished first.

"They're the champions and they deserve it," said a dejected Hickory manager Carlos Cardoza. "We played good ball, they just played better than us."
