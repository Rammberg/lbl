Santana Earns AL Player of the Week
Monday, June 1st, 2020
At the age of 29, Danny Santana might have some potential he hasn't realized yet -- if his success at the plate last week is any indication. The Texas center fielder put up some exceptional numbers the past seven days with a .560 batting average. For his efforts Santana collected the American League Player of the Week Award.

He racked up 14 hits in 25 at-bats, 3 home runs and 5 RBIs last week in 5 games.

For the year Santana has hit .348 with 9 home runs, 32 RBIs and scored 27 runs.
