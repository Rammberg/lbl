2020 Southern Division All-Stars Stars Shine
Tuesday, June 23rd, 2020
The best of the best took to the field at SRP Park tonight, giving SAL fans a show in the annual all-star game.

The Southern Division earned bragging rights with a 7-3 win over the Northern Division.

Brady Cox was named the MVP for his efforts, as he batted 1-for-2 in the contest.

"I'm just glad we won, that's the main thing," he said. "The personal accomplishment isn't something I strive for in an all-star game."
