National League East Division Champs Crowned: Atlanta Braves
Monday, September 14th, 2020
Those who doubted them are eating crow and those who believed in them are reminding everyone of their faith. The Atlanta Braves have captured the crown as National League East Division champions.

"It's nice to wrap this up a little bit early so we don't have to sweat it out down the stretch," said right fielder Ronald Acuna Jr.. "A lot of people didn't know how we would make out this year, so it's nice to show people what our team is all about."

The club currently has a 88-61 record which is good for their 36th division title.

The team will be vying for its 4th World Series.
