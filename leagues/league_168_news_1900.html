Decker Grabs GCL Best Batter Award for August
Tuesday, September 1st, 2020
Nick Decker said he hopes everyone remembers his performance in August if he ends up struggling this year. His performance certainly was memorable, as the 20-year-old Boston (GCL) Red Sox right fielder was a step ahead all other Gulf Coast Rookie League hitters and was named Batter of the Month.

On the month, he hit .343 with 1 home run, 14 RBIs and 16 runs scored.

Thus far this year Decker has hit .315 with 5 home runs and 32 RBIs.
