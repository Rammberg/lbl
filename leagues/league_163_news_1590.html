SAL's Best Player Named
Monday, August 17th, 2020
The votes have been cast and counted and the winner of the South Atlantic League Player of the Week has been chosen. It was presented earlier today to Michael Toglia for his red-hot week of hitting.

The Asheville left fielder took home the trophy by splurging with 9 hits in 27 at-bats for a .333 batting average, driving in 13 runs and scoring 6 times.

Thus far this season Toglia is hitting .288 with 14 home runs, 51 RBIs and 51 runs scored. He has 82 hits in 285 at-bats and has played in 78 games.
