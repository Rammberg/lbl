Well, that's just what Florentino of the Kansas City (DSL) Royals 2 did against the Milwaukee (DSL) Brewers. He went to the plate 5 times and 5 times he delivered base hits, but his club still suffered a 8-7 defeat.

Omar Florentino singled in the 1st, singled in the 2nd, hit an RBI single in the 4th, singled in the 7th and hit an RBI single in the 8th.
