Hockin Picks up MID Monthly Pitching Honor
Tuesday, September 1st, 2020
The top-rated pitcher in the Midwest League this month was South Bend Cubs reliever Chad Hockin. He won the league's premier pitcher award for August by saving 13 games in 13 save opportunities.

During the period Hockin also had no wins and no losses. The South Bend relief specialist had 13 strikeouts and 2 walks in 14 innings, permitted 11 base hits and compiled a 0.64 ERA.

Looking at his pitching stats this season, Hockin has made 50 relief appearances, pitched 58 innings and fashioned a 3-3 record with a 1.24 ERA. He has recorded 25 saves, struck out 55 and allowed opposing clubs to hit .179 when he is on the hill.
