NL Star of the Week Honored
Monday, July 13th, 2020
Justin Turner of the Los Angeles Dodgers might have lost a step over the years, but the 35-year-old third baseman can still get it done. He beat out several other worthy National League hitters over this past week to claim the Player of the Week Award.

Turner posted a .500 batting average with 2 home runs and 8 RBIs last week while scoring 6 runs. For the week he was 14 for 28.

In his stats this season, Turner has posted a batting average of .313 with 102 hits in 326 at-bats, 9 home runs and 47 RBIs. He has also scored 77 runs, walked 48 times and compiled a .415 OBP.
