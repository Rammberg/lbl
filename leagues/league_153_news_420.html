Veteran Cespedes Hits Three Dingers
Thursday, May 28th, 2020
The old superstition that "bad news comes in threes" certainly came true for the Philadelphia Phillies today.

New York left fielder Yoenis Cespedes tripled up on the home runs to propel the Mets to the 13-4 win at Citizens Bank Park.

"We had our chances to win the game, but we just didn't come through," Philadelphia manager Joe Girardi said. "Cespedes hit the heck out of the ball."

In addition to the homers, Cespedes collected 3 hits in 5 at-bats, 9 RBIs and scored 3 runs.

"Easy swings get you home runs," he said after the game. "I kept telling myself not to press. They have to be nice and easy swings."

Yoenis Cespedes hit a two-run home run off Jake Arrieta in the 1st, hit a three-run home run off Jake Arrieta in the 3rd, walked in the 5th, grounded out in the 7th, hit a GRAND SLAM off Victor Arano in the 8th and struck out in the 9th.

So far this year Cespedes is batting .190 with 6 home runs, 18 RBIs and 16 runs scored.
