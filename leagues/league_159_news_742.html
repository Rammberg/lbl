Rookie Stockton Ports' shortstop Leads North Division to Win in Annual All-Star Game
Tuesday, June 23rd, 2020
California League put its best players on the field today, and the game certainly showed that.

In the annual Midseason Classic, the North Division came out over the South Division by a final score of 6-1. Leading the way for the North Division All-Stars was shortstop Jeremy Eierman.

"I go out there and do my best every game, All-Star game or not," the star said after his great show, going 1-for-1 in the contest.

Taking a look at his lifetime stats, Eierman is hitting .214 with a total of 18 home runs.
