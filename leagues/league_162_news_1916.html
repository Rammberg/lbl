Schick Has Season End
Tuesday, September 1st, 2020
Alex Schick has to call it a season. The Cedar Rapids Kernels reliever learned today that the full extent his injury suffered on 08/29/2020 is worse than hoped. He will not play again this year according to club doctors, who diagnosed the injury as a ruptured ulnar collateral ligament and stated that he would not be able to play for 11 months. The team will likely place Schick on the injured list today.
