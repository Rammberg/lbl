Mets Get No Resistance from Brewers in 10-0 Beat-Down
Monday, June 15th, 2020
Today was Michael Wacha's turn in the rotation for the New York Mets and he made the most of it, shutting out the Milwaukee Brewers on 3 hits.

The 28-year-old right-hander led the Mets to an easy 10-0 victory, allowing 1 walk and tallying 3 strikeouts.

"I hope we miss him next time," Milwaukee manager Craig Counsell said. "Wacha certainly pitched a strong game against us today. He was in a groove, throwing everything for strikes. Throwing hard. Changing speeds."

To date this season, Wacha has registered 11 starts, 5-6 won-lost mark, 4.67 ERA, 69.1 IP, 43 strikeouts and 22 walks. Opposing teams have hit .260 against him.
