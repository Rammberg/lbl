Super Week for Charleston's Pasteur!
Monday, May 11th, 2020
Isaiah Pasteur of the Charleston RiverDogs had an outstanding week and today was voted the Player of the Week by the South Atlantic League.

For the week the 23-year-old third baseman batted .429 (12-for-28) with no home runs and 4 RBIs.

Pasteur is currently batting .289 with no home runs, 15 RBIs and 14 runs scored. He has 28 hits in 97 at-bats.
