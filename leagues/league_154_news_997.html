Sanchez on Fire: 5-for-6 for Charlotte
Friday, July 10th, 2020
There aren't many who could be pro ballplayers and there are even fewer who could put up 5 hits in a day.

Yolbert Sanchez is one of those rare few, as he went 5-for-6 against the Indianapolis Indians at BB&T Stadium today. The monster effort from the 23-year-old shortstop helped the Charlotte Knights take the win, 12-3.

"We made some bad pitches and he took advantage of it," said Indians manager Brian Esposito. "That's the game, though. Don't make mistakes and you'll win ballgames. Make mistakes and, well, you saw what happened today."

Yolbert Sanchez singled in the 1st, singled in the 3rd, singled in the 4th, flied out in the 5th, hit a two-run double in the 7th and singled in the 8th.

Thus far this season in 17 games Sanchez is batting .351 with 1 home run, 6 RBIs and 18 runs scored. He has 27 hits in 77 at-bats.
