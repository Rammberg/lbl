Rodriguez Nabs AC Weekly Award
Monday, August 17th, 2020
Ronny Rodriguez punched, poked and pummeled the baseball last week and today he garnered a majority of the votes for the American Conference Player of the Week award.

The San Antonio Missions third baseman slashed out a .500 average with 8 hits in 16 at-bats, 3 home runs and 5 RBIs.

So far this year, Rodriguez is batting .213 with 10 home runs and 36 RBIs.
