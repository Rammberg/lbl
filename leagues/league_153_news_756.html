Molina Gets Number 2000
Thursday, June 25th, 2020
For Yadier Molina, it was a red-letter day. The St. Louis Cardinals catcher slapped his 2000th career hit. That his team defeated the Miami Marlins, 7-3, made it even sweeter.

His manager paid tribute to Molina after the game: "I've followed Yadier since he came into the league," he said. "Fine ballplayer. Fine work ethic. I'm not surprised by what he's achieved."

The 37-year-old Molina finished the day with 2 hits in 4 at-bats.

His numbers show Molina is carrying a .280 lifetime batting average with 158 home runs and 937 RBIs.
