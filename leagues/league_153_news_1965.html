Week's Best Player Honored by NL
Monday, September 7th, 2020
It can be difficult for a young player to step out of the shadows and assert himself in the National League, but last week Ronald Acuna Jr. of the Atlanta Braves reminded everyone just why he's here as he threw up numbers that made him an easy selection for Player of the Week. Acuna Jr. rocked opposing pitching for a .560 average (14-for-25), 4 home runs and 12 RBIs to come away with the award.

Looking at his season stats, Acuna Jr. has compiled a .352 batting average and a .460 on-base percentage, while hitting 39 home runs and driving in 120 runs.
