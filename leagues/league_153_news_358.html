Big Day for Diaz: Swats 5 Hits
Saturday, May 23rd, 2020
Isan Diaz did a number on the Nationals in the Miami 8-6 victory today, and the number was 5. The Marlins second baseman banged out 5 hits in 6 at-bats at Nationals Park to lead his club past the Washington Nationals.

Isan Diaz doubled in the 1st, flied out in the 3rd, singled in the 5th, singled in the 6th, singled in the 9th and hit a solo-shot off Daniel Hudson in the 11th.

In 32 games this season Diaz has batted at a .330 pace with 31 hits and 5 home runs.
