Bullpen Ace Wins Best AL Pitcher
Monday, June 1st, 2020
Ty Buttrey was the anchor in the Los Angeles bullpen last month and snatched up the American League Pitcher of the Month honors for May. The 27-year-old is in the prime of his career and pitching like he intends to stay around baseball for quite some time to come.

He threw 15.1 innings over 15 relief appearances and collected a 1-0 record with 20 strikeouts and 11 saves while etching his 1.76 ERA.

To this point in the season, Buttrey has 11 saves in 23 relief appearances with a 1-1 mark and 2.25 ERA. He has 24 strikeouts in 24 innings with 9 walks.
