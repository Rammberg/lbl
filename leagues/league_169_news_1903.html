Reliever Snags PIO Top Hurler Trophy
Tuesday, September 1st, 2020
So when does a fireman win an award?
When he's named Nick Brown, that's when.
The Idaho Falls reliever won the Pitcher of the Month Award honors today for August, thanks to the solid numbers he put up coming out of the bullpen.

He made 7 relief appearances and compiled 3 saves with a 2-0 mark, 7 innings, 5 strikeouts and a 0.00 ERA.

This year in 23 relief appearances, Brown has collected 10 saves while posting an ERA of 6.97 and 22 strikeouts in 20.2 innings. His totals for the year also include 3 wins and 5 losses.
