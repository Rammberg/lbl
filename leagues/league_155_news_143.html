Chisholm Garners AC Batter of the Month Award
Friday, May 1st, 2020
When Jazz Chisholm stepped up to the plate in April he did it with confidence, and today he was honored with the American Conference Batter of the Month award.

The young Wichita shortstop posted a .291 batting average with 5 home runs, 12 RBIs and scored 16 runs. Chisholm also worked pitchers for 8 walks to post a .381 on-base percentage.
