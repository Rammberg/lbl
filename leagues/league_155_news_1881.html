August's Best Starter Named in PC
Tuesday, September 1st, 2020
There's something about a young rookie winning the Pacific Conference Pitcher of the Month trophy that sends shock waves through the league. Such is the case with Logan Gilbert, a 23-year-old starter for the Tacoma Rainiers.

In August, he topped off the month with a 4-0 mark and 1.45 ERA in 7 starts, tossed 43.1 innings, fanned 48 and held opposing teams to a .170 average.

In 26 starts this year Gilbert has crafted a 11-4 record and a 3.89 ERA.
