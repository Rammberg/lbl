SAL Player of the Week Chosen
Monday, July 27th, 2020
Swinging a hot stick last week, Michael Toglia picked up the South Atlantic League Player of the Week Award today.

The 21-year-old left fielder for Asheville lashed 8 hits in 22 at-bats as he put together a .364 average with 4 home runs. Toglia also drove in 8 runs, scored 7 times and compiled a .462 OBP.

This season Toglia is batting .276 with 58 hits and 7 home runs. He has scored 33 times and has 33 runs batted in.
