Hanson Bangs 3 HR Against Reno
Tuesday, September 8th, 2020
Alen Hanson brutalized the Reno Aces pitching staff, launching three home runs in today's 9-0 victory at Cheney Stadium. The Tacoma Rainiers shortstop was red-hot, the ball just exploding off his bat all day.

"I don't try to hit homers," he said later. "I try to go out there and put a good swing on the ball, and sometimes it is going to go out of the park. I didn't try to hit the first one or the second one or the third one."

For the game, he went 3-for-4 with 5 RBIs and 3 runs scored.

"Too much Alen Hanson today," Aces manager Chris Cron said. "That's my assessment of the game. That's what our problems boiled down to."

Alen Hanson hit a solo-shot off Taylor Clarke in the 2nd, struck out in the 4th, hit a solo-shot off Edwin Jackson in the 6th and hit a three-run home run off Matt Grace in the 8th.

This year Hanson has batted at a .250 pace with 54 hits, 6 home runs, 26 RBIs and scored 30 times.
