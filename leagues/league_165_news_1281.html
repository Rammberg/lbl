Williams Dominates in 6-0 Win over North Division All-Stars
Tuesday, July 28th, 2020
All-Star Salem-Keizer Volcanoes' second baseman Jaxon Williams had a night to remember.

Not only did he take the field with the game's best players, he was also a huge factor in the 6-0 South Division victory over the North Division All-Stars.

"We had a meeting before the game, and I was struck by the talent in the room. Old stars, young stars, it's a game I'll never forget," Williams said. "I talked to a few of my friends from back home, and they told me to go out and win the game for them. I'm sure they'll be thrilled."
