Cardinals Jansen Named GCL's #1 Player
Monday, August 10th, 2020
The Gulf Coast Rookie League has selected its Player of the Week. The honor went to Cody Jansen, catcher of the St. Louis (GCL) Cardinals.

In a standout performance, he lit up the league with a .500 average to win the award. Jansen went 9-for-18 for the week, totaled 1 home run with 4 RBIs and scored 7 times.

Jansen currently has a .374 batting average with 40 hits, 5 homers, 26 RBIs, 19 runs scored and a .452 OBP.
