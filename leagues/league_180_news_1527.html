Injury Sidelines Ramirez for Season
Tuesday, August 11th, 2020
After several days of waiting for test results, bad news was delivered to Yoniel Ramirez today. The San Francisco (DSL) starting pitcher will be out the rest of the year due to a torn labrum he suffered on 08/09/2020.

This year Ramirez has fashioned a 2-2 mark, 1.91 ERA and pitched 47 innings. Opposing clubs are batting .213 against him and he has logged 27 strikeouts.
