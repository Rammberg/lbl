Injury Shuts Down Henzman for Season
Thursday, April 30th, 2020
The Birmingham Barons have lost the services of Lincoln Henzman for the rest of this season. He suffered a stretched elbow ligament in a recent ballgame and the follow-up report from the medical staff is not good. Henzman is out for the year.
