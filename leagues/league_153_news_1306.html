Torres of New York AL Batter of the Month
Saturday, August 1st, 2020
When Gleyber Torres stepped up to the plate in July he did it with confidence, and today he was honored with the American League Batter of the Month award.

The young New York shortstop posted a .379 batting average with 14 home runs, 31 RBIs and scored 23 runs. Torres also worked pitchers for 12 walks to post a .454 on-base percentage.

Thus far this season Torres has hit .317 with 33 home runs, 95 RBIs and scored 83 times.
