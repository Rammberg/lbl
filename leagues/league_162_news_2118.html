Cougars Prevail, as Round 1 Goes to the Wire
Tuesday, September 15th, 2020
Depending on your loyalties, the Round 1 was either thrilling or heartbreaking.

The series went down to its final game, with the Kane County Cougars and the Clinton LumberKings giving their fans plenty of drama. In the end, the Cougars came out on top, winning 6-3 at NelsonCorp Field today to take the series 3-2.

"We're lucky to be going into the Round 2," said Kane County pitcher Andy Yerzy. "If all the series are like this, it'll definitely be an entertaining postseason."

Yerzy earned series MVP honors, hitting for a .294 average, with 1 home runs, 5 RBI and 2 runs scored.

The Kane County Cougars will play against the South Bend Cubs in the next round. South Bend won their series against the Lansing Lugnuts 3 games to 0. The Round 2 schedule will be announced after the Round 1 finishes.
