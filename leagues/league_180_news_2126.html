Espada Garners DSL Top Skipper Award
Wednesday, September 16th, 2020
The Miami (DSL) Marlins called a press conference to announce that field manager Angel Espada had been named Dominican Rookie League Manager of the Year earlier in the day. Staffers congratulated the 45-year-old baseball guru as he approached the podium.

"This city has allowed me to craft a winning ball club," said Espada. "When you take on a new job, there's always some adjustment period, but everyone here has been cooperative. From the bat boys on up. Here's to many more successful seasons for the Miami (DSL) Marlins!"

Espada led Miami (DSL) to a 53-19 record this year and a first place finish in the Boca Chica Northwest Division.
