Injury Brings Season to End for Thomas
Sunday, June 7th, 2020
After several days of waiting for test results, bad news was delivered to Connor Thomas today. The Peoria starting pitcher will be out the rest of the year due to a damaged elbow ligament he suffered on 06/06/2020.

Thomas has fashioned a 5-2 mark for the season, 2.30 ERA and thrown 47 innings in 9 starts. Opposing clubs are batting .233 against him.
