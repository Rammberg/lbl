Albies Bangs Out 5 Base Hits
Sunday, June 28th, 2020
It's not something you read about in the sports pages every day -- a 5-for-7 day. For many, it might come around only once in a career -- and for Ozzie Albies, it came around today.

The 23-year-old second baseman scattered hits all over Marlins Park to pace the Atlanta Braves to a 33-27 victory over the Miami Marlins.

Miami skipper Don Mattingly told reporters in the press room that "Albies deserves a hat-tip."

"He hit some good pitches against us," he said. "In fact, according to our scouting reports, Albies hit several that he is not supposed to be able to hit."

Ozzie Albies singled in the 2nd, flied out in the 3rd, hit an RBI single in the 4th, hit a GRAND SLAM off Sam Dyson in the 4th, flied out in the 5th, hit an RBI single in the 7th, singled in the 8th and walked in the 9th.

This year Albies is batting .303 with 15 home runs.
