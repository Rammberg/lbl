Setback For Nationals and Max Scherzer
Monday, August 10th, 2020
Things have gotten worse for Max Scherzer of the Washington Nationals. The 36-year-old starting pitcher will not be back as soon as originally expected. Scherzer is undergoing treatment for bone chips in his elbow, but his doctors have expanded his recovery time to at least 8 months. An unidentified team official told BNN, "Max tried to rush his rehab and suffered a setback."

Max Scherzer is expected to undergo surgery in Washington(District of Columbia) tomorrow.

His record this season is 2-3 in 7 starts and Scherzer has a 3.67 ERA.
