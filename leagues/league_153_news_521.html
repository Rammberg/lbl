Season-Ending Injury for Axford
Friday, June 5th, 2020
Obviously disappointed, John Axford confirmed today that his season is officially over. The Texas reliever offered mostly monosyllabic responses to reporters. "Nope," he said, when asked if he might possibly return this year. The 37-year-old Axford suffered a torn ulnar collateral ligament on 06/02/2020 and has been out of action since.

So far this season Axford has no saves in 16 relief appearances with a 1-1 record and 10.59 ERA.
