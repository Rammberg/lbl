Young Star Helps Propel North to Win in Midseason Classic
Tuesday, August 4th, 2020
Pioneer League put its best players on the field today, and the game certainly showed that.

In the annual Midseason Classic, the North came out over the South by a final score of 4-3. Leading the way for the North All-Stars was right fielder Burle Dixon.

"I go out there and do my best every game, All-Star game or not," the star said after his great show, going 2-for-3 in the contest.
