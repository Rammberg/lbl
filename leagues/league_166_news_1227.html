APP Awards Player of the Week Trophy
Monday, July 27th, 2020
Will Holland is respected by most Elizabethton Twins fans for his no-frills style of play. The 22-year-old second baseman went about his business with vigor last week to grab the Appalachian League Player of the Week honors.

He compiled a .478 average (11-for-23) with 3 home runs, 8 RBIs and 6 runs scored.

In his 2020 season totals, Holland has 5 home runs, .317 BA, .401 OBP, 20 RBIs and 26 runs scored.
