Red-Hot Gonzalez DSL Player of the Week
Monday, July 13th, 2020
A hot-hitting week got Luis Gonzalez the Dominican Rookie League Player of the Week trophy. He really wore out his welcome with league pitchers.

The Arizona (DSL) third baseman slammed them for a .591 average, 13 hits in 22 at-bats, no home runs, 4 RBIs and scored 2 runs.

In the postgame press conference, Gonzalez shared his hitting philosophy with the BNN scribes: "Just look for the seams and hit between them... nothin' more than that."

Presently Gonzalez is batting .294 with 37 hits and no home runs. He has knocked in 7 runs and scored 7 times.
