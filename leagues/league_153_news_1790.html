Narvaez Has Hot Hand, Bangs 5 hits
Saturday, August 29th, 2020
No matter what the Cincinnati Reds threw at him today, Omar Narvaez hit it. He easily handled Reds pitching, going 5 for 6 and helping the Brewers to a 25-8 triumph at Great American Ball Park.

"The way I was swinging today, I think I could have closed my eyes and hit the ball," he told BNN reporters in the postgame interview.

Told of Narvaez's quip, Reds skipper David Bell said he "would take him up on that next time."

Omar Narvaez singled in the 2nd, flied out in the 4th, hit an RBI single in the 5th, doubled in the 6th, singled in the 8th, hit an RBI single in the 8th and walked in the 9th.

A look at his current stats shows Narvaez with a .227 average, 8 home runs, 38 RBIs and 34 runs scored. He has played in 86 games.
