Herrera Bashes 5 Base Hits
Tuesday, July 28th, 2020
Getting 5 hits in a game has always been memorable feat in baseball. In the Scranton WB's 11-10 win over Louisville today, right fielder Rosell Herrera was red-hot, scorching them for hit after hit at Louisville Slugger Field.

"To hit the ball consistently well, you have to relax," Herrera said afterward. "I was relaxed for the first four hits, but I have to admit, I got a little tight when I realized I had a chance for five. On the last at-bat, I was ready to swing at anything close."

Rosell Herrera struck out in the 2nd, singled in the 4th, hit a three-run home run off Jose De Leon in the 5th, singled in the 7th, walked in the 10th, singled in the 12th and hit an RBI single in the 14th.

So far this year Herrera is batting .240 with 2 home runs, 16 RBIs and 18 runs scored.
