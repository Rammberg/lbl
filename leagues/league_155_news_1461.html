AC Trophy Goes to Monge of Wichita
Monday, August 10th, 2020
So far in his young career, Joseph Monge has kept a low profile, but after his performance at the plate last week, he attracted the notice of the rest of the American Conference in a big way as he was named the Player of the Week today for his efforts.

The 25-year-old Wichita Wind Surge right fielder hit at a torrid .500 clip as he collected 12 hits in 24 at-bats, 1 home run, 3 RBIs and 6 runs scored.

Hitting .301 to date, Monge has 3 home runs and 16 RBIs. He has played in 43 games.
